IN THE DISTRICT COURT, Of the Third Judicial District of Washington Territory, holding Terms at Seattle for the Counties of King and Kitsap.

HABEAS CORPUS. No. 4437.

The People of the United States of America, to the Sheriff of King County Chief of Police of City of Seattle. Greeting:

We command you, that you have the body of Lee Kee by you imprisoned and detained as it is said, together with the time and casue of such imprisonmenet and detention, by whatsoever name said Lee Kee shall be called or charged, before the HON. ROGER S. GREENE, Judge of the aforesaid Court at the Court house in said City of Seattle on the 27th day of May 1885 at 5 o'clock P.M. to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning the said Lee Kee And have you then and there this writ.

WITNESS THE HON. ROGER S. GREENE, Judge of said District Court and the seal thereof this 27th day of May A.D. 1885. JAMES SEAVEY, Clerk. By James P Ludlow Deputy
