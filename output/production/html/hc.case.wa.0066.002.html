In the matter of the application of Han Lee for a writ of Habeas Corpus in behalf of Seo Kim restrained of her liberty by Ah Chow

Now avers the above named Han Lee by D Higgins his attorney, in answer to the return of the said Ah Chow [?] and attys:

1st that the said Ah Chow did have the said Seo Kim in his custody at the time of the issuing of said writ of habeas corpus and did unlawfully restrain her of her liberty

2d that she was at the time of her arrest detained and restrained of her liberty by his authority and that he was entering[?] through his agents to effect her removal from this territory against her will for the purpose of carrying or having her carried to the city of Victoria in British Colombia

D Higgins

Atty for Petitioner
