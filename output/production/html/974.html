  • Summary of Proceedings: John Martin served two years at the Nebraska State Penitentiary on a burglary conviction. The day he was released, he was arrested and charged with having committed an attempted robbery in Iowa several years prior. Martin claimed he had been in a different state at the time of the attempted robbery and petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, the writ was allowed and Martin released from custody.
