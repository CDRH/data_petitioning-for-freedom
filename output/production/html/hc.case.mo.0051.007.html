The State of Missouri

To the Keeper of the Common Jail of St. Louis County Greeting:

You are hereby commanded to receive the body of James Allison Parker, a runaway slave into your common Jail, and him safely keep until he shall be taken away by his master, and all reasonable charges paid, or until he be otherwise discharged in due course of Law

Given under my hand this 11th day of Sept 1858

Signed Peter W. Johnstone

Justice

Executed this within writ by delivering the body of the within named defendant to the Jailor of Saint Louis County Sept 11 1858

Signed A. J. Crowwell
