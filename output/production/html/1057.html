  • Summary of Proceedings: Joe Lachapell was arrested, charged with using "obscene language in the presence of ladies," and jailed for 12 1/2 days after refusing to pay a $25 fine. Lachappel petitioned a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. He argued that cursing in front of women was not a crime, the act making it so having been repealed decades earlier. After hearing the evidence, Lachappel was released.
