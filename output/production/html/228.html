  • Summary of Proceedings: Aubrey Pickard petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus to retrieve Leonard Pickard from Genevieve Pickard and two others, which was granted. Although not specified in the documents, it appears likely that Leonard was Aubrey's son and Genevieve his ex-wife, as the judge had the cause "transferred to Judge Morning to be heard in connection with application to modify divorce decree." After hearing the evidence, Judge Morning dismissed the writ, awarded custody of Leonard to Genevieve, and ordered Aubrey to pay her court costs.
