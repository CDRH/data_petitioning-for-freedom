  • Summary of Proceedings: Samuel and India Coffman were married and one day India left with their two minor children, Blanche and Olive. Samuel asked for a writ of habeas corpus to be issued to return the children to his care saying India did not have the right means to take care of the children. The writ was granted and the children were ordered to be returned to Samuel. However, four years later, the girls were living with India and her family in Mills County, Iowa.
