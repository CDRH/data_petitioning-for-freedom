  • Summary of Proceedings: Leroy Allison was being detained at the Nebraska State Hospital for the Insane on the authority of a warrant issued by the commissioners of insanity for Cass County, Nebraska. He petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, it was found that the commissioners had neglected to impose a sentence on Allison. The writ was allowed and Allison released from custody.
