State of Missouri County of St Louis } ss

Hugh Logne personally appeared before me Peter W Johnstone a Justice of the Peace in and for said County, and being duly sworn says that on the night of the 24th May 1853 he arrested as a run-away slave a negro man calling his name George Turney - and further says he has good reason to believe the said George is a Runaway slave, and prays the Justice may commit said George for safe keeping

Hugh Logne

Sworn and subscribed before me this 25th day of May 1853

Peter W Johnstone Justice
