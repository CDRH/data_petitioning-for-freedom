  • Summary of Proceedings: Carrie Schastale filed this petition asserting that Sister Florida was being unlawfully deprived of her liberty by Sister Holdwig And St. Joseph Hospital in Douglas County, NE. “Sister Florida,” whose real name was unknown, was 14 or 15 years old at the time of the petition. She entered the institution for short-term care but was being held against her will for over a year, she was being deprived of her natural rights to “go and come” from the institution. Schastale filed the petition on behalf of “Sister Florida” because she was detained and unable to apply for the writ on her own. On 16 July 1914, Judge Sutton ordered that the writ be allowed.
