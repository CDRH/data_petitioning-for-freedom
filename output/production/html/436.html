  • Summary of Proceedings: On April 8, 1919 R.E. Wilkinson was arrested and booked at the Omaha City Jail as part of an investigation. No charges were brought against him, and he was not given bond or a hearing. 2 days later, he filed a petition for a writ of habeas corpus against J.T. Dunn, the Omaha Chief of Detectives. The petition was dismissed at the cost of the relator. The fate of R.E. Wilkinson is unknown.
