  • Summary of Proceedings: Getchie was arrested for vagrancy. After the evidence was announced, he was convicted and sentenced to thirty days and for the last six days he could only eat bread and water. No formal entry was made in the records of the judgment or the sentence in the police records. The court decided that forgetting to record was simply an error that did not affect the process under which Getchie was held. Therefore, the judge ruled that Getchie should remain in jail for the rest of his sentence.
