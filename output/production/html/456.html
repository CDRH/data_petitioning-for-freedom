  • Summary of Proceedings: Charles Bailey was jailed in the City Jail of South Omaha for 30 days "unless discharged by the court," though he had not been found guilty of any crime. His mother, Sarah Bailey filed this habeas corpus petition on his behalf on March 29, 1907. The petition stated John Briggs, Chief of Police, and James Breen, a jailor, were holding Charles. The writ was allowed, returnable on April 1, 1907. On April 1, in Courtroom 1 of the Douglas County Courthouse, Charles was discharged.
