Washington Territory}

King County} SS

I H. G. Thornton, the specially deputis[?] officer to whom the within writ was directed for service[?] state that I have been unable to find Ah Chow to whom said writ is directed and upon whom it was to be ordered, although I have made diligent search[?] for him I further state from inspection[?] and belief that the said Ah Chow has absconded from the County or conceals himself so that no service[?] can be issued[?] upon[?] him[?] of this writ

H.G. Thornton
