Territory of Washington

County of Walla Walla } ss

In the District Court of the 1st Judicial District

In the matter of the complaint and application of Charles Gahres and Martha J Gahres for a writ of Habeas Corpus

Upon reading the complaint of said Charles Gahres and Martha J Gahres duly signed and verified by them whereby it appears that one Etty Dora Cravens a minor child of the Plffs is illegally detained by WH Barret and Issabella Sutton at the town of Wallulo in said county of Walla Walla in the said Territory of Washington and stating therein wherein the illegality of said detentions consists from which it appears to me that a writ of Habeas corpus ought to issue it is ordered that a writ of Habeas Corpus issue out of and under the seal of the District Court of the first Judicial District   of the Territory of Washington in and for Walla Walla and Yakima counties directed to said WH Barret and Isabella Sutton commanding them to have the body of the said Etty Dora Cravens before me in the court room of the said court on the 29th day of June 1871 at 12 oclock M of that day to do and receive that shall then and there be considered concerning the said Etty Dora Cravens together with the time and cause of her detention and that they have then and there the said

Dated June 27th 1871
James K Kennedy
Associate Justice Supreme
Court Washington Territory
& Judge 1st judicial
district Wash Terr.
