  • Summary of Proceedings: On January 30, 1918, Henry Peterson filed for Habeas with attorney H. H. Bowes against sheriff, Michael Clark. The petition was granted, and on return of the writ, the judge ruled in favor of Peterson's claim that the police magistrate in Peterson's Police Court trial had exceed his jurisdiction in sentencing Peterson to 60 days and that the charge does not come under the laws of Nebraska. Peterson was discharged on January 31.
