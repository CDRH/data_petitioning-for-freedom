Licthenberg Judge July/91

Dear Friend Mrs Fouritain I hope that you are not offended with me for you no that it was the beer that was talking. I can not take more then one glass of beer scence I was sick and you now that I had two or three yesterday, and it went to my head, I did not no what I said untill Mamie told me late, in the evening you now yourself that you are the last in Seattle that I would offend. Because you were all ways a good kind friend to me I cannot forget your kindness. I now that you are too kind hearted to keep in spite. Be sure and come to morrow to see me. If I did not care for you I would not writ this and send Mamie over with it Believe me to be your since Friend

Idella Keevan
