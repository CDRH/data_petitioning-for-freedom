  • Summary of Proceedings: L. L. Lindsey petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus after County Court Judge Lansing found him in contempt of court. Lindsey appealed to the District Court claiming that Sheriff Miller was unlawfully detaining him. Judge Holmes granted the petition. In his decision, Holmes ruled that the County Court was without jurisdiction, and Holmes ordered that Lindsey be released from custody.
