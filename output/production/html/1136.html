Trimble wrongfully placed Hendrickson in the Home for the Friendless. This case follows a county court habeas petition for Hendrickson's freedom, which was filed by her foster father, Nels Danielson. Judge Tibbetts granted Wilcox's petition. After hearing evidence, he judged that Lizzie be released from Trimble's custody.
