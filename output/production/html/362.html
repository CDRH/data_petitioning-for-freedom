  • Summary of Proceedings: On February 20, 1917, Roy C. Jordan filed a petition for a writ of habeas corpus, claiming that Chief of Detectives Steve Maloney unlawfully restrained him of his liberty in the Omaha City Jail. He claims that Maloney illegally and unlawfully arrested him on February 17 without a warrant and without a complaint or a criminal charge filed against him. Thus, Maloney detained him without a warrant or authority of law. The same day, the judge accepted the petition and filed a writ of habeas corpus; however, the outcome of this proceeding is unknown.
