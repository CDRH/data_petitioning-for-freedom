State of Missouri

To James Castello Jailor of the County of St Louis Greeting:

You are commanded to have the body of Mary Wilson before the Law Commissioners Court of the County of Saint Louis on the 21st day of April 1854 at 12 OClock M. together with the time and cause of her detention by you, to do and receive what shall then and there be adjudged concerning the said Mary Wilson who as alleged in the annexed petition is restrained of her liberty by you. And have you then and there this writ with your return thereon.

Given under my hand and the seal of said Court at St Louis this 21st day of April 1854

Jno W. Colvin

Law Com
