IN THE DISTRICT COURT OF THE UNITED STATES FOR THE WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON, NORTHERN DIVISION.

In the matter of the Application Marie Robine Grayson, alias Marie Robina, for a Writ of Habeas Corpus ) NO. 2780.

ORDER ENLARGING TIME

Now on this 17th day of September, 1914, upon motion of attorney for Appelant, and for sufficient cause appearing, it is ordered that the time within which the Clerk of this Court may prepare, certify and transmit to the United States Circuit Court of Appeals the transcript of the record in this cause be, and the same is hereby extended to and including the 30th day of September, 1914.

Jeremiah Neterer
JUDGE
