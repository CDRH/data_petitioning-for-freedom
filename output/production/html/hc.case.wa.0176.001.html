United States District Court Western District of Washington Northern Division.

In the Matter of the Application of Marie Miler for a Writ of Habeas Corpus }No.2457.

Order for Withdrawl of the Transcript of the Bureau of Immigration.

Upon the oral motion of the United States Attorney; It is Hereby Ordered, That the government may permanently withdraw the Bureau of Immigration Transcript, filed by the government as a part of the government's return to the Writ of Habeas Corpus in the above entitled matter.

Dated this 4 day of Feburary, 1914.

Jermiah Neterer Judge.

Recieved the above named Bureau of Immigration Transcript this 4th day of Feburary, 1914.
