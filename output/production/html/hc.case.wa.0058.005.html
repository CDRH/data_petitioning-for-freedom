In the District Court of the third Judicial district of Washington Territory holding terms at Port Townsend

In the matter of the application of Robert Moulton for a writ of Habeas Corpus

On reading and filing the petition of Robert Moulton for a writ of Habeas Corpus to take into his custody from the custody of one James Eaves his two minor children named Rosa and Nellie Moulton and it appearing from said petition that said children are illegally restrained and kept from the possesion custody of their parent and legal guardian of their persons to wit the said Robert Moulton It is therfore ordered that the writ applied for be granted and be duly issued by the clerk of this court

Peter Clerk [?]
