







In the District Court,
Of the Third Judicial District of Washington Territory, holding
Terms at Seattle, for the Counties of King and Kitsap.

Habeas Corpus. No. 4689.

The People of the United States of America, to the Sheriff George Finleyson
of King County Greeting: Greeting:

We command you that you have the body of George Walker John San Francisco and Pedro
O'Brien
by you imprisioned and detained as it is said together with the time and cause of
such imprisonment
and detention, by whatsoever name said George Walker John San Francisco and Pedro
O'Brien
shall be called or charged, before the Hon. Roger S. Greene, Judge of the aforesaid
Court at
Seattle, in the Court House in said City of the 2nd day
of November 1885 at 9 o'clock A.M.
to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning the said George
Walker, John San Francisco and Pedro O Brien
And have you then and there this writ.

Witness The Hon. Roger S. Greene, Judge of said
District Court, and the seal thereof this 31st day
of October A. D. 1885.

James Seavey, Clerk
By James P. Ludlow Deputy

Court Seal
