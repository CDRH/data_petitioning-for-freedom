Territory of Washington County of Spokane } SS

Mary Teller being first Duly sworn on oath states that she is well acquinted with James Glispen Sheriff of Spokane County & AJ Duncan who is his deputy and [] Penfield Coroner of Spokane County that said James Glispen is the prosecuting [?] in the case of the Territory vs Mary Teller that he has been very active in pushing the prosecution in this case and in endeavoring to secure a conviction[?] that he was a material witness before the committing magistrate and since then has made an affadavit relative to the killing which is here to attached that affadavit believes that the interest taken by said sheriff in the prosecution of this case has biased him to such an extent that defendant cannot be assured of a fair and impartial jury if summoned by said sheriff that the Deputy sheriff Duncan is also an important and material witness on behalf of the Territory and was one of the witnesses called by it at the preliminary examination   and testified as to statements made by defendent while she was under arrest and in his custody and although he had been required by Defendants council not to allow her to make any statements to any person: the Coroner is also a material witness on behalf of the Territory and testified against the Defendant at the preliminary examinations

Mary E. Teller

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 15 day of December 1887

Geo M Forrtes
