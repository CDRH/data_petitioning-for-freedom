  • Summary of Proceedings: T. A. Dunn was arrested for vagrancy on December 25th, 1912. Arthur F. Mullen argued that William Alstadt attempted to open court and try and determine a criminal case on December 25, 1912, which is Christmas, a legal holiday. Attempting to open court on said day is illegal and all doings and judgements of Alstadt are null and void. The petition was granted, but the fate of Dunn is unknown.
