  • Summary of Proceedings: Oluf Larson submitted this petition stating that he was being unlawfully detained by John J. Donahue, Omaha Chief of Police. The petition states that Larson was “informed against” in Douglas County on 29 January 1912 for attempting to evade the exemption laws of the State of Nebraska and violating said exemption laws on 23 January 1912. Larson stated that the complaint on which he was being held did not state or charge any offense or crime against the laws of Nebraska.
