  • Summary of Proceedings: Nellie F. Wilcox petitioned for a writ of a habeas corpus to retrieve Lillian Lee, a fifteen-year-old girl whom Wilcox had exercised custody over "the same as a mother" for nearly a decade, from Francis and Mary Fuller, which was granted. After the subsequent hearing, however, counsel for the petitioner had the case dismissed. Lee's fate is unknown.
