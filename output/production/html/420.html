  • Summary of Proceedings: Balch was admitted to Fort Steilacoom (Hospital for the Insane) in June 1883. Admitted for "religion and incorporation and homicidal tendencies." Later in 1888 he petitioned for freedom, saying he was not insane. Original commitment and physician's note included. The petition was allowed but it is unknown if Balch was freed. Balch is noted for being in the Oregon First Cavalry Regiment.
