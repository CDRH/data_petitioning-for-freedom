  • Summary of Proceedings: Oliver M. Wilson petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus to retrieve Addaline A. and Oliver P. Wilson, presumably his children, from their mother, Florence Morgan, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, the writ was denied and the children remanded to the custody of their mother. However, all of the Wilsons are shown living together in Lincoln in the 1920 census, so apparently Addaline and Oliver managed to work out their differences.
