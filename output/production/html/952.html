  • Summary of Proceedings: Frank Nelson was arrested and jailed for an unspecified reason. He petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. The respondent, Charles Wauzer, answered that Nelson had been removed from his custody by the sheriff of neighboring Webster County. The judge dismissed the case, acknowledging that it was now outside the court's jurisdiction. Nelson's fate is unknown.
