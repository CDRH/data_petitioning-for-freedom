  • Summary of Proceedings: On 3 January 1916, Ernest R. Powell filed a petition for a writ of habeas corpus. On 4 January 1916, Douglas County Sheriff Felix J. McShane served the writ to Omaha Police Chief Henry W. Dunn. On the same day, both parties along with their attorneys, Julius S. Cooley (Petitioner) and George A. Magney (Respondent) appeared in Court before James P. English. All parties agreed that the cause should be dismissed without prejudice. As such, Judge English dismissed the petition without prejudice. The details of Powell’s detention are not included.
