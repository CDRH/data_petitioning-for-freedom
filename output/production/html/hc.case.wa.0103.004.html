1832

In the United States District Court of the Second Judicial District of Washington Territory holding terms at Tacoma W.T.

In the matter of Prospero Depelippes and Filip Perell

Writ of Habeas Corpus.

The President of the United States of America to Lewis Byrd sheriff of Pierce County, W.T. and Keeper of the County Jail of said County.

We command you that the bodies of Prospero Depelippes and Felip Perell by you detained and imprisoned, it is said, you have forthwith before our United States District Court of the Second Judicial District for the Territory of Washington, at the City of Tacoma said Territory together with the cause of the detention of the said Prospero Deppelippes and Filip Perell to undergo and receive what our said court shall consider concerning them in this behalf; and have you then and there this writ.

Witness the honorable John P. Hoyt Judge of the United States District
