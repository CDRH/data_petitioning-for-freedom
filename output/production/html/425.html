  • Summary of Proceedings: John Bellamy was arrested and jailed on suspicion of being a fugitive from slavery. Frank Burke, who knew Bellamy since he was a child and knew him to be free, petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus on his behalf, which was granted. At the hearing, multiple witnesses confirmed that Bellamy was free. He was ultimately released.
