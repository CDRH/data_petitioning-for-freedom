IN THE DISTRICT COURT OF THE UNITED STATES FOR THE WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON NORTHERN DIVISION.

IN THE MATTER OF THE APPLICATION OF ELSIE MILLER DICKINSON, alias ELSIE MILLER, FOR A WRIT OF HABES CORPUS.:

NO. 2965. ORDER DISCHARGING PETITIONER ETC.

Upon reading and filing the stipulation dated February 26, 1915, by and between Vanderveer & Cummings, attorneys for petitioner, and Clay Allen, Esq., United States District Attorney for the Western District of Washington, attorney for Henry M. White, Commissioner of Immigration, respondent herein, to the effect that the above named petitioner be discharged from further custody, and that the bond heretofore filed by her to secure her appearance in the above entitled action be cancelled,

It is now, therefore, ORDERED that the above named petitioner Elsie Miller Dickinson, alias Elsie Miller, be, and she hereby is, discharged from further custody, and that the bond heretofore filed by her herein to secure her appearance in the above entitled proceeding be, and the same hereby is, cancelled and discharged.

Done in open court this 2d day of March, A. D. 1915.

Edward E Cushman
Judge.
