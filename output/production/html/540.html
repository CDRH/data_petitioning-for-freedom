  • Summary of Proceedings: Eva Tolls, alias Eva White, petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus on the grounds that there had been no complaint leveled against her. The respondents, Martin White and Daniel W. Iler claimed that she was of suspicious character, a prostitute, and a vagrant. There are numerous subpoenas for J. S. Miller to return Tolls to court, but after the writ was allowed, there are no documents indicating her fate.
