County of Saint Louis Ss:

The State of Missouri

To Robert Duncan & Sally Adams Greeting:

You are hereby commanded that the body of Julia, alias Mary Ann, an infant of colour, in your custody, as it is said, unlawfully detained, together with the cause of her detention by whatsoever name she may be known, you forthwith have before our honorable circuit court now in session at the city of Saint Louis, then & to do, submit, and receive whatsoever our said court shall in that behalf consider

Witness John Ruland, clerk of our said court, in open court with the seal thereof hereto affixed, this twenty fourth day of August in the year of our Lord eighteen hundred and thirty six.

John Ruland Clerk

By

J.D. Johnston D.C.

 

Julia- alias Mary Ann

vs

Robert Duncan & Sally Adams

" By the Habeas Corpus act "
