In the District Court of the Second Judicial District of Washington Territory holding terms at Tacoma in and for the County of Pierce.

In the matter of the application of Ah Sing for a Writ of Habeas Corpus. WRIT of Habeas Corpus.

The United States of America and the Territory of Washington to T. J. Hamilton, United States Marshal for Washington Territory

GREETING.

We command you that you have the body of Ah Sing by you imprisoned and retained as it is averred, together with the time and cause of of such imprisonment and detention, by whatsoever name said Ah Sing shall be called or charged before Frank Allyn, Judge of the District Court of the Second Judicial District of the Territory of Washington holding terms at Tacoma in and for Pierce County at the Chambers of said Judge in the Court House where said Court is held in the County of Pierce W.T. on the 27th day of September, A.D. 1888 at 1:30 o'clock in the forenoon afternoon of that day, to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning the said Ah Sing. And you have then and there this Writ.

WITNESS my hand and seal as the Clerk of said Court this 26th day of September A.D. 1888.

A. Reeves Ayres, Clerk

By LO Ceblast Deputy Clerk.
