









In the District Court of the 3rd Judicial District in and for the County of King Territory
of Washington.

Territory of Washington}
County of King} SS

In the matter of Frank OBrien Habeas Corpus

And now comes the said OBrien by his Atty John E. Costello and says that he is unable
to procure the services of aan attorney for pay and that he is unable to pay necessary
clerk fees up on the filing of his petition for writ of Habeas Corpus and asks to
the same be filed.

Frank OBrien

by his Atty John E. Costello

Sworn to before me
