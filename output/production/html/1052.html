  • Summary of Proceedings: Charged with highway robbery and sentenced to five years hard labor, William Hayes petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus against the warden of the Nebraska State Penitentiary, George Leidigh. Judge Cornish granted the petition. After reviewing the evidence, Cornish denied the writ and remanded Hayes back into the custody of Leidigh. Hayes was originally tried with his alleged co-conspirator, June Bent, who also petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus. See hc.case.ne.0585
