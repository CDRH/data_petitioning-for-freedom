County of S Louis [?] The State of Missouri To the Jailor of S Louis County Pierre Chouteau Sr Bernard Pratte and Alexis Amelin - Greeting -

Whereas it has been made appearant[?] to our Circuit Court now in session at the City of S Louis wihtin and for the County of S Louis that you the Jailor of S Louis County, and Pierre Chouteau Sr Bernard Pratte and Alexis Amelin, detain in your Custody certain persons of colour namely Catiche alias Catherine, Julie, Joseph &[?] Helen who have petitioned and attained leave from our said court to sue certain persons to establish their freedom. These are therefore to come [?] [?] that the bodies of the said Catiche Julie[?] Joseph and Helen by you detained under safe and secure conduct together with the day and cause of their being taken and detained by whatever names they may be called, you have before the Judge of our Circuit Court on Friday the 19th day of May instant at the Court house in the City of S Louis Then and there to do and receive in whatsoever our said Court shall consider in that behalf.

Witness Archibald Gamble Clerk of said Court at S Louis the 10th day of May 1826
Archibald Gamble Clerk
