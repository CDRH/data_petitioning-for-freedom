  • Summary of Proceedings: On April 13, 1921, Chester A. Richards was arrested. On April 14, he petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus. The respondents replied on the same day and stated that they arrested him for allegedly defrauding an inn-keeper. Chester’s fate is unknown. However, the appearance docket shows that someone was subpoenaed and the writ was delivered to Gilbert Dolan on May 23.
