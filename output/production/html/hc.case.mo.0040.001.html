Law Commissioners' Court

August Term 1858, No. 107

Edwards, William, In the matter of; Edwards, Martha, minor child, In the matter of Habeas Corpus

William arrested in Iowa while on board the Steamboats "Sarasen" by the captain of the boat and then brought to St. Louis on June 2, 1857; was born in Tampico, Mexico, "where slavery is not tolerated; Martha was a minor child of 9 year of age; was forcibly taken from her mother, Eliza Edwards, who petitioned for her release from jail and for Martha to be returned to her custody
