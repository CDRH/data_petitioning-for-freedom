Territory of Washington

County of Walla Walla

In the District Court of the first Judicial District

Charles R Gahres and Martha J Gahres, Plffs
vs
WH Barrett and Isabella Sutton Defts

To WH Barrett and Isabella Sutton Defendants of W A George and N T Caton their Attorneys

You will please take notice that the plaintiffs in the above entitled action hereby appeals to the supreme court of this territory from the order of order entered in above entitled action in the said District Court on the 12th day of July 1871 awarding the custody of Etta Dora Cravens to Defts and also the Judgement entered at same time in said action in favor of Defendants in said action for and against Plaintiffs and from the whole thereof

 

Dated this 13th day of July 1871

Frank P Dugan

Attorney for Plaintiffs and Appelants
