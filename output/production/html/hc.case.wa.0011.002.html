Territory of Washington
County of Walla Walla }

In Justice Court before O P Lacy Justice of the Peace

Territory of Washington
vs
Mary Oneil }

Charged with being an habitual Drunkard

Complaint filed Oct 10th 1881 and warrant issued and the Deft arrested Oct 11th 1881 and arraigned on said charge and after hearing the evidence of J G Justice and J H Lucas on the part of the Territory the Court finds that the said Mary Oneil is an habitual Drunkard and a disorderly person and upon said finding the counsel did order and adjudge that the said Mary Oneil give Bond in the sum of one hundred Dollars for her good behavior for the period of three months or in default thereof be committed to the county jail for said term and the said Mary Oneil having failed to give bond, as required the said Mary Oneil has committed to Jail Oct 17th 1881
