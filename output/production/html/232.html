  • Summary of Proceedings: Ella Baker petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus to retrieve Grace Baker from the custody of Thelma Falbey, which was granted. Ella was Grace's grandmother and Thelma Falbey was Grace's mother. Ella had custody of the child after Thelma's divorce from Harry Baker, her son and Grace's father. On September 5, Thelma had taken the child, saying she was going to take her to the movies, but they never came back. While the hearing was pending, the sheriff deposited Grace with the matron of the Lincoln Detention Home. After hearing the evidence, Grace was remanded to the custody of Ella.
