  • Summary of Proceedings: John N. Baldwin, the petitioner and petitioner attorney, filed a writ of Habeas Corpus on behalf of Anna Walter in 1922 after her incarceration for being an alleged fugitive from justice from the state of Maine. The warrant from Maine stated to arrest Margaret Sterling, aliases Mrs. A. Willard and Mrs. Robert Walter, for fraud. Anna Walter's petition stated that she was not in Portland, Maine, during the time of the crime. Furthermore, Robert Walter, husband of Anna, verified that he is her husband and can verify that she was not in the state of Maine during the time of the alleged offense. The petitioner team filed for a new trial and appealed to the Supreme Court; the appeal to the Supreme Court of Nebraska was dismissed at the cost of the petitioner. A new writ of Habeas was filed in February of 1922 after the Supreme Court dismissal; the outcome is unclear.
