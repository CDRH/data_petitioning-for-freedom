St Louis Circuit Court
Case Files
July Term 1828, No. 7

Dolly, a free woman of color vs John Young

Trespass; Assault &amp; Battery; False Imprisonment

Attorneys: Plaintiff - James Evans &amp; George Strother
Defendant - McGirk, Isaac C.

Note: Freedom suit, plaintiff, about 40, claimed defendant forcibly removed her to
Feever River mines [now Galena River] in Northwest Territory [now Galena, IL &amp; southwest
Wisconsin], holding her there for 6 months. Writ of habeas corpus to produce Dolly
included.
