  • Summary of Proceedings: Robert Welts was convicted of selling liquor without a license in Kearney County, Nebraska and fined $100. Since Welts could not pay, he was jailed. After serving for some time, he was released, only to be jailed again in Adams County. Welts declared that he was insolvent and had served long enough to satisfy his debt under state law. He petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, Welts was released.
