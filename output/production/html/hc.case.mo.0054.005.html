In obedience to the command of the foregoing writ I have here in Court the body of George Turney or Terrele, and for my return say that he was committed to my custody on the 25th day of May 1853 on warrant issued by Peter W Johnstone a Justice of the Peace within and for the County of St. Louis charged as being a runaway slave, which warrant has been by me delivered to Jno M Werner Sheriff of the St. Louis County

This 18th day of June 1853

James Castello

Jailor of St. Louis County
