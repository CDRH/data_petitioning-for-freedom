  • Summary of Proceedings: On October 18, 1923 Knud Knudsen was charged with selling milk and cream below the standard contrary to the ordinance of the city of Omaha, and sentenced to serve 15 days in jail. He filed a petition for a writ of Habeas Corpus on October 19, arguing that there is no ordinance authorizing a jail sentence for such offense. On this same day Knudsen was discharged, and on October 26 the judge ordered the writ to be returned.
