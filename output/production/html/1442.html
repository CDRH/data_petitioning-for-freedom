  • Summary of Proceedings: L. Griffith was the father of Mary Griffith (1 yr. 6 mos. old). Her mother died on October 24, 1894, only about a month before this case. Abigail St. John secured the posession of his daughter without his consent and against his wishes. L. Griffith alleged he was competent, ready and willing to provide support of his daughter. He also said that St. John refused to return his daughter back to him. The writ of Habeas Corpus was issued, but nobody showed up for the case, so the cause was dismissed.
