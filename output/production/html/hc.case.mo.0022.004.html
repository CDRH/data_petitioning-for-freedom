State of Missouri

To the sheriff of St Louis County Greeting

Whereas a certain negro man who calls himself James Young and says he is free but has no letters of emancipation with him, nor has he in any way satisfied me of his claim to freedom, these are therefore to require you to take the said James Young into your safe keeping, and discharge him when therein[?] to required by law - & for which this shall be your warrant, given under my hand this 11th October 1823.

Signed Peter Ferguson

Justice of the Peace

County St Louis

Returned Executed this writ by delivering the body of James the within named boy to the sheriff October 11th 1823

Signed John Bush

June 1824

Copy of commitment of James Young
