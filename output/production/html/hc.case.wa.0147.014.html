In the Superior Court of the State of Washington for the County of King

JUVENILE COURT

IN RE THE WELFARE OF GLADYS HAWK Ward of the Court}

No. 4385 ORDER OF DISCHARGE

Now, on this 10th day of July, 1916 this matter coming on regularly for hearing upon application of Lilburn Merrill, chief probaton officer of this Court, in behalf of Gladys Hawk, a ward of the Court, who heretofore, to wit; on the 20th day of November, 1914, was committed to the temporary custody of the Girls' Parental School; and it appearing to the Court that said child was thereafter, to-wit; on the 10th day of January, 1916, temporarily paroled from said Girls' Parental School to Mrs. C. E. Hunt, 2343- 44th Avenue Southwest, Seattle; and it further appearing to the Court that the said Gladys Hawk was recently married

IT IS THERFORE ORDERED by the Court that the said Gladya Hawk be, and she hereby is, discharged from the custody of the Girls' Parental School and from further supervision of the Court.

King Dykeman
Judge

Recorded in January Book B. page 30.
