The State of Missouri

To James Castello Greeting

You are commanded to have the body of Joseph Adams before the Law Commissioners Court of St Louis County on forthwith together with the time and cause of his detention by you to do and receive what shall then and there be adjudged concerning the said Joseph Adams & who as alleged in the annexed petition is restrained of his liberty by you

In testimony whereof I hereto set my hand & seal of said Court at St Louis June 17th[?] 1854

Jno W Colvin

Law Com
