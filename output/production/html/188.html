  • Summary of Proceedings: Harry Meehan was arrested on February 7th for carrying a concealed weapon. Since he worked seasonally for the police as a dog catcher, he claimed that he was allowed to concealed carry. Harry filed a petition for habeas corpus on February 11th. The petition argued both that he was allowed to conceal carry and that the crime would be a felony so the court that arrested him had no jurisdiction. The petition was approved; however, the response to the writ and outcome are unknown.
