In obedience to this writ I have here in court the body of the within named James Young who was committed to jail on the 11th October 1823 by virtue of a warrant issued by Peter Ferguson Esquire one of the Justices of the Peace within & for the County of St Louis will now fully appear by the said warrant a copy of which is presented annexed and makes a point of this my return to the said writ.

21st June 1824

J K Walker

Sheriff
