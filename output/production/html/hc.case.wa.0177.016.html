UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASINGTON NORTHERN DIVISION.

In the Matter of the Application of Tonka Bajlo for a Writ of Habeas Corpus.

No. 2459

ORDER DISCHARGING ORDER TO SHOW CAUSE.

The above entitled matter having come regularly on for hearing this 15th day of May, 1913, on the petition for writ of habeas corpus, order to show cause, respondent's return thereto, briefs of respective counsel submitted in lieu of oral argument, together with affidavits attached to petitioner's brief and reply to the return, and the court having heretofore filed a written opinion herein and being fully advised in the premises,

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that said order to show cause be and the same is hereby discharged and the case dismissed.

Edward E Cushman

O. K. as to form.

Chas H Ennis & Fenley Bryan Attorneys for Petitioner.
