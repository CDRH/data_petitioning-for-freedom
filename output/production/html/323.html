  • Summary of Proceedings: Lee Kee was charged with unlawfully keeping a house where people inhaled opium and charged with a fine of 50 dollars and 20.70 dollars in case 4437 (hc.case.0074). Kee defaulted on the payment and was therefore sentenced to 30 days in prison. In this case, he filed for a writ of habeas corpus on the grounds that the Justice of the Peace who sentenced him had no authority to do so. The court granted the petition and released Lee Kee.
