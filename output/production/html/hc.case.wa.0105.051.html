District Court 2nd Judicial District of Washington Territory

The Grand Jury being called Frank Clark Atty for James Riley appears and moves the Court that a person charged with an offence and held in custody to appear at the present term of court appears and moves the Court that said Riley be brought into Court for the purpose of exhibiting[?] the right of challenge which motion the Court do overrule because counsel had first appeared for Riley

Ruling excepted to - Exception allowed
