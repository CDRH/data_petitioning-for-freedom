







CS Penfield sworn and testified

Am coroner of Spokane County a man came to me yesterday and said there had been a
man killed near Deep Creek Falls about four o'clock in the afternoon in company with
sheriff Glispen I came to Deep Creek Falls staid over night. this morning I went to
the place held one inquest over the body of a man supposed to be Franklin Teller found
him lying on his face with his hands underneath him with the left side of the face
exposed to view there was considerable blood under his head found blood between house
and deceased but none between deceased and gate in examination of the body found the
skull fractured on left side found large indentation  found no bullet holes found
a small hole behind right ear at first glance supposed to be a bullet hole but after
examining it closely found no trace of bullet on the skull

The verdict rendered was that the deceased came to his death by at the hands of Mary
Teller by the use of a club or cudgel aided by other partners and the killing of the
deceased Teller was murder in the first degree
