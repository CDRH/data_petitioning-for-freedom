  • Summary of Proceedings: Belden Finley petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus to retrieve his son, Verne Finley, from the custody of Verne's grandparents, Jesse and May Carter, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, the writ was denied and Verne remanded into the custody of his grandparents. The judge ordered that Belden pay the Carters $3 per week, that the Carters teach Verne to "love and respect" Belden, that Belden be allowed to take temporary custody of Verne at "seasonable times," and that Belden be allowed to re-apply for custody of his son when he could better demonstrate his ability to care for him.
