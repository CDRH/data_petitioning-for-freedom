Territory of Washington,
County of Columbia } SS

I, J H Hosler Sheriff of Columbia County W.T., do hereby certify that I served the within warrant on the within named Jacob Schuman by arresting him in county and Territory aforesaid, on the 27th day of July 1886, and now have him before the court.

Dated this the 27th day of July 1886. By M A Cavanaugh Deputy.

J H Hosler Sheriff of Columbia County W.T.

 

Territory of Washington,
County of Columbia } SS

To the Sheriff, or any constable of said County

WHEREAS: A.L. McCauley has this day complained in writing, under oath to the undersigned, one of the Justices of the Peace in and for said county, that on the 27th day of July 1886, in said county and Territory, Jacob Schuman did unlawfully and without having first obtained a license therefor sell to him the said A.L. McCauley one pint of Lager Beer

Therefore, in the name of the United States, you are commanded forthwith to apprehend the said Jacob Schuman and bring him before me to be dealt with according to law.

Given under my hand this 27th day of July 1886.
J.C. Dorr
Justice of the Peace
