  • Summary of Proceedings: By his next friend William Clark, Anson accused Henry Mitchel and Henry Russel of assaulting him with the intention of reducing him to slavery. The judge elected to issue a writ of habeas corpus. Mitchel and Russel were then arrested on kidnapping charges and Anson was jailed while awaiting his hearing. Mitchel and Russel pleaded not guilty, and Anson was ordered to be hired out while his status was being considered.
