  • Summary of Proceedings: Edna Lambert petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus to retrieve her daughter, Gean Seeley, from the custody of W. G. Seeley and his wife, which was granted. The 1920 U.S. federal census indicates that W. G. and Della Seeley were Gean's grandparents. After hearing the evidence, Gean was remanded to the custody of the Seeleys, and Lambert ordered to pay the court costs.
