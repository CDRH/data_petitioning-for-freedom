At Chambers September 2nd 1873

Charles Gahres & M J Gahres
vs
W H Barrett & Isabella Barrett

In District Court 1st Judicial District Washington Territory

This cause coming up before the Judge of said Court for hearing and the parties having submitted the same upon the pleadings and proofs and the same having been taken under advisement by the Judge, now on this 2nd day of September AD 1873 the said parties by their respective counsel and in proper persona appeared before said Judge at the Courthouse in Walla Walla W.T. Whereupon said Judge delievered his written opinion herein which was duly filed, whereby it is awarded that Defts plea herein is sufficient and that the writ is dismissed

Wherefore it is ordered considered that said writ of Habeas Corpus be and the decree is hereby dismissed and that the Custody of said child Ella Dora Cravans be and the same is hereby awarded to said Defendants WH Barrett and Isabella Barrett and that they said WH Barrett and Isabella Barrett have and recover of and from the said Plaintiffs Charles Gahres and Martha   Martha J Gahres their costs and disbursements herein taxed at and that execution issue therefore
