  • Summary of Proceedings: Joseph DeKlotz was being jailed on a charge of having engaged in "gambling, chance or fraud." He petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted, the judge also ordering that he be released for the duration of the proceedings. After hearing the evidence, the writ was allowed and DeKlotz released. The county attorney appealed this to the state supreme court, which affirmed the decision of the lower court.
