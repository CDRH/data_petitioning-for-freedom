Territory of Washington
vs
Mary A Oneil Deff }

Complaint for
Vagrancy

On this 23rd day of August 1882 the said Mary A Oneil was brought before me charged on oath of J G Justice with being an habitual Drunkard and a disorderly person and on being arraigned the said Mary A Oneil pleads guilty to said charge and upon J G Justice being sworn on the law of the Territory the court finds that said Mary A Oneil guilty of being an habitual Drunkard and disorderly person and finds her the said Mary A Oneil guilty of vagrancy and the said court did order and adjudge that the said Mary A Oneil give bond for her good behavior in the sum of one hundred dollars for the period of three months and in default thereof be com mitted to the County Jail of Walla Walla County for said period of three months

and the said Mary A Oneil [?]   day of August 1882 Committed to Jail

Given under my hand this 23rd day of August 1882

OP Lacy
Judge of the Peace
