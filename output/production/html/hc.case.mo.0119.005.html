State of Missouri, CITY AND COUNTY OF ST. LOUIS } SS.

Personally appeared before me, O D. Filley Mayor of the city of St. Louis, John Gilman who, being duly sworn, upon his oath complaineth and saieth that one Martha Adaline Edwards or Edger a female minor under the age of fifteen years, hath been found in the city of St. Louis exposed to the corrupting influence of a house of prostitution kept by her mother

John Gilman

Sworn to and subscribed before me this 21 day of August A.D. 1858

OD Filley

Mayor of the city of St. Louis.
