  • Summary of Proceedings: Harry L. Davis was a fugitive from justice in Kings County in New York and had a warrant out for his arrest for his crime of grand larceny. He obtained $335 worth of goods of H. W. T. Mall & Company by false pretenses. The call for his arrest and subsequently the warrant traveled through authorities starting in the police department in New York City, through Missouri, with Davis finally being arrested in Omaha. The writ was allowed but Davis was not discharged from prison.
