United States of America Territory of Washington 3rd Judicial District } SS.

In the United States Commissioner Court Before Eben Smith United States Commissioner in and for said District at Seattle King Co

The United States of America vs Yu Gum } Order

And now on this 7th day of May AD, 1886 at Seattle in said King County upon trial had and in consideration of the evidence produced it is by me now considered and adjudged and hereby certified that said Yu Gum defendant above named is a Chinese Laborer and not entitled to come into or remain in the United States of America and it is further ordered and adjudged by me that she be, by the United States Marshal of Washington Territory, returned to British Columbia that being the Country from which she came

Given under my hand and official seal at this 7th day of May AD 1886

Eben Smith

United States Commissioner in and for the 3rd Judicial Dist of Wash Territory at Seattle in King Co
