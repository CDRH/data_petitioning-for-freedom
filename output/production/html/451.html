  • Summary of Proceedings: On August 13th, 1918, the bound party, Matt G. Glasse, was arrested under report of being a fugitive from the law from Lyons County, Kansas. On August 14th, 1918, a petition for a writ of Habeas Corpus was filed and a hearing was ordered for August 15th. A return was filed on August 16th, but the substance of this return is unknown. On January 26th, 1920, the case was dismissed for want of prosecution.
