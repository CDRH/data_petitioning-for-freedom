  • Summary of Proceedings: In December 1875, William Martz was arrested and jailed in Pacific County, Washington after two men, Hiram Wing and Hiram Paulding, declared to a justice of the peace that they would "not be responsible for the conduct" of Martz any longer. Martz petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus in the territorial district court, which was granted. His fate is unknown.
