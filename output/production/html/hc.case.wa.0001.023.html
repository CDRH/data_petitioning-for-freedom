Territory of Washington Spokane County } SS

To the sheriff or any constable of said county

Whereas Neil Smyth has this day complained in writing under oath to the undersigned one of the undersigned Justices of the peace in and for said county that on 31st day May 1887 near Deep Creek Falls in said county Mary Teller John Dugan and Wm Smyth he truly believes did murder Franklin Teller. Therefore in the name of the United States you are commanded forthwith to apprehend the said Mary Teller John Dugan and Neil Smyth and bring them before me to be dealt with according to law

Given under my hand this 31st day of May 1887

FL Pierce Justice of the Peace in and for Spokane Co WT
