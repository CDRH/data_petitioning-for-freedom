United States District Court WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON

HABEAS CORPUS

The President of the United States of America To King Dykeman, Seattle, Washingon,

Greeting:
We Command you, that you have the body of Mrs. Gladys Fredrika Fuson by you imprisoned and detained, as it is said, together with the time and cause of such imprisonment and detention, by whatsoever name said Mrs. Gladys Fredrika Fuson shall be called or charged before the Hon. C. H. Hanford, Edward E. Cushman United States District Judge for the Western District of Washington, at Court Room in the City of Seattle in the Northern Division of said District of Washington, on the 27th day of October, 1916, A.D 189 000, at two o'clock in the after noon, to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning - - - - - the said Mrs. Gladys Fredrika Fuson. And have you then and here this writ.

WITNESS the Hon.C.H. HANFORD, Edward E. Cushman, Judge of the United States District Court for the Western District of Washington, this 26th day of October in the year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred and nine hundred sixteen.

Frank L. Crosby, Clerk

By Ed m Laken Deputy Clerk
