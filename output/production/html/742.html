  • Summary of Proceedings: Charged with highway robbery and sentenced to five years hard labor, June Bent petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus against the warden of the Nebraska State Penitentiary, George Leidigh. Judge Cornish granted the petition. After hearing evidence, Cornish denied the writ and ordered that Bent be remanded back into custody. Bent was tried along with his alleged co-conspirator, William Hayes, who also petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus. See hc.case.ne.0586
