In the District Court holding terms at Seattle.

In Re Lee Kee Petition for writ of Habeas Corpus. } No. 4444.

I, J H McGraw, Sheriff of King County W.T. do hereby certify that on the 1st day of June 1885, at Seattle, in said King County, I served the writ of Habeas Corpus issued by the above entitled matter, a copy of which is hereto annexed upon James H Woolery, Chief of Police of the City of Seattle, by delivering the same to him personally, at said City of Seattle, in said County

Dated at Seattle the 1st day of June 1885. J H McGraw Sheriff
