The State of Missouri

To R. F. Logan, Jailor of Saint Louis County, Greeting:

You are commanded to have the body of Eleck Loan before the Law Commissioners Court of St Louis County this 10h day of November 1856 at 11 OClock P.M. then and there together with the time and cause of his detention by you to do and receive what shall then and there be adjudged concerning the said Eleck Loan who as alleged in the annexed petition is illegally restrained of his liberty by you and have you then and there this writ with your return thereon.

Witness M Williams Clerk of the Law Commissioners Court with the seal thereof hereto annexed at office with him[?] this 1st of Nov. 1856

Matthew Williams

Clk
