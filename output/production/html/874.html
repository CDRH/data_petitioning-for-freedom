  • Summary of Proceedings: Mortimer (Matto) Fox alleged that Seavey and Bebout pretended to hold and imprison him by virtue of a complaint filed in the police court by A. Keysor, that professed Fox was a fugitive from justice from California. Fox was arrested in California for forgery, although he alleged that there was no legal indictment. The records from California state that Fox intended to defraud C. M. Bates by forging the endorsement of the check in San Francisco. Fox’s bail was set to $2,000 in California. The fate of Fox is unknown in this specific case.
