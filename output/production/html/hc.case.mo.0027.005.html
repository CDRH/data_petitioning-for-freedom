In obedience to the above writ appears now Henry Steinberg and in answer thereto says that the body of the above named child Edward Scheiber is not in his custody or possession not under his control, nor is the same detained by him in any manner whatsoever, given under his hand this 13th day of May AD 1862

Henry Steinberg

Sworn to and subscribed before me May 13th 1862

S Rice Clk
