SUBPOENA

Territory of Washington County of Spokane} ss

To Dolly Willows, Fannie Webber and Frank Webber

In the name of the United States, you are hereby required to appear before the undersigned, one of the Justices of the Peace in and for the said County, on the 10 day of December 1885 at 11 o'clock A.M. at his office in Spokane Falls W.T. to give evidence in a certain cause then and there to be tried between The City of Spokane Falls Plaintiff- and Frankie Switsinger Defendant- On the part of the plaintiff

Given under my hand this 10 day of December 1885.
J.J.L. Peel
Justice of the Peace
