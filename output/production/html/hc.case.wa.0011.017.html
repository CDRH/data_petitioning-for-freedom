{Territory of Washington {vs {Mary Ann ONeil Deft

Action for the Crime of vagrancy as an habitual drunkard.

And held under Bonds in the Sum of 100.00 dollars for security for good behavior for 60 days and in default of the same was sent to the County Jail on said day until such security be found. or such vagrant be discharged according to Law
