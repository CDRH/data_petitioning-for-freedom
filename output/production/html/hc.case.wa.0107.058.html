In the District Court holding terms at Olympia

In re applica Petetion of Gus Bendjus Et al for writ of Habeas Corpus

To George L Moody Prosecuting Attorney:

Please take notice that a writ of Habeas Corpus has been issued to the Sheriff of Thurston County, commanding said Seriff to bring us the the bodies of Gus Bendjus et al before the Hon. Frank Allyn, judge of the Second Judicial District of Washington Territory that they may be discharged.

You will please appear & show cause to the contrary.

The writ is made returnable at Tacoma Court House at 10 o clock am Oct 13. 1887.

Dated, Olympia Oct 8. 1887

Bignold & Robinson
Attorneys for Petetioners

Service by copy accepted this 10 day of Oct at 8 o clock pm 1887

George J. Moody
Pros Atty.  

137

Filed on the 11th day of October 1887.

HM Sutton
Clerk
