The State of Missouri

To the Matron Keeper or Governess of the House of the Good Shepherd

Whereas Eliza Engle on behalf of Frances Vanhorn has this day presented to our Judge of the St Louis Circuit Court at Chambers her petition duly verified by affidavit setting forth that the said Frances Vanhorn is illegally restrained of her liberty by you at the City and County of St Louis

These therefore command you that you have the body of the said Frances before our said Judge of said Court at the Circuit Court Room in the Court House in the City and County of St Louis at 11 o'clock AM on Thursday Friday the 25th and together with the time and cause of such detention and restraint to do and receive what shall be then and there considered concerning the said Frances Vanhorn. Witness the Honorable James C. Moody Judge of said Court at Chambers in the City and County of St Louis this 22 day of August 1865

James C Moody

Judge
