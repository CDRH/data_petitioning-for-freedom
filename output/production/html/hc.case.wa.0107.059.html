No. 137

Territory of Washington}
vs.}
Gus Bendjus et al.}

Comes now the Grand Jury by their foreman in the presence of said Grand Jury present to the court an indictment against Gus Bendjus - Robert Strangland - Ben Fredrickson - Pete Peterson - Frank Nelson - Fred Stromberger - John Blending - Andrew Johnson - Leander Brown - John Saxton - Alex Agren - John Thompson - Thomas Jacob - E. Emel - Andrew Johnson and Charles Burke for the cause of Riot, which they say they say they find a true bill in which twelve of their number concur which indictment is by the court received and by the clerk duly filed in the court in the prescence of said Grand Jury.

Frank Allyn Judge
