In the district court of the Second Judicial District of Washington Territory

Territory of Washington
vs
G. Bendjus et al

Territory of Washington
County of Chehalis} ss
Affidavit

Lewis B Bignold being first duly sworn on Oath says that, C Holberg, W H Amedon, and G I Roderich were Grand Jurors at the last September term of the District Court for Chehalis County, that they each of them told this affiant during the week last past that one John I Medcalf was remanded as a Witness before said Grand Jury when the three cases wherein the Territory of Washington plaintiff and G Bendjus et al are defendants was being investigated by them that his testimony was given before them previous to the indictment being filed in open court that he, the said Medcalf was subpoenaed on behalf of the Territory of Washington and gave evidence relating to the cases aforesaid, that said John I Medcalf's name no where appears on any of the three said indictments, now on file in the aforesaid court.

Lewis B Bignold

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 10th day of Oct 1887

Mason Irwin, Notary Public, Wash. Tery
