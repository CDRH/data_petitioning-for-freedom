  • Summary of Proceedings: John Crawley was arrested and jailed on suspicion of being a fugitive from slavery. William Logan, whose relationship to Crawley is unclear, asserted that Crawley was a free man and petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus on his behalf, which was granted. Others asserted that Crawley was enslaved by a man named Stephen Gardier. After hearing the evidence, Crawley was released.
