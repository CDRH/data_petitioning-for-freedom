







[WAL-2398]

Know All men by these presents that one
John B Montague as principal and 
William P. Adams as surety are held 
and fairly bound under J H Sheffler and 
Josephine Sheffler in the sum of five 
hundred dollars lawful money of the United 
States for the payment of which well and 
truly to be made and done we bind our
selves our heirs executors &amp; administration
Jointly and severly firmlyby these presents
sealed with our seals and dated the 
11th day of November 1876.

The conditions of this obligation 
is such that whereas the said J H Sheffler &amp; Josephine Sheffler have upon complaint
made by them against the above 
Counder John B Montague caused one
Edna Elenor Edwards or Edna Elenor Montague to be this day brought before his 
Honor S G Wingard Judge of the District 
Court of this 1st Judicial District of Washington Territory at his Chambers at Walla

Walla County on a writ of Habeas Corpus 
and whereas the hearing of said matter 
is by the said Judge upon consent of 
parties adjourned to the 15th day of November 1876 at the time of 10 o clock 
A M of said day at his said chambers 



Now if the aforementioned John B Montague shall on the said 15th day of November 1876
at said time have and produce
the said junior child Edna Elenor 
Edwards otherwise call Edna Elenor Montague before said judge at his said chambers,
and  shall in the mean time 
keep said minor child in a proper 
manner free and away from all evil 
and improper associations and influences 
then this obligation is to the null and
void otherwise to be and remain in 
full force
J B Montague
W. P. Adams

sealseal
