  • Summary of Proceedings: Louie Simmons and Joe Cavarra were arrested in Douglas County on March 9th, 1923. The next day the two men filed a writ of Habeas Corpus, claiming they were wrongfully arrested without a warrant and were being unlawfully held in custody. The case was dismissed on January 27th, 1925, and the fate of the bound parties is unknown.
