To L.E. Gray Master of the Bark Gem of the Ocean

You are hereby Summoned to appear before me J.R.Lewis Judge of the District Court of the 3d Judicial District of said Territory held at Seattle in and for the counties of King Kistap and Snohomish at my chambers in the city of Seattle and said County of King on the 3d day of May A.D. 1875 at the hour of 1 Oclock PM to show cause if any you have. Why admiralty process shall not issue out of the District Court aforesaid, against the above named Bark whereof you are master her tackle apparel and furniture to answer the demands of Peter Nelson W Smith H Elsner C Whaley G Mattson Charles Ludblad Dennis Coffy and Jacob Annir late mariners on board said Bark for there wages as such mariners earned in her her voyage from the port of San Francisco to the Port of Seattle between the 8th day of April and the [] day of May A.D. 1875

Given under my hand this 9 day of May A.D. 1875

J.R.Lewis

Dist Court 3d Dist

In Admiralty
