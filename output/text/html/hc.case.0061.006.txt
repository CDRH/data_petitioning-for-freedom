







Office of United States Attorney of Washington Territory
Seattle, April 30 1889

Wm H Whittlesey Esq.
Clerk Dist. Court
Port Townsend

Sir,

In case No. 1237 In Re application of Lee Sing Wot &amp; C you will find enclosed the
original papers &amp; the order of the Court denying the writ. Record the order &amp; if Mr
Bradshaw desires a copy furnish it to him.

The petitioner has leave to withdraw the certificate of the Chinese Consul on filing
copies with you.

Very Respectfully
W H White
U.S. Atty
