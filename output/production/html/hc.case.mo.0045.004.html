To the Honorable the St Louis Law Commissioners Criminal Court of St. Louis

The petition of George Washington Kirk respectfully represents

That he is a free man of color, domiciled and residing in the state of Ohio, and has been free for the space of sixteen years, having with his father and his family been manumitted in Hamilton County State of Ohio on the 13th day of February AD 1840, by his owner Exum Louis of Hinds County in the State of Mississippi, as will appear by a certified copy of the record of manumission from the Superior Court of Cincinnati in the State of Ohio which is filed herewith.

He also filed herewith to prove his identity and that is and has been free, the affidavits of Casey Kirk, his father, of J.S. Engert, R.F. Engert, Henry F. Teetor and James Richardson, good and lawful men residing in said City of Cincinnati

Your petitioner represents that he is confined in the common jail of St Louis County by James A Felps the keeper thereof, under authority he is informed of Thumer Maddox former Sheriff of said County of St Louis who committed him to said jail as a runaway slave and as your petitioner is informed has advertised that he will sell your petitioner as a runaway slave. He has asked and been refused a copy of his commitment

Your petitioner alleges that his imprisonment is illegal in this that your petitioner is a free man and not a slave, and that no person claims him as owner by right or by law.

The Judge of the Criminal Court is now absent from this County as he is informed

And your petitioners will ever pray &c

George Washington Kirk

By Whittelsey Atty

County of St Louis }

George Washington Kirk being sworn upon his oath says that the facts in the above petition stated are true

Geo Washint Kirk

his X mark

Sworn to and subscribed before me Aug. 11 1856

A. F. Jaeger

not public

Charles C Whittelsey attoney for petitioner & also in addition that no other application has been for the relief sought has been made to and refused by any other court or officer superior to this court

Chas C Whittelsey

In re Geo Washington Kirk

Petition for Habeas Corpus

Filed Aug 11th 1856
