  • Summary of Proceedings: Max Rosenthal, was found maintaining a solicitor without having a license and therefore violating Ordinance number 977 of the City of Omaha. Rosenthal, petitioner, files for a writ of Habeas Corpus on grounds that the City Council of Omaha does not have the authority to enact an ordinance of this kind as it conflicts with the constitution of the United States and Nebraska. Judge Lee Estelle finds that Rosenthal is unlawfully deprived of his liberty and approves the writ.
