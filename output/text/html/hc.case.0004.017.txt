







Territory of Washington
County of Walla Walla

I hereby certify that I served the within Summons and annexed Complaint on the within
named Defendent W.H. Barrett at Walla Walla County in Said Territory on the 30th day
of August A.D. 1873 by Delivering to him personally true copies of the Same Certified
to be such by W. H. Andrews Clerk of the District Court of the first Judicial district
of Washington Teritory this 1st Day of September 1873

B.W. Griffin Sherriff
by Henry Windler Deputy
