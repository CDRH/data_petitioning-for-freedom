Indictment for violating the local option Law

Not a true Bill Lewis Heece Foreman of the Grand Jury

Grand Jury in their presence and filed September 21 1886 by JW Jessel Clerk

 

1433

In the District Court of Columbia County Washington Territory holding terms at Dayton

The Territory of Washington
vs
Jacob Schuman}

Jacob Schuman is accused by the Grand Jury of the County of Columbia in the Territory of Washington by this indictment of the crime of violating the local option law committed as follows

The said Jacob Schuman on the 27th day of July 1886 in the City of Dayton in the County of Columbia Territory of Washington did unlawfully and without having first obtained a license therefore sell to one AL McCauley one pint of Lager Beer contrary to the force of the statutes in such case made and invoided and against the peace and dignity of the Territory of Washington

Dated at Dayton in the County and Territory aforesaid the [] day of [] AD 1886

R.T Sturdevant
Prosecuting Attorney
