United States District Court Western District of Washington Northern Division

In the Matter of the Application of Margaret Roy for a Writ of Habeas Corpus

No. 4516 Stipulation

IT IS STIPULATED AND AGREED by and between Ralph S. Pierce,Attorney for the above named applicant, and United States Attorney Robert C. Saunders, attorney for respondent, Henry M. White, that the exhibit attached to respondent's return herein, being the record of proceedings before the Immigration authorities, and the testimony adduced in said proceedings with relation to the deportation of said applicant, may be withdrawn from the records andfiles of this court.

Dated this 28th day of February, 1919.

Ralph S Pierce

Attorney for Petitioner

Robt C Saunders

United States Attorney.
