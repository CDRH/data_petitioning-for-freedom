  • Summary of Proceedings: Arrested and charged with grand larceny, Frederick Frazier petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus claiming that Sheriff Trompen was unlawfully detaining him. Judge Holmes granted the petition. After hearing evidence, Holmes denied the writ and remanded Frazier back into police custody. Holmes also ruled that Frazier be allowed to bail provided he pay the sum of 500 dollars. It is unknown whether Frazier posted bail.
