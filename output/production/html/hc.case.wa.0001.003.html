Territory of Washington County of Spokane ss.

In the District Court of the Territory of Washington, and for the Fourth Judicial District thereof, holding terms at Spokane Falls, in said county and Territory.

Territory of Washington,

Plaintiff.

vs.

Mary E. Teller,

Defendant.

+++++++++++++++++

To the Sheriff of Lincoln County--Greeting:

In the name of the United States you are hereby commanded that you have the body of Mary E. Teller by you imprisoned and detained, as it is said, together with the time and cause of such imprisonment and detention by whatever name said Mary E. Teller shall be called or charged, before the Honorable George Turner, Judge of the District Court of the Fourth Judicial District of the Territory of Washington, at his Chambers in the city of Spokane Falls, in Spokane County, on Thursday, the 14th day of July, 1887, at the hour of Ten o'clock A. M. to do and receive what shall then and there be considered of her the said Mary E. Teller, and have you then and there this writ with your return thereon.

Witness the Hon. George Turner, Associate Justice of the Supreme Court of Washington Territory, and Judge of said Court, and the seal thereof affixed, this 18th day of July, A. D. 1887.

D. A. Clement Clerk
