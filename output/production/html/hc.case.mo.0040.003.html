State of Missouri

To Charles Rodeman Jailor of St Louis County Whereas it is represented to me that William Edwards Free man of colour is held confined restrained and illegally imprisoned by you in the St Louis County Jail, you are therefore commanded to have the body of the said William Edwards before me in the court room of the said Commissioners Court on Saturday together with the time and cause of his imprisonment and detention with your return to this writ to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning the said William Edwards imprisoned or detained
