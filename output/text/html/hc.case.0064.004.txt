








Territory of Washington
County of King

David Hughes, Alexander S. Hughes Joseph Brannen James Boyd and David Hart on oath
each for himself says that he has heard read the above petition that they are the
petitioners named, that they know the contents of said petition and that the same
is true as they verily believe
David Hughes
Alexander S Hughes
Jos. Brannon
James Boyd
David Hart

Subscribed &amp; Sworn to before me this 14h day of May 1875
WH White
Notary Public
W.T.
