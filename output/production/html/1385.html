  • Summary of Proceedings: Samuel Jackson, a Chilkat Alaskan Native, was arrested after he "struck one M. A. Reason with his hands and fists" at the Chemawa Indian School. He was charged with assault and battery in the Justice's Court of Salem, Oregon and jailed when he refused to pay the $20 fine. Jackson petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus in the Marion County Circuit Court, which was granted. Jackson argued that the Justice's Court did not have the jurisdiction to try him, since he committed the offense on federal land. After hearing the evidence, Jackson was released. (A second petition was found in this file. There is no obvious relationship between the two petitions.)
