  • Summary of Proceedings: Arrested on the charge of premeditated murder, Archibald S. Jones petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus claiming that Sherriff Fred A. Miller was unlawfully depriving him of his liberty. He also claimed his right to bail. Judge Strode granted the petition. After reviewing the evidence, Strode ruled that Jones was not deprived of his liberty but was entitled to bail at a sum of five hundred dollars. It is unknown whether or not Jones posted bail.
