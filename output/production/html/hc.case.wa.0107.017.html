No 135

In the District Court at Montesano

Territory of Washington} vs.} Gus Bendjus Et. al.}

This application coming on for a reduction of Bail and it being apparant that Defts. can not give the argument heretofore required and considering the great expense thereby about to be inposed in the County. The Court having become satisfied a lesser amount of bail will be as effectual & be to better effect as above.

Therefore it is ordered that the bail heretofore fixed be reduced to $400 for each of the accused now under arrest this to cover the three charges in each ones case. and upon good security being given by each of said parties in the sum of $400 said parties to be released till next term of Court.

At Chambers
Oct. 1st 1887

Frank Allyn
Judge
