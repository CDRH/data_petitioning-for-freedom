Territory of Washington}

County of Pierce} SS

To the Sheriff any constable and the Keeper of the Jail of said County

Whereas Mary Arlington of Tacoma Pierce County Washington Territory has been brought this day before the undersigned one of the Justice of the Peace in and for Pierce County charged on the oath of Mr Brottan with having on the 23d day of January 1885 in said county committed the offence of visiting a House used by one Henry Arlington for the purpose of a record for the Purpose of Smoking opium by other Persons and that then and there being the said defendant Mary Arlington did smoke and inhale opium Contrary to the form of the Statute in such cases made & provided and in the progress of the trial of said charge it appearing to the said Justice that the said Mary Arlington has been guilty of the offence of being found in a house resorted to for the purpose of smoking opium in possession of opium papers and apparatus for the purpose of smoking opium committed at the time and place aforesaid and whereas the said Mary Arlington has failed to   give Bail in the sum of $100.00 for her appearance to answer at the next term of the District Court as required by me thereupon in the name[?] of the United States you are hereby commanded to receive the said Mary Arlington into your custody in the said Jail and her there safely keep until she be discharged by due course of Law.

Given under my hand this 23rd day of January 1885

AE Lawrence

Justice of the Peace
