I WH Bush Sheriff of Chehalis County Washington Territory do hereby certify that I served the writ of Habeas Corpus of which the within is a true copy by by handing to and delivering leaving with Levicy Hume wife of the respondent of the said Wm Hume one of the respondents would therein and to whom said writ was addressed, the said writ of Habeas Corpus in Chehalis County Washington Territory, on the 17th day of September AD 1888

WH Bush Sheriff

T. H. Goodell Deputy

Serving Writ 1.00

Copying & R 1.00

Returning .20

$2.20 Pay

In the Matter of the application of Maria K. Anderson for writs of Habeas Corpus.

Writ directed to William and Levicy Hume

I certify that the within is a true copy of the original writ in the above written as received by me

WH Bush Sheriff of Chehalis County
