  • Summary of Proceedings: Anna Tatum petitioned with a writ of Habeas Corpus on behalf of Glen Hull who was detained in the Omaha City Jail. Glen Hull was arrested on January 19th, 1917, and the petition states there was no reason given for his arrest. The Chief of Police Henry Dunn and Captain of Police Henry Heitfeild were ordered to appear in court with Glen Hull on January 23rd, 1917. After the hearing the writ was approved and Glen Hull was ordered to be released from police custody.
