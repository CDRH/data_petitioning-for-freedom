United States of America.
Territory of Washington}
Second Judicial District}

To JC Pearson; CH Gilbreath; Abel Goss; HC Winkler; GN Scommons; Wm Valentine; Charles Wade; William Church

In the Name of the United States of America, you are hereby required to appear at the Court House in Montesano, on the 15th day of May, 1888 at 9 o'clock in the forenoon to give evidence in a certain cause, wherein Territory of Washington Plantiff, and Gus Bendjus et al Defendants on the part of Plaintiff

Witness Frank Allyn, Esquire, Judge of the District Court of the Second Judicial District of Washington Territory, this 1st day of May, A.D. 1888. HM Sutton, Clerk.

 

Territory vs Gus Bendjus Et al. Subpoena. Returned and filed this 15th day of May A.D. 1888. HM sutton, clerk. Vidette Print, Montesano, W. T.

Territory of Washington, County of Chehalis} I hereby certify that the within subpoena came into my hands on the 8th day of May A.D. 1888, and I served the same on the within named witnesses, to-wit

J.C. Pearson, C.H. Gillbreath, Able Goss, Wm Church, GN Scommon, Wm Valentine, Charles Wade, and HC Winkler by exhibiting and reading the same to each of them.

WH Bush

Sheriff of Chehalis County
