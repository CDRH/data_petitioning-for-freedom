







Territory of Washington County of Washington}
In Justice Court before O P Lacy Justice of the Peace

To the constable and the Keeper of Jail 
of said County 

Whereas at a Justice 
Court held at my office in said 
County for the Trial of Adaline Oniel 
for the offense hereiniafter stated the 
said Adaline Oniel was convicted 
of being --- on the 29th day of March 
1876 and for a long time past thereto
of being a habitual drunkard and 
disorderly person and upon said 
conviction the said court did adjudje
and determine that the said Adaline 
Oniel gave bonds in the sum 
of one hundred dollars for her
good behavior for the term of three 
months or in default thereof that 
she be committed to the county Jail 
for the term of three months and 
whereas default has been made
in giving Bond as required by 
me therefore you the said Constable 
are commanded in the name of the 
United States forthwith to convey 
the said Adaline Oniel and deliver 
her to the said Keeper and you the 



said Keeper are hereby commanded to receive 
the said Adaline Oniel into your 
custody in said Jail and her 
there safely Keep until the 29th 
day of June 1876 or until she 
be discharged by due course of 
Law.

Dated March 29th 1876 
OP Lacy
Justice of the Peace 

Territory of Washington 
vs 
Adaline Oniel 
Committed
