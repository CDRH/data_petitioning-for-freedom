  • Summary of Proceedings: Frederick C. Test claimed that his 6 month old son, Jo Janelene Test, was unlawfully detained by F.A Birby and Lou Birby. He claimed to have been the natural guardian and only gave temporary custody to Jo’s maternal grandmother, F.A Birby, preceding his wife’s death in Santa Fe, New Mexico. He was a lieutenant in the U.S Army and desired to take his son back to New Mexico, as Mrs. and Mr Birby were refusing to give him back his custody. Ultimately, Test was granted back full custody of his child.
