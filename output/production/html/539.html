  • Summary of Proceedings: Joseph W. Fleming was arrested by the chief and captains of police for Omaha. The sheriff of Cumings County allegedly called them to arrest the petitioner for defrauding Peter Shinstock. Joseph Fleming filed a petition for habeas corpus on June 23rd, 1919 in which he argued that he could not be arrested and charged by officials in another county. The petition was approved and he appeared in court on the same day. The writ was dismissed for "prosecutorial related costs".
