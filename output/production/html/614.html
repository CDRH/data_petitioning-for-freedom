  • Summary of Proceedings: Pearl Dils had taken her son, Willard Dils, to Boyd County, Nebraska while in the processing of divorcing her husband, James Dils. James petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus to retrieve Willard, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, the court ordered that James and Pearl share custody of Willard while their divorce was pending. Each was to have custody of Willard for one week at a time, dropping the boy off at the office of James' attorney, E. E. Spencer, on Saturday afternoons. Neither was to remove Willard from Lancaster County nor take him to their relatives' homes without permission of the other. Once this was decided, James withdrew his petition.
