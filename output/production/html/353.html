  • Summary of Proceedings: In 1905, 15-year-old Willie Vangart was found guilty of theft and sent to the State Industrial School and was ordered to stay there until he turned 16 unless discharged according to law. Petitioner Frank Vangart, the father of Willie, then filed for the release of Wilie Vangart to release Willie who has now reached the age of 16. Judge Kennedy finds that Willie is indeed unlawfully deprived of his liberty, and he is released from custody.
