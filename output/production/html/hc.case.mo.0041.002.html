The State of Missouri

To James Castello Jailor of the County of Saint Louis Greeting

You are commanded to have the body of Henry Freeman before the Law Commissioners Court of Saint Louis County on the Fifth day of August AD 1853 at 9 Oclock AM. together with the time and cause of his detention by you to do and receive what shall then and there be adjudged concerning the said Herny Freeman who as alleged in the petition hereto annexed is restrained of his liberty by you. And have you then and there this writ with your return thereon.

Given under my hand and the seal of said Law Commissioners Court this 4th day of August AD 1853

John W. Colvin

Law Com
