  • Summary of Proceedings: Joseph Tebo was charged in Thurston County to four months in jail, a fine of $50.00, and costs of trial. On April 15, 1910, Tebo and attorney, Charles T. Dickinson, presented a petition for habeas against Douglas County sheriff, E. F. Brailey, charging that Tebo had no right to be held in Douglas County. On April 18, Tebo was released from Douglas County custody and transferred back to Thurston County custody.
