The State of Missouri

To James Castello Jailor of the County of Saint Louis Greeting

You are commanded to have the body of Adam Riley before the Law Commissioners Court of St Louis County on the 11th day of Aug inst 1853 at 3 o'clock PM together with the time and cause of his detention by you to do and receive what shall then and there be adjudged concerning the said Adam Riley who as alleged in the annexed petition is restrained of his liberty by you. And have you then and there this writ with your return thereon.

Given under my hand and the seal of said Court this 10th day of August AD 1853

Jno W Colvin

Law Com

No 98

August Term 1853

Habeas Corpus in the matter of Adam Riley

"By the Habeas Corpus Act"

MacDonald Atty

Issd Aug 11th 1853

Returnable August 11th 1853 at 3 PM

costs pd [?] [?] 2

August 11th 1853 Discharged 2 P222
