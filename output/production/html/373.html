  • Summary of Proceedings: Mike Aboud, a previous resident of Oklahoma, unlawfully claimed to own several items to receive $37 from the Bank of Aline under false pretenses. Afterward, he fled to Nebraska. Thomas B. Ferguson then requested the Governor of Nebraska, John Mickey, to arrest and extradite Aboud. Ferguson specifies that the Governor of Nebraska should place Aboud in the custody of Gus Hadwiger, Sheriff of Woods County, to bring to Oklahoma—Mike Aboud's presumed relative, Elias Aboud, petitions for a writ of Habeas Corpus on his behalf. Judge George Day finds Aboud to be lawfully detained and rejects the writ of Habeas Corpus, and he is then extradited to Oklahoma.
