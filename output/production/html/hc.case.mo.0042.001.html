Law Commissioners' Court

October Term 1858, No 180

Guba, Miles, In the matter of

Habeas Corpus

Committed to St. Louis County Jail in March 1857 as a runaway slave; got on board a steamboat at Cairo, Illinois; clerk on boat asked him for his fare and his free papers, but he had neither; stated he was born free; petitioned listed 4 reasons why Guba was unlawfully arrested; ordered to be discharged
