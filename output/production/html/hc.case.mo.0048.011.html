State of Missouri

County of St Louis } SS

Personally appeared before me Louis DuBreuil Justice of the Peace, within and for the County aforesaid Gerard Doyle who being sworn upon his oath says that he did on the 7th day of April 1855, arrest & apprehend Adam Marshall a negro slave and that he verily believes that said Adam Marshall is a run- away negro and belongs to Mary L. Smith of Shelbyville Kentucky.

G. Doyle

Sworn to & subscribed before me this 7th day of April 1855. Louis Du Breuil

Justice of the Peace
