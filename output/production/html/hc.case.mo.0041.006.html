The State of Missouri

County of St Louis } Ss Alfred B. Holton Richard Lawling personally appeared before me Peter W. Johnston a Justice of the Peace and being duly sworn says that he has arrested as a Runaway Slave a colored man calling himself Henry Freeman said negro was found in the city and county of St Louis on the 19th day of July 1853.

Alfred B. Holton

Sworn to and and Subscribed before me this 20th July 1853

Peter W Johnston Justice
