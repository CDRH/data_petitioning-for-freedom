Territory of Washington County of King } ss

To any Constable and the Keeper of the Jail of said County;

Whereas Ah Sam and Ah Who of King County W.T. has been brought this day before the undersigned one of the Justices of the Peace in and for said County; charged on oath of Wm Murphy with having on the 19th day of Feby 1885 in said County committed the offense of running a gambling game as proprietors thereof and in the progress of the trial of said charge it appearing to the said Justice that the said Ah Sam and Ah Who have been guilty of the offense of conducting a game of tan as proprietors thereof committed at the time and place aforesaid: and, Whereas the said Ah Sam and Ah Who have failed to give bail in the sum of for each, one hundred dollars for his appearance to answer at the next term of the District Court, as required by me; which said term will be held at Seattle in said King County W.T. on the first   Monday in April 1885 to wit April 6th 1885.

Therefore, you the said Constable in the name of the United States are commanded hereby forthwith to convey and deliver the said Ah Sam and Ah Who to the said Keeper; and you the said Keeper, are hereby commanded to receive the said Ah Sam and Ah Who into your custody in said Jail, and them there safely keep until the said term of said Court to await its action on said charge or until they shall be discharged by due course of law

Dated this 21st day of February 1885. G A Hill Justice of the Peace in and for King County W.T.
