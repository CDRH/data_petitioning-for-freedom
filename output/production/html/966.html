  • Summary of Proceedings: On December 20, 1906, Fred Schneiderwind stole one dozen plates, according to H.S. Daniel. On January 21, 1907, Fred was sentenced to 90 days being absolutely void, on the charge of petit-larceny. The habeas file was entered January 22 and filed on January 30. On January 30, the writ was allowed, returnable January 31, 1907. However, on January 31, the writ was denied, and the case was to remain undecided until February 22, 1907. On October 3, 1907 the case dismissed due to a court motion for warrant of prosecution. Note the case file ends on January 30 without documentation reflected in the docket.
