  • Summary of Proceedings: Robinson, petitioner, was arrested for possession of two chisels outside of a stone store owned by Maurice J. McKilligan. Robinson filed for a Writ of Habeas Corpus in front of acting police Judge G. Stevenson. Bail for Robinson was set to five hundred dollars, and set to one hundred dollars for McKilligan, Wright, Mansfield, and Byrne to ensure they show up to act as a witnesses for the alleged possession of burglary tools.
