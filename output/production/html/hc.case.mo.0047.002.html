To the Honourable John W Colvin Judge of the Law Commissioners Court of Saint Louis County Missouri

Your Petitioner Joseph W Loan respectfully represents that he is and for a long time past has been the owner of a slave named Eleck Loan that said slave was imprisoned in the Saint Louis County Gaol on or about the 25th day of October A.D. 1856 by the commitment of Peter W Johnstone a Justice of the Peace as a Runaway slave, in the information of one Hugh L White, that said slave is now held in confinement against his will and the will of your petitioner by one John Robert F Logan keeper of the said County Gaol of Saint Louis County, Missouri. Your Petitioner states that he is a resident of Platte County in the state of Missouri, and that his said slave Eleck Loan is not a runaway slave but that his said slave Eleck had full and legal authority to travel from Platte County Missouri to the City of Saint Louis Missouri. Your petitioner therefore prays that a writ of Habeas Corpus may be issued by your Honourable Court returnable forthwith requiring said John Robert F Logan keeper of the Saint Louis County Gaol to appear before your Honorable Court with the person of the said Eleck Loan, to inquire into the cause of the detention of the said Eleck Loan, slave as aforesaid and belonging to your Petitioner and to grant the said Eleck Loan his discharge and his delivery to your Petitioner according to Law

Jos W Loan

By John Loan

John Loan, being duly sworn on his oath saith the above petition and the matters therein as stated are true

John Loan

Sworn to & Subscribed Nov 1st 1856 before me M William
