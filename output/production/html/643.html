  • Summary of Proceedings: Petitioner, Morris Yost, filed this Application for a Writ of Habeas Corpus on behalf of Emma Yost - alias Emma Urlas - stating that she had been arrested and placed into custody of Chief Henry Dunn, Chief Stephan Maloney, and two jailors with real names unknown on 14 December 1912. The application was filed on 28 December 1912. The application stated that Emma had been taken into custody without any order of commitment or other process authorizing the detention. No complaint had been filed against her charging her with the commission of any crime or offense against the laws of the State of Nebraska; thus, the detention was without any legal authority. Judge Sutton dismissed the case on 17 January 1913.
