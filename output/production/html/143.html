  • Summary of Proceedings: John Wilson was arrested on authority of Lucas County, Iowa, for burglary and left. Held by Douglas County, Wilson filed a habeas petition on December 2, with attorney, A. S. Richie, against John J. Donahue. Wilson charged that his arrest was wrong and that he had never spent time in the state of Iowa; therefore, Wilson could not be a fugitive from justice. His petition was allowed, but his fate is unknown.
