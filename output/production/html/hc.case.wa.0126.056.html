Territory of Washington vs. Sterling P White[?]

Territory of Washington
Spokane County } ss

J. S. Beardon of Lincoln County in said territory being first duly sworn accuses S.P. White Lyle White of the crime of murder committed as follows: The said S. P. White on the 21st day of December A.D. 1885 at the County of Lincoln in the Territory of Washington did then and there with a certain revolving pistol then and there loaded with gun powder and a leaden bullet feloniously and maliciously and with malice aforethough assault Amos Light Beardon and did then and there discharge said pistol, and bullet against to and in the body of said A. L. Beardon and thereby did wound said Beardon with a mortal wound and the said Beardon of said mortal wound as aforesaid   [?] AD 1885 die[?] in Lincoln County in said Territory

Submitted & sworn to before me this 17th day of April AD 1885 }

J.L. Beardon his X mark [?] Notary Public
