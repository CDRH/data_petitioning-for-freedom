







In the Dist Court of the 3d Jud Dist of Washington Territory held at Seattle in and
for the counties of King Kistap and Snohomish

In the matter of the application of Peter Nelson W Smith H Elsner C Whaley G Matson
Charles Lundblad Dennis Coffy &amp; Jacob Annir for a writ of Habeas Corpus

To the Hon JR Lewis Judge &amp;c and now come the aforesaid applicants on 5th May of 
to the sufficing and  the return of the said Lewis V Wycoff Dpty Marshal &amp;c to the
said  Sg.

1st that said return does not show upon what charge or for what offense said applicants
are held, or before what court they are held to answer

2 And said applicants aver the truth and fact to be as follows viz: that on or about
the 8th day of April AD 1875 they shiped on board the Bark Gem

of the Ocean L E Gray Master for a voyage from San Francisco to Seattle Puget Sound,
And that afterwards on or about the 11th day of the same month the said master proceeded
to sail with said Bark and with these applicants on board without them signing any
shipping contracts or agreement in writing or in print whatever that on or about the
23d day of April aforesaid, the said Bark arrived at the Port of Seattle King County
Washington Territory at which time &amp; place said applicants are entitled to then discharge
from said vessel, and then and there demanded to be so dischaged and paid off and
that in consequence of such demand to be discharged and paid off, and for no other
cause, the said Master L.E. Gray procured the arrest and imprisonment of these applicants
after the arrest of said



vessel at said port of Seattle as aforesaid for alleged disobedience of orders and
lawful commands of said Master and Officers of said Bark.

Also  said return is in the   and insufficient

WH White &amp; J J McGilora
Attys for applicants
