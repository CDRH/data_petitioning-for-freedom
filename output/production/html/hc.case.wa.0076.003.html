In the District Court holding terms at Seattle.

In the application of George Walker et. al, No. 4689.

Territory of Washington Count of King SS

J. H. McGraw Sheriff of King County W.T. do hereby certify that on the 1st day of Novermber 1885 at Port Blakeley. W. T. I served the writ of Habeas Corpus issued in the above entitled matter, a copy of which is hereto annexed, upon George Finleyson, he being the person to whom said writ is directed by delivering the same to him personally.

Dated at Seattle the 2nd day of November 1885, J. H. McGraw Sheriff
