  • Summary of Proceedings: Chin Quock Wah petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus in 1915. Hoping to join his father in Boston, Massachusetts, Chin Quock Wah was detained in Seattle, Washington and was denied admittance into the U.S. His petition was granted, however the writ was dismissed by Judge Neterer. Chin Quock Wah appealed to the Supreme Court, to which the following proceedings and Chin Quock Wah's fate remains unknown.
