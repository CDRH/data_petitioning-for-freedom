In Justice's Court, Before J.C. Dorr, Justice of the Peace

Territory of Washington
Plaintiff
Jacob Schuman
Defendant

Complaint

Filed July 29 1886
J.C. Dorr
Justice of the Peace

 

Territory of Washington County of Columbia } SS

AL McCauley being duly sworn on oath says that in the Precinct of Dayton in the County of Columbia in the Territory of Washington on the 27th day of July 1886 Jacob Schuman did unlawfully and without first having obtained a lisence therefore sell to him the said AL McCauley one pint of Lager Beer

AL McCauley

Subscribed and sworn to before me July 27th 1886

J.C. Dorr
Justice of the Peace
