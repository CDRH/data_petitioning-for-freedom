The Petitioner decrees to the return filed in the case matter of of Francis Vanhorn to the writ of Habeas Corpus for the following reasons -

1 It does not set forth the character or purpose of the House of the Good Shepherd

2 The return does not state for what purpose for which cause or which time said Francis was placed in charge or custody of the Mother Superior of said House of Good Shepherd

3d The return does not state any sufficient cause for the confinement of said Frances

4th The return does not set forth any legal ground for the confinement of said Francis

All which is submitted to the Court and asking the Court to adjudge said return defective and for the discharge the said Francis she has option to go free or remain in said confinement as she may choose--

Th S. Epsy

Atty for applicant

In the matter of Francis Vanhorn Habeas Corpus

Decrees of return to writ
