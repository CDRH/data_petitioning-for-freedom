  • Summary of Proceedings: Philip Lowry enlisted in the U.S. Army. He later claimed that he was a minor and petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. His father, John Lowry, may have compelled Philip to file the petition. Edwin Sumner claimed that Philip had already deserted from the Jefferson Barracks, although trial notes suggest he had been recovered. Regardless, after hearing the evidence, Philip was released from custody.
