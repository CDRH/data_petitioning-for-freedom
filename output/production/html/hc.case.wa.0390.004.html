In the matter of the Application of John Doe for a Writ of Habeas Corpus } In the Dist Court of the 1st Jud Dist. Washington Territory in and for Walla Walla County

To the Hon Jas K. Kennedy Judge of said Court

The petition of John Doe respectfully shows

1st that he the said John Doe is unlawfully restrained of his liberty, is unlawfully confined imprisoned and detained by one James McAullif Sheriff of Walla Walla County W. T. and is now imprisoned and held by said McAuliff in the County Jail of Walla Walla County W. T.

That the said imprisonment detention confinement and restraint are illegal

3th That the affiant does not know of the cause of said restraint or illegality or pretenses for the same

That this petitioner was not convicted of any crimes or violation of the Statute of Washington Territory nor has he been arrested or committed by any committing magistrate for the same nor is he now held by any order of the Court or Committing magistrate of Washington Territory

Wherefore your petitioner pray that a   writ of Habeas Corpus may be granted, directed to the said James McAuliff Sheriff as aforesaid commanding him to have the body of Your petitioner before your honor at a time and place therein to be specified to do and receive what shall then and there be considered by your Honor concerning your petitioner together with the time and cause of his detention and writ and that our petitioner may be restored to his liberty

Dated Oct. 13/69

L A Mullan & E. C. Ross atty for Petitioner

Territory of Washington County of Walla Walla

L. A. Mullan being first duly sworn says that he is atty of the petitioner named in the foregoing petition and that he knows the content named in of the foregoing petition and that the smae is true as he verily believes

L. A. Mullan

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 13th day of Oct 1869

Chas W Frush
Justice of Peace
