  • Summary of Proceedings: Anna Mead petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus against her husband John Mead for the custody of her child, Ellen. John had apparently kidnapped the child on May 18, 1889, from Anna's home in Raymond, Nebraska. The matter was urgent, as Anna was still breastfeeding. Judge Field granted the petition the same day and ruled that the bound party be released and that John Mead relinquish his parental rights.
