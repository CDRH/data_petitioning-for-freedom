  • Summary of Proceedings: The petitioners, C. A. Clayton, George Rosenthal, William Lewis, John McDonald, Charles Olsen, Charles Kales, Fredericke Linde, and C. Roshman were held against their liberty and consent on the ship Kate Davenport by the captain of the ship, J. E. Howland. The petition was filed by the next friend of the petitioners, Alfred Cale, who was also a notary. The case was heard by judge Roger Greene, who granted the petition and issued the writ of habeas corpus.
