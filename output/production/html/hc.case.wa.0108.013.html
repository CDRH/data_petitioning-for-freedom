In the District Court of the Second Judicial District of Washington Territory, Holding terms at Montesano in and for Chehalis County.

In the Matter of the application} of Maria Kristina Lundvall Peterson} for a writ of Habeas Corpus} order granting writ

On reading and filing the petition of Maria Kristina Peterson duly signed and verified by her, whereby it appears that her minor child Ida Lundvall is illegally detained and restrained of her liberty by Levicy O. Hamilton at the residence of said Levicy O. Hamilton in said Chehalis County, and Territory of Washington and stating wherein the illegality consists from which it appears to me that a writ of habeas corpus ought to issue;

It is ordered that a writ of habeas corpus issue out of and under the seal of the district court of the second Judicial District of the Territory of Washington in and for the County of Chehalis, directed to the said Levicy O. Hamilton, commanding her to have the body of the said Ida Lundvall before me in the Court room of the said Court forthwith

to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning the said Ida Lundvall together with the time and cause of her detention and that she have then and there this said writ.

Frank Allyn J

Dated Sept 17 1888
