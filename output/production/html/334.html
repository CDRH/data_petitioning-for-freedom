  • Summary of Proceedings: Mr. Michael Mullaly appeared before Judge A. L. Sutton on 22 February 1912 to submit his petition for a writ of habeas corpus for his minor daughter, Dorothy Mullaly. He states that he took his daughter to St. James Orphanage to be taken care of and that when he returned to pick up his child, Reverend Father Roach refused to allow him to take his child. On 23 February 1912, Judge Leslie ordered that Dorothy Mullaly be discharged from the custody of St. James Orphanage and Reverend Father John Roache.
