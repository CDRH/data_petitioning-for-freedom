







Regular Terms of Court. Are Held At Walla Walla- On 2d Monday of May and the 4th Monday
of November of each year. Colfax- On the 2d Monday of June and the 2d Monday of December
of each year. Pomeroy- on the 3d Monday of March and the 1st Monday of October of
each year.

Office of the Clerk of the U.S. Disctrict Court of the First Judicial District of
Washington Ter.
Alfred N. Marion, Clerk.
Walla Walla, W.T.

Walla Walla,188

On re application of George Grant for release on Habeas Corpus.

This case having come on to be heard on the return of the Archer S. Bowlels sheriff.
The defendant and  D Ejbert his attorney and T.J. Anders on the part of the Territory
being present before his home. WG Langford Judge sitting in chambers. The evidence
being heard and argument by counsel. It is was adjudjed and decreed that the said
Geo Grant be discharged and released from the custody of said sheriff.

William G. Langford
Judge
