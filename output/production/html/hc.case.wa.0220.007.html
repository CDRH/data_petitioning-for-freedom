IN THE DISTRICT COURT OF THE UNITED STATES FOR THE WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON NORTHERN DIVISION.

IN THE MATTER OF THE APPLICATION OF ELSIE MILLER DICKINSON, ALIAS ELSIE MILLER, for a WRIT OF HABEAS CORPUS.

NO. 2965 ORDER DIRECTING ISSUANCE OF WRIT OF HABEAS CORPUS ETC.

The petition of Elsie Miller Dickinson, alias Elsie Miller, having been today presented to the court in open court praying that a Writ of Habeas Corpus issue to Henry M. White, Commissioner of Immigration, and alleging that the said Henry M. White unlawfully detains the said Elsie Miller Dickinson and deprives her of her liberty within the district and division aforesaid, and the court having duly examined such petition, it is now, after full consideration,

ORDERED that a Writ of Habeas Corpus in due form of law do forthwith issue out of and under the seal of this court to Henry M. White, Commissioner of Immigration, directing, commanding and requiring the said Henry M. White to produce before this court at a regular session thereof, appointed to be holden at the Federal Building in the City of Seattle, in said district, on the 24th day of February, 1915, at the opening of said court on said day, the body of said Elsie Miller Dickinson, alias Elsie Miller, and to then and there return said Writ with a full cause of the detention of the said Elsie Miller Dickinson full set forth.

It is further ORDERED that at the same time and place the said Henry M. White, Commissioner of Immigration, do certify to this court all the proceeding had before Albert H. Joy and Thos. W. Lynch, as Immigration Inspectors, and before 1   the said Henry M. White, or any other official of the Immigration Service in such district, subsequent to the 12th day of October, 1914, respecting the deportation of the said Elsie Miller Dickinson, alias Elsie Miller, to the end that such record having been inspected such order may be made herein as of right and justice ought to be made.

It is further ORDERED that the said Writ of Habeas Corpus contain a clause directing that the said Elsie Miller Dickinson, alias Elsie Miller, be released from custody of the said Henry M. White upon the filing in the office of the Clerk of this court a bond in the sum of one thousand dollars ($1000) conditioned for the obedience to any orders of this court in said matter made, by the said Elsie Miller Dickinson, and in the case of the discharge of said Writ of Habeas Corpus by this court rendition by the said Elsie Miller Dickinson of her said body into the custody of the said Henry M. White; such bond to be in a form to be approved by the United States District Attorney for this district and with sureties satifactory to said district attorney, and upon the filing of such a bond so approved, let the said Elsie Miller Dickinson, alias Elsie MIller, be discharged from custody pending the further order of the court in this proceeding.

Done in open court this 18th day of February, A. D. 1915.

Edward E. Cushman
Judge. 2
