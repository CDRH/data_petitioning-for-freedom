  • Summary of Proceedings: Jacob Dahr was arrested and jailed in St. Louis upon an accusation of having committed highway robbery in New Orleans and fleeing from justice. He asserted that the governor of Louisiana had not submitted the proper forms to retrieve him and petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, Dahr was remanded to the custody of his jailer.
