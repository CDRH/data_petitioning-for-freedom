IN THE DISTRICT COURT OF THE UNITED STATES FOR THE WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON NORTHERN DIVISION

IN THE MATTER OF THE APPLICATION OF GUSSIE GOLDMAN WOODS, ALIAS GUSSIE GOLDMAN, for a WRIT OF HABEAS CORPUS.

No. 2960 ORDER DIRECTING ISSUANCE OF WRIT OF HABEAS CORPUS ETC.

The petition of Gussie Goldman Woods, alias Gussie Goldman, having been today presented to the court in open court praying that a Writ of Habeas Corpus issue to Henry M. White, Commissioner of Immigration, and alleging that the said Henry M. White unlawfully detains the said Gussie Goldman Woods and deprives her of her liberty within the district and division aforesaid, and the court having duly examined such petition, it is now, after full consideration,

ORDERED, that a Writ of Habeas Corpus in due form of law do forthwith issue out of and under the seal of this court to Henry M. White, Commissioner of Immigration, directing, before this court at a regular session thereof, appointed to be holden at the Federal Building in the City of Seattle, in said district, on the 24th day of February, 1915, at the opening of said court on said day, the body of said Gussie Goldman Woods, alias Gussie Goldman, and to then and there return said Writ with a full cause of the detention of the said Gussie Goldman Woods fully set forth.

It is further ORDERED that at the same time and place the said Henry M. White, Commissioner of Immigration, do certify to this court all the proceedings hadbefore Albert H. Joy and Thos. W. Lynch, as Immigration Inspectors, and before   the said Henry M. White, or any other official of the Immigration Service in such district, subsequent to the 12th day of October, 1914, respecting the deportation of the said Gussie Goldman Woods, alias Gussie Goldman, to the end that such record having been inspected such order may be made herein as of right and justice ought to be made.

Done in open court this 17th day of February, 1915.

Edward H. Cushman
Judge.
