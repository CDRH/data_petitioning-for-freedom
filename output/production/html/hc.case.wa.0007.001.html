[WAL-2480]

Samuel F. Montgomery vs. Edwina Montgomery } Habeas Corpus

This cause coming up to be heard before S C Wingard Judge of said Court on this 31st day of May 1877 at his chambers in Walla Walla City, Kennedy and George Esqrs appearing as attys for Complainant and Thomas N Brents Esqr appearing on the part of said Defendant and the said minor child Myra Bell Montgomery being together with the said Defendant present before the Court and the Court having heard the argument of respective Counsel and duly Considered the matter. It is ordered that said Minor Child Myra Bell Montgomery be and the custody of said child is hereby awarded to said complainant Samuel Montgomery.
