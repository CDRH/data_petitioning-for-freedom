State of Missouri

County of St Louis } SS

Before me a Justice of the Peace within & for the county aforesaid cause August Homan and made oath that he did apprehend as a run away slave, William Blackburn a negro man so calling himself who pretends & says he is a free man but has no evidence of his freedom.

Sworn to & Subscribed before me this 11th April 1855 Louis De Breuil

Justice of the Peace }

August Homan
