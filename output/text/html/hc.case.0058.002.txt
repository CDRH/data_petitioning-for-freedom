









In the Dist Court holding terms at Port Townsend W.T.

In the application of Rob. Moulton for a writ of Habeas Corpus to James Eves Defendant
} Answer

To the Hon R.S. Greeves Judge of the above entitled Court

James Eaves said for return answer to the petition here in says

1. He denies that he illegaly restrains either of said children or that did so at
any time or that he restrains or has restrained them at all

For further defense he says:

That said children were placed in his care and custody by said petitioner over two
years ago and since have been

in his care and custody with his consent

2d That on the 14th day of Nov, 1883 before the petition in this cause was filed,
he placed said children in the Central Hotel at Port Townsend W.T. at the request
of said petitioner and there left them for the custody of the petitioner,

3d That the petitioner went there for them and attempted to take met them at the outer
door, on the street, alone, and attempted to take them away, and as defendant is informed
they refused to go and ran away from him. And still refuse to leave defendant and
go to the petitioner but insist on remaining with defendant

4h that at all said children are here subject to the order of this court, that defendant
makes no claim on said children what ever except they refuse to leave him.

Wherefore defendant prays the Court that the pe he be hence dismissed with his costs
and the Court will  make such  of said minors as shall seem meet
DW Wilken
for Deft
