IN THE DISTRICT COURT OF THE UNITED STATES FOR THE WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON, NORTHERN DIVISION.

In thematter of the application of Marie Robina Grayson alias Marie Robina, for a Writ of Habeas Corpus ) No. 2780 ORDER

WHEREAS there was heretofore filed in the above entitled matter a bond for the sum of one thousand ($1000.00) dollars on the Government's appeal herein, and it appearing that said appeal has by stipulation of the parties herein been dismissed, now therefore,

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the sureties on said bond be, and they hereby are discharged and released from any liability hereunder.

Dated this 28th 5th January Feb 1915.
