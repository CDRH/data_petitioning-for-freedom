  • Summary of Proceedings: Anna Sultz, bound party and petitioner was deprived of her liberty by Andrew Farar, Superintendent of the County Hospital in Douglas County, Nebraska. Sultz was placed in the County Hospital by the Board of Insanity after an application by her husband. She alleged that she was of sound mind and was not able to have a hearing or produce witnesses before the board. Judge Sutton finds that she is unlawfully deprived of her liberty, and she is released from custody.
