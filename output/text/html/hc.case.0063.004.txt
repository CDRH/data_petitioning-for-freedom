







In the District Court for the Third Judicial District of the Territory of Washington
holding terms at Seattle in and for the Counties of King, Kitsap &amp; Snohomish

In re Ah Sing and Ah Yoel } Habeas Corpus

And now Octo: 25 1873 this case was heard and signed at Chambers upon Petition, Writ
and Return, and the Court order that the above named prisoners be discharged from
Custody and that the Respondent Frederick Minick pay the costs of this proceeding
within 20 days from this date or attachment to issue
O Jacobs
Judge &amp;c
