U.S. Dist Court 2d Jud Dist W.T.

Catharine L. Martine vs Jesse Martin

Writ of Habeas Corpus for Mary M. Martin

Filed March 3d 1868

FHenry Clerk

I hereby appoint John Mills of Thurston County to serve the within process for me

JH Kellett Sheriff of Thurston County W.T.

The within come to hand on the 2d day of March 1868 and on the 3d day of said Month I served the same upon the within named Jesse Martin at the house of James Turner Thurston County W.T. by reading the contents of the same to him

J H Kellett Sheriff of Thurston Co W.T.

Served By John T. Mills deputy

Sheriffs Fees

Serving 2.00
