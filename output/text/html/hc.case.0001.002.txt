







To the Clerk of the District Court,

Let the writ issue as prayed in the complaint returnable before me at chambers at
Spokane Falls at 10 AM Thursday July 14th 1887. The writ is granted only for the purpose
of inquiring into the question of bail. Let notice of the time and place of hearing
before the prosecuting attorney

George Turner
Judge


In the matter of the application of Mary E. Teller for a writ of habeas corpus
Petition of Mary E. Teller
