In obedience to the command of the foregoing writ I have here in court the body of Henry Freeman and for my return say that he was committed to my custody on the twentieth day of July 1853 on warrant of commitment issued by Peter W Johnstone a Justice of the Peace within and for the County of St Louis on charge of being a runaway slave which warrant is made a part of this return St Louis August 5th 1853

James Castello Jailor
