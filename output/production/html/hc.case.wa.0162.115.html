Territory of Washington
County of Spokane} ss

Dr. Morgan, J.N. Huscka Pearl Worthingtom Ruby Stanly W Bonner TO Joseph Bradshaw, W.D. Gillespie, Jos Mabius In the name of the United States you are hereby required to appear before the undersigned, one of the Justices of the Peace in and for the said County, on the forthwith day of 188, at o'clock M. at his office in Spokane Falls W.T. to give evidence in a certain cause then and there to be tried between the Territory of Washington Plaintiff and Ray Raymond Defendant

On the part of the Defendant

Given under my hand this 29 day of June 1886

J.J.L. Peel
Justice of the Peace
