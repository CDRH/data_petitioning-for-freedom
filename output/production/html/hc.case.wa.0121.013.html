Recognizance.

Territory of Washington,
County of Columbia } SS

In Justice Court, before J.C. Dorr Justice of the Peace.

KNOW ALL MEN BY THESE PRESENTS, That I, A.L. McCauley acknowledge myself firmly held and bound unto the Territory of Washington in the sum of one hundred Dollars for which payment well and trurly to be made, I bind myself, my executors and administrators, if default shall be made in the condition following: The condition of this Recognizance is such, that if the said A.L. McCauley shall personally appear at the next term of the District Court to be held in and for said county, then and there to give evidence on the part of hte Territory against Jacob Schuman charged with the offense of selling Lager beer in violation of the "Local Option Law" alleged to have been committed in said county, on the 27th day of July A.D. 1886, as well to the Grand Jury as to the Petit Jury, and shall not depart the court without leave; then this Recognizance to be void, otherwise in full force

Executed and approved this 16th day
of August A.D. 1886, in presence of
J.C. Dorr
Justice of the Peace.

A.L. McCauley
