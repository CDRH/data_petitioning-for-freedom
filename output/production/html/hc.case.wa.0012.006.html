In the Probate Court of Whitman County Washington Territory before W. N. Ruby Probate Judge

In the matter of Amos Gustin and Catherine Gustin adopting Daisy Geneva Departte minor child of James G. Departte and Elizabeth Departte

The petition of Amos Gustin & Catherin Gustin this cause came on to be heard in open court Held at Olympia in Whitman County Wash T[?] on January 24th 1888 at the regular term of said Court on the petition of Amos Gustin and Catherine Gustin for leave to adopt Daisy Geneva Departte a minor child [?] by birth and for change of name and it appearing to the court after examing said petitioners on oath that said Amos Gustin and Catherine Gustin were husband and wife and inhabitants of Washington Territory and residing in Whitman County Wash Territory and desirous of adoption of said Daisy Geneva Departte and it farther appearing that said Daisy Geneva Departte was a minor child not three years of age and had no legal guardian and had been abadoned by her parents James Departte and Elizabeth Departte Thereupon the court appointed S. J. Chadwick (a desent and suitable person) to act in the proceedings as the next friend of said minor child and it farther appearing that the said S. J. Chadwick after an examination of the proceedings and parties was satisfied to have said persons adopt said child and had given his written consent to said adoption and it farther appearing to the court upon   an examination of Catherine Gustin separate and appart from her husband Amos Gustin that she of her own free will and [?] desired said adoption to adopt said Daisy Geneva Departte and it further appearing that the said petitioners and the Court being fully satisfied of the ability of the aforesaid petitioners to bring up and educate said Daisy Geneva Departte properly and also of the fitness and propriety of such adoption

It is therefore ordered that from this date towit January 24th 1888 said minor child Daisy Geneva Departte to all legal intents and purposes is the child of said petitioners Amos Gustin and Catherine Gustin and that the name of said child is hereby changed from Daisy Geneva Departte to Daisy Geneva Gustin

Done in open Court January 24th A. D. 1888

W. N. Ruby

Probate Judge
