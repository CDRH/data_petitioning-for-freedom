







In Justices Court Seattle Precinct King County W.T. Before W. Soderberg Justice of
the Peace

Territory of Washington Plaintiff vs Catharine Gilbert Defendant ) Commitment

United States of America

Territory of Washington}
County of King} ss

To any constable of said County and to the Keeper of the County Jail of said County
Greeting

Whereas at a Justice's Court held this day at the office of the undersigned in the
City of Seattle in said county before the undersigned a Justice of the peace of said
county for the trial of Catharine Gilbert for the offense hereinafter stated the said
Catharine Gilbert was convicted of having on the 28th 1st day of April 1888 in said
county committed the crime of disposing of beer to an Indian and upon conviction the
said court did adjudge and determine that the said Catharine Gilbert should pay a
fine of $100 and $- costs and should be imprisond in



the county Jail of King County W.T. for the period of fifty days or until the said
fine and costs be paid and Whereas the said Catharine Gilbert has made default in
the payment of said fine and costs. Therefore you the said H.G. Thornton are hereby
commanded forthwith to convey and deliver the said Catharine Gilbert to said Keeper
and you the said Keeper are hereby commanded to receive the said Catharine Gilbert
into your custody in said Jail and him  there keep for the space of 50 days unless
she shall sooner be thence discharged by due course of law.

Dated of Seattle WT this 3 day of May 1888

N. Soderberg
Justice of the Peace Seattle precinct King County W.T.
