  • Summary of Proceedings: On May 3rd, 1920, Ethel Siefken submitted a petition on behalf of her three children Marvel (9), Elmo (8), and Kennard (7). The children were in the custody of Mr. and Mrs. Wes Conley. The Siefkens also had three other children, Madeleine (5), Eva (4), and Helen (2), who Ethel apparently retained custody of. Mr. and Mrs. Conley claimed custody of these children based off of an agreement with their father, Albert Siefken. Ethel and Albert had filed for divorce in May of 1920. Ethel desired the custody of her children because she believed that they were not receiving proper care, attention, and companionship. The judge granted Ethel’s petition. W. C. Condit, Sheriff of Dodge County, was granted temporary custody of the children for the habeas proceedings. On May 6th, the writ was granted and Ethel was awarded custody of the three children.
