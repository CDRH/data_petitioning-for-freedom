









Territory of Washington }
County of Jefferson } SS

Robert Moulton being first duly sworn on oath says that he is the petitioner in the
above application for writ of Habeas Corpus that he makes said application on behalf
of Rosa and Nellie Moulton his minor children that he has read the foregoing petition
knows the contents thereof and believes them true
Robert Moulton

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 13h day of November 1883
C.M. Bradshaw
Notary Public
