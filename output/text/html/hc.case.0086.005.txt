







IN THE DISTRICT COURT,

Of the Third Judicial District of Washington Territory, holding Terms at Seattle,
for the Counties of King and Kitsap.

HABEAS CORPUS

The People of the United States of America to the Sheriff Mary Elizabeth Miller &amp;
RJ Miller of King County Greeting:

We command you, that you have the body of Mary Alice Powers by you imprisoned and
detained as it is said, together with the time and cause of such imprisonment and
detention, by whatsoever name said Mary Alice Powers shall be called or charged, before
the HON. ROGER S. GREENE Richard A Jones Judge of the aforesaid Court at his chambers
in the Court House in Seattle King County W.T. on the 10th day of April A.D. 1888
at the hour of 10 oclock AM to do and receive what shall then and there be considered
concerning the said Mary Alice Powers And have you then and there this writ.

WITNESS THE HON. ROGER S. GREENE Richard A. Jones Judge of said District Court, and
the seal thereof this 9th day of April A.D. 1888

W.E. Ledgewood James Seavey Clerk.
By  Deputy

Court Seal
