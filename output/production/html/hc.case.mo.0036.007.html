State of Missouri

County of St Louis

Wm Pennington & Michael Lawler appeared before me Liberty Waite a justice of the Peace in and for said County and being duly sworn says they have taken up a run away slave in city of and St Louis of said county and state, a negro about 23 years old who goes by the name of Alex S. Butler this affiant believes the said Alex S. Butler is a runaway slave and prays a commitment may be had for his safe keeping

Wm Pennington

Michael Lawlor his x mark

Sworn to and subscribed before me this 7th day of September 1854

Liberty Waite

Justice of the Peace
