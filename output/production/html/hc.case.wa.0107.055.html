Territory of Washington.
County of Chehalis.}

I hereby certify that I received the within warrant on the 16th day of September AD 1887 and the same on the 17th day of September AD 1887 by arresting and taking in custody the following named defendants, Gus Bendjus, Robert Strangland, whose true name is Robert Stangland, Ben Fredrickson, Frank Nelson, Andrew Johnson, Leander Brown, John Thompson, Alex Agren, Thomas Jacobs, and court not then being in session, and the judge of the within named District Court having reduced the bail required to $400.00 for each defendant under arrest, said amount of bail being for all charges against said parties. I did on the 19th day of October 1887, accept bail for the following named persons to wit Gus Bendjus, Robert Stauglaud, Ben Fredrickson, Frank Nelson, Andrew Johnson, Leadner Brown, Alex Agren, and Thomas Jacobs conditioned that they should appear at the next regular term of the District Court   to be held for Chehalis County, and from term to term thereafter until duly released by due process of law. In accepting said bail I discharged said parties from custody, and I have been unable after diligent search to find any of the other of the defendants named in the written warrant.

Dated this 14 day of November AD 1887.
W. H. Bush. Sheriff of Chehalis County
