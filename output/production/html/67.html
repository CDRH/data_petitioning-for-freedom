  • Summary of Proceedings: Estella Meyers was arrested at the insistence of Martin White for charges related to grand larceny. Her bail was fixed at 800 dollars, paid, and she was released on bail. Then, again she was arrested by White even with no complaint having been filed at that time. Judge Slabaugh decided that Meyers had been unlawfully detained, ordered her release, and for White to pay the court charges.
