I have the body of the within named Matilda before said court as within commanded she was on the twenty sixth instant taken out of the possession of Henry C Russell & Henry G Mitchel against whom warrant has issued for kidnapping the said Mitchel was arrested by the name of Martin Mitchel the said Matilda was put in the gaol of Jefferson County for safe keeping & has been by me detained for no other purpose than her personal safety

G. Hammand

May 30th 1832

Matilda

By the Habeas Corpus act
