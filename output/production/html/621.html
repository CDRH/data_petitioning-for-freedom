  • Summary of Proceedings: Herman and his accomplices unlawfully and fraudulently cheated and defrauded the United States National Bank of Omaha, First National Bank of Omaha, Union National Bank of Omaha, and Omaha Savings Bank. They pretended to be an organization called the Omaha and Council Bluffs railway Employees Association and that they had arranged a charity ball in Omaha to raise funds to establish a free hospital ward for railroad men and their families dependent on public charity. They claimed they were agents of said Employee Association and sold tickets for the ball, pretending they were admission. Herman alleges that he was wrongfully arrested and detained after an examination upon one certain complaint and the complaint didn’t charge a crime. Judge Ambrose ruled to keep Herman detained.
