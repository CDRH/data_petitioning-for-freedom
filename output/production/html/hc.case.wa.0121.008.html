In Justice's Court, Before J.C. Dorr, Justice of the Peace

An order having been made on this 27th day of July 1886 by J.C. Dorr a Justice of the Peace in and for Columbia County W.T., that Jacob Schuman, who was brought before said justice on a charge of selling malt liquor without license, be admitted to bail in the sum of Five Hundred dollars for his appearance before said justice to be examined upon said charge on 29th day of July 1886 at 10 o'clock A.M. of said day I L.E. Harris a resident of Columbia County, hereby undertake that the above named Jacob Schuman will appear before said justice at the time above named and will hold himself amenable to the orders of the Court, or if he fail to perform either of these conditions, that I will pay to the Territory of Washington the sum of five hundred dollars

L.E. Harris

Witness
J.C. Dorr
