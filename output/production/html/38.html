  • Summary of Proceedings: James Burns was arrested on authority of Lucas County, Iowa, for burglary and left. Being held by Douglas County, Burns filed Habeas petition on December 2 with attorney, A. S. Richie, against John J. Donahue. Burns charged that his arrest was wrong and that he had never spent time in the state of Iowa; therefore, Burns could not be a fugitive from justice. Petition was granted and writ was issued, but fate of Burns is unknown.
