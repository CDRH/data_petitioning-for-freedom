  • Summary of Proceedings: On May 20,1924, Fred Busch filed a writ of habeas corpus on behalf of himself after repeatedly being sent to the Nebraska State Hospital for the Insane at Lincoln by his wife, Anna Busch. Around May 18th or 19th, Fred and Anna experienced domestic difficulties, leading to Anna making an oral complaint. On May 19th, the Commissioners of Insanity caused a finding to be made without any hearing or trial and Fred Busch was arrested and confined in the Douglas County Jail. On June 5, 1924, the writ of habeas corpus and return was filed. The outcome of the case and the fate of the bound party is unknown.
