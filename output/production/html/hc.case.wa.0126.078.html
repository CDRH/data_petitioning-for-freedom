Territory of Washington County of Lincoln} ss

We the undersigned a jury empannelled by J.W. Carpenter a Justice of the Peace and Acting coroner in and for said county do find a verdict as follows.

That the deceased was A.L. Beardon that his death was caused by a pistol shot. That said shot was fired by one S. White alias Bud White and that said shot caused the death of A.L. Beardon.

C.J. Blackfan
H.F. Warren
J.A. Black
S.J. Short
Chas. G. Brindle
Wm. Robertson

 

Filed March 5th 1885
Kate W. Feauerbach
Clerk
