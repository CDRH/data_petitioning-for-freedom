  • Summary of Proceedings: Russell and Nettie Matson petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus to retrieve their children, Mary Alice Matson and Roscoe Brown, from the custody of chief probation officer of the juvenile court Sarah B. Gregory, police matron Dora Doyle, and probation officer Ida Pearce, which was granted. The two children had been held in a detention home since October 26 without a warrant or committment. The parties agreed to merge the cause with a related proceeding in the juvenile court. After hearing the evidence, the judge denied the Matsons' writ and remanded their children to the custody of the respondents.
