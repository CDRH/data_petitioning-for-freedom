  • Summary of Proceedings: Vera Baker petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus to retrieve Thomas Smiley from the custody of William and Pearl Smiley, which was granted. At the hearing, counsel for the petitioner requested a recess to gather evidence in support of the "character of the home of the petitioner and its surroundings," which was also granted. At a second hearing six months later, the court denied the writ and remanded Thomas to the custody of the Smileys. Vera twice excepted to this judgement, but nothing came of her efforts.
