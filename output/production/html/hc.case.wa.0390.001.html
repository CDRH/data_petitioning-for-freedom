Territory of Washington
County Walla Walla } SS

Before me, James K. Kennedy, Judge of the 1st judicial district of Washington Territory, personally appeared this 13th day of October, 1869, James McAuliff, Sheriff of Walla Walla County, who makes oath and says that he has reason to believe, and verily does believe that one John Suelling committed the crime of Grand Larceny in the County of Umatilla, State of Oregon, in the latter part of the year 1868; that he was committed to the County jail of said county on said charge, that he sawed his way out of said jail, and made his escape therefrom, and is now a fugitive from justice; that the Sheriff of Umatilla County has made an application to the Governor of the State of Oregon for a requisition on the Governor of Washington Territory for the body of the said John Suelling to answer said charge.

James McAuliff

Subscribed and sworn before me this 13th day of October, A. D. 1869

James K. Kennedy Judge &c.  

Aff of James McAuliff

Filed Oct. 13th 69

R P Reynolds
Clerk
