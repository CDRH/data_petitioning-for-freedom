







IN THE DISTRICT COURT

Of the Third Judicial District of Washington Territory, holding Terms at Seattle for
the Counties of King and Kitsap.

No 5043

HABEAS CORPUS

The People of the United States of America to the Sheriff of King County J.E. Howland
Greeting:

We command you,, that you have the bodies of C.E. Clayton, Geo Rosenthal, Wm. Lewis,
John McDonald, Charles Olsen, Chas Kales, Frederick Londeand C. Roschman by you imprisoned
and detained as it is said, together with the time and cause of such imprisonment
and detention, by whatsoever name C.E. Clayton, Geo Rosenthal, Wm. Lewis, John McDonald,
Chas Olsen, Chas Kales, Frederick Londe and C. Roschman shall be called or charged
before the Hon. Roger S. Greene John P. Hoyt Judge of the aforesaid District Court
of the Second Judicial District of Washingotn Territory at Olympia Thurston County
Washington Territory at his office in said City on the eleventh day of October 1886
at the hour of three oclock P.M. of said day to do and receive what shall then and
there by considered concerning the said C.E. Clayton, Geo Rosenthal, Wm. Lewis, John
McDonald, Charles Olsen, Charles Kales, Frederick Londe and C. Roschman. And have
you then and there this writ.

Witness the Hon. Roger S. Greene, Judge of said District Court, and the seal thereof
this Ninth day of October A.D. 1886
James Seavey, Clerk.
By Wm L. Ludlow Deputy
