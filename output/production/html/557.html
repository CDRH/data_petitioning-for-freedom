  • Summary of Proceedings: Blanche Finn, the petitioner, filed for a writ of Habeas Corpus after she was arrested without a legal warrant or authority by E. F. Brailey, sheriff. Finn was then convicted of vagrancy and challenged that conviction on the basis that the Police Magistrate had no jurisdiction to convict her of the sentence because the punishment exceeded three months imprisonment. Judge Sutton found that the petition should be granted.
