No 135

In the District Court at Montesano:

Territory of Washington, }
vs }
Gus Bendjus Et Al}

The Grand Jury of Chehalis county having returned into this Court an indictment against the defendants in the above named cause charging them with the crime of Malicious mischief and it appearing to the court that said defendants are not in custody or under recognizance to appear and answer in this Court, it is ordered that the clerk of this Court issue a warrant for the arrest of said defendants forthwith

Dated September 16th 1887

Frank Allyn
Judge.

Comes now the Grand Jury and by their foreman in the presence of the said Grand Jury present to the Court one indictment against Gus Bendjus Robert Stranglant Ben Fredrickson, Frank Nelson Andrew Johnson, Leander Brown John Thompson, Alex Agren, Thomas Jacobs; for the Crime of Malicious Mischief which they say they find a true Bill in which twelve of their number concur which indictment is secured by the Court and by the Clerk duly filed in open Court in the presents of said Grand Jury

Frank Allyn
Judge.
