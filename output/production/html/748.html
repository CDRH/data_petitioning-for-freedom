  • Summary of Proceedings: Fred Neff claimed to have been unlawfully deprived of his liberty by Henry Dunn and Steve Maloney. He claimed to have been taken before Vince Hasacall (Police Magistrate) and was given an appeal bond to the sum of $300. Neff was waiting to see if Hascall would take a cash bond or would require a personal bond. However, twenty four hours later, Hascall came to the conclusion that he wouldn’t take a cash bond and that his chance for a personal bond had expired. Therefore he claimed to be illegally restrained of his liberty. The petition was granted, however the fate of the bound party is unknown.
