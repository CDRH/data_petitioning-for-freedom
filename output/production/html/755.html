  • Summary of Proceedings: Austin Campbell was arrested on June 23, by Chief of Police, John Briggs. Mary Campbell and attorney John M. Macfarland, petitioned on behalf of her husband, Austin Campbell, on June 28 for habeas against John Briggs. Mary Campbell argued to Judge Lee S. Estelle that no charge has been brought against her husband since arrest and that he is being held too long under suspicious character - claiming this to be a violation of the statute of the State of Nebraska. A different judge, Willis G. Sears, issues writ. On June 29, the writ was sustained, and Austin Campbell was released.
