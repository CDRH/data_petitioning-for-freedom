Territory of Washington County of Spokane

In the matter of Mary Teller

Whereas it has been made duly to appear to the court by the sheriff of said county that the above named Mary Teller was on the 10th day of June 1887 by John J Booge Esq a Justice of the Peace in and for said County duly committed to his custody to abide the action of the Grand Jury of said county on a charge of murder in the First degree in default of entering into recognizance to appear before said court at its next regular term to be held in the said county in the sum of five thousand dollars and it being made to appear to the court that there is no safe or proper jail or place of confinement in said Spokane County wherein said Mary Teller may be placed or kept in custody and that there is a safe and proper jail in the City of Sprage in Lincoln County in the Fourth Judicial District

 

It is therefore ordered that said Mary Teller be and hereby is committed to the Custody of the sheriff of said Lincoln County and he is hereby commanded to receive said Mary Teller into his custody and her safely to keep until duly discharged from his custody by the further order of the court

George Turner

Judge
