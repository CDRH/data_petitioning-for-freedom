  • Summary of Proceedings: On November 28, 1877, Joseph Wells filed to retrieve custody of his minor wife, Matty, from her mother and step-father, Martha and William Church. Joseph alleged that on June 26 Martha and William had "forcibly" kidnapped Matty and that William did so in order to "violate her chastity." William and Martha claimed that Matty came to their home willingly and was staying out of her own desire. In response, Joseph denied their claims and stated that the defendants threatened to kill him if he attempted to speak to Matty. While the judge's decision in the case is missing from the file, it appears as though Matty returned to live with Joseph from an 1880 census record.
