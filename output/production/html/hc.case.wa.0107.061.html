The Grand Jury of the County of Chehalis Territory of Washington composed of good and lawful persons from the body of the Second Judicial District, and for the County aforesaid, duly elected, impanneld sworn and charged to inquire of and true presentment make to the said Disctrict Court of the regular September term thereof A D 1887 of the said District Court begun and held at Montesano in the said County of Chehalis in the said County of Chehalis, in the District aforesaid on the 12th day of September A.D. 1887 of all offenses commited   Page 2 in the said County against the Territory of Washington on their oaths accuse:

Gus Bendjus - Robert Stranglaud - Ben Frederickson - Pete Peterson - Frank Nelson Fred Stromberger - John Blending - Andrew Johnson - Leander Brown - John Saxton John Thompson - Alex Agren - Thomas Jacobs - E. Emel - Andrew Anderson and Charles Burke by this indictment of the crime of Riot commited as follows, to writ the said Gus Bendjus - Robert Stranglaud - Ben Frederickson - Pete Peterson - Andrew Johnson Leander Brown - John Saxton - John Thompson - Alex Agnew - Thomas Jacobs - E. Emel Andrew Anderson and Charles Burke on the 9th day of September A D 1887 and in the night time, and within one year next proceeding the date hereof, in the said County of Chehalis, in the said Second Judicial District, in the Territory of Washington then and there being with certain other ill disposed and and evil minded persons to the grand jury unknown of the number of one hundred or upwards, with force and arms, and guns, pistols & knives did assemble and gather themselves together, and being so assembled & gathered together, the day and year aforesaid and in the night time thereof, at and in the said County of Chehalis & Territory of Washington the pound net the property of C.B.   Page 3 Allen and A.T. Harris of the value of six hundred dollars, and the pound net the property of and blonging to F. Colbert and the Aberdeen Packing Company of the value of one thousand five hundred dollars and the fish trap the property of and belonging to C.H. Gilberth and [] Campbell of the value of six hundred dollars, and the pound net of Walt Fridenberg and John Hunter such parties being the owners thereof, and of the value of six hundred dollars, and the pound nets the property of and belonging to Wm Church of the value of eight hundred dollars, and the pound nets that value of $600.00 beloning to one Wilson Graham, all of the said property, then and being in the said county and territory aforesaid, with knives and other implements to the Grand Jury unknown willfully, unlawfully, feloneously and in a vioent and tumultous manner, cut, tear, savage, raige[?] and pull down and destroy and to the great damage of the said C.B. Allen and A.T. Harris, F Colbert, and the Aberdeen Packing Company, C.H. Gilberth and [] Campbell, Walt Fredenborg and John Hunter, Wm Church, and Wilson Graham and to the evil example, and great terror of all good citizens of this common wealth.
