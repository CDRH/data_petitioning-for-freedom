  • Summary of Proceedings: Milly and her two children, Eliza (6) and Bob (2), filed for a writ of habeas corpus against Mathias Rose at St. Ferdinand in the County of St Louis. Milly and her two children were unlawfully under custody of Rose and his family. The court utilized the act of the legislature of 1807 to release Milly and her children from Rose and Milly was granted the sum of $500.
