Office of the Sheriff of King County W.T.

I J H McGraw, Sheriff of King County W.T. do hereby return the Hon. R. S. Greene, Judge of the District Court holding terms at Seattle, that before the giving to me of the annexed writ the said Qui was committed to my custody and is detained by virtue of another writ a copy of which is hereto annexed: the original of which I also herewith produce; nevertheless I have the body of said Qui before you at the time and place mentioned in said annexed writ.

Dated at Seattle, the 18th day of November 1884

J. H. McGraw Sheriff

Sheriff's fees: Bringing up Prisoner 1.00 Copy of Writ .80 Travel 2 miles .20 certificate .50 $2.50
