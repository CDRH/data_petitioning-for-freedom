  • Summary of Proceedings: J. B. and Sarah Montgomery divorced in 1871. The Douglas County Circuit Court ordered that J. B., not Sarah, should have custody of their daughter, Mary Montgomery. Sarah refused to comply with the court order, prompting J. B. to petition for a writ of habeas corpus in Marion County, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, the Marion County Circuit Court awarded custody of Mary to J. B., found Sarah to be in contempt, and ordered her to pay all court fees.
