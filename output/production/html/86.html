  • Summary of Proceedings: Laura M. Parker (formerly Laura M. McCaulley) filed a writ of Habeas Corpus on behalf of her two children, Joyce McCaulley and Ray McCaulley Jr. on April 26th, 1923. Laura claimed that the two children were being deprived of their liberties by being held at Nebraska Eastern Star Home. Sadie Boorman and Bess M. Bauer, representatives of the Star Home claimed that the children were being rightfully kept their, as Ray McCaulley, the children's father had placed them there and paid for their expenses. On January 27th, 1925, the case was dismissed, and the fate of the children are unknown.
