Garret S Van Wagoner[?] being duly sworn says that on the 1st day of November 1856, he at the request of the Petitioner whose name is signed to the above petition demanded of the person in charge of the Saint Louis County Gaol of one Louis Musick who is in the custody of John Robert F Logan Jailer or Keeper of the Jail in Saint Louis County a copy of the warrant order in process by which Eleck Loan a slave belonging to one Joseph W Loan is held in confinement by said Logan and was refused a copy of the same by said Louis Musick.

G S Van Wagoner

Sworn to and subscribed 1st 1856 before me

M William
