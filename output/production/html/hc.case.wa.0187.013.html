UNITED STATES OF AMERICA. DISTRICT COURT FOR THE WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON.

In re Marie Robina Grayson, alias Marie Robina. ORDER.

2780

The petiton of Marie Robina Grayson, alias Marie Robina, having been to day presented to the Court, in open Court, praying that a writ of habeas corpus issue to Henry M. White, Commissioner of Immigration directed, and alleging that the said Henry M. White, unlawfully detains the said Marie Robina Grayson, and deprives her of her liberty, within the District and Division afroresaid, and the Court having duly examined such petition, it is now, after full consideration,

ORDERED that a writ of habeas corpus in due form of law do forthwith issue out of and under the seal of this Court, to Henry M. White, Commissioner of Immigration, directed, commanding and requiring the said Henry M. White, to produce before this Court, at a regular session thereof, appointed to be holden at the Federal Building, in the City of Seattle, in said District on the 3rd day of July, 1914, at the opening of said Court on said day, the body of said Marie Robina Grayson, alias Marie Robina, and to then and there return said writ, with a full cause of the detention of the said Marie Robina Grayson, fully set forth.

IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that at the same time and place the said Henry M. White, Commissioner of Immigration, do certify to this Court all the proceedings had before Charles A. Turner as County Immigration Inspector, and before the said Henry M. White, or any other official of the Immigration service in such District, subsequent to the 1st day of January, 1914, respcting the deportation of the said Marie Robina Grayson, alias Marie Robina, to the end that such record having been inspected such order may be made therein as of right and justice ought to be made.

-1-  
6/27/14 S. E. L.

IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that the said writ of habeas corpus contain a clause directing that the said Marie Robina Grayson, be enlarged and released from custody of the said Henry M. White, upon the filing in the office of the Clerk of this Court, in the sum of two thousand dollars a bond, conditioned for the obedience to any orders of this Court, in said matter made, by the said Marie Robina Grayson, and in case of the discharge of said writ of habeas corpus by this Court rendition by the said Marie Robina Grayson, of her said body into the custody of the said Henry M. White, such bond to be in a form to be approved by the United States District Attorney, for this District, and with sureties satisfactory to said District Attorney, and upon the filing of such bond, so approved, let the said Marie Robina Grayson, alias Marie Robina be discharged from custody pending the further order of this Court, in this proceeding.

Done in open Court this 27th day of June, 1914.

By the Court

Jeremiah Neterer
Judge. Ok. Allen
