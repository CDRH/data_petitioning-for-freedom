September the 28th AD 1887

To Will Billings
Sheriff and Jailer of Thurston County

I herewith put into your care, for safe keeping, there being no jail in Chehalis county, the following named persons arrested, on warrants duly recieved out of the District Court of the Second Judicial District, holding terms at Montesano in and for the county of Chehalis, said warrants being issued on indictments brought against the said folling persons, by the Grand Jury of Chehalis County on September 15th 1887. The names of the jailed as follows:

Gus Bendjus, Robert Strangland, Ben Frederickson, Frank Nelson, Andrew Johnson, Leander Brown, John Thompson, Alex Agren, Thomas Jacobs. You will keep these parties until I come for them, unless sooner discharged by law.

WH Bush
Sheriff of Chehalis County
Wash. Terry.
