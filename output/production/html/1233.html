  • Summary of Proceedings: James Warden petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus to retrieve his six children from the St. Thomas Orphanage, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, the writ was denied and Warden ordered to pay the court costs. He appealed the decision to the state supreme court, which upheld the decision of the lower court.
