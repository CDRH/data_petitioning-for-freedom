IN THE DISTRICT COURT OF THE UNITED STATES FOR THE WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON NORTHERN DIVISION.

*******

IN THE MATTER OF THE APPLICATION OF CELIA BERG O'NEILL FOR WRIT OF HABEAS CORPUS.

No. 2963 ORDER

*******

It appearing that Celia Berg O'Neill was held by Henry M.White United States Commissioner of Immigration, at Seattle, upon a charge of being an alien prostitute, and that application was made by Celia Berg for a writ of Habeas Corpus, which writ was issued herein directed to said Henry M.White to produce said Celia Berg before this court that inquiry might be made as to the regularity of such detention, and it after ward appearing that said Celia Berg O'Neill was the wife of Fred O'Neil a native born American citizen and that no legal cause existed for the detention of said Celia Berg O'Neill and a stipulation having been entered into by Clay Allen, U.S.District Attorney, and Gill, Hoyt & Frye, attorneys for said Celia Berg that said Celia Berg be discharged and an order of dismissal of the above proceedings be entered.

NOW, THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the discharge of said Celia Berg O'Neill be approved and the above entitled proceeding be dismissed.

Jeremiah Neterer
Judge.
