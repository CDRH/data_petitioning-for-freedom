1832
Ex. "A."

Territory of Washington}

County of Pierce} ss.

S. L. Humphrey being duly sworn by me complains that Prospero Depelippes Felip Perell are seamen on board the bark Eldora of which he is the master, that said bark is now in the port of Tacoma Washington Territory that said Seamen is under written contract to return to San Franciso in said Bark as seaman thereof, that said bark is now loading and will be ready for sea in about ten days that said vessel is an American vessel and that said Seamen has willfully on the 27th day of June 1886 at Tacoma deserted said vessel without leave and has refused to do duty thereon, and he prays that a warrant may issue against said seaman and that they be dealt with according to law

S. L. Humphrey

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 29th day of June 1886

Alex Campbell

Justice of the Peace
