  • Summary of Proceedings: Martin D. Reilly and Christy Reilly, the petitioners, adopted Ruth Reilly in October of 1920 with a decree from the Juvenile Divison of the Circuit Court of New Madrid County, Missouri. Ruth Reilly was then unlawfully detained by Mrs. Elizabeth Reilly and Mrs. Ellen Dennett. Martin and Christy Reilly then filed a writ of Habeas Corpus is issued so they could possess sole custody of Ruth. Judge Leslie ordered the writ of Habeas Corpus to be allowed, and Martin and Christy Reilley were issued custody of Ruth.
