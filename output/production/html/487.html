  • Summary of Proceedings: George Schuler refused to pay a fine for "Keeping for sale malt, spirituous, and vinus liquors without a license or druggist permit." The country judge of Sarpy County, Edgar Howard, ordered his arrest for refusing to pay the fine. John W. McDonald arrested him in Douglas county. Schuler said that he was unlawfully restrained and that the mittimus was defective and that the sheriff had no authority to hold him.
