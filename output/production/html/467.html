  • Summary of Proceedings: On August 8, 1921, Emma Morgan was arrested in Omaha and held for investigation without charge; however, on August 10, Morgan was charged with vagrancy and sentenced to serve 90 days in jail. On August 15, Morgan provided an appeal bond of $500.00, and the magistrate approved this appeal bond and ordered her release. Morgan remained imprisoned, and as a result, she filed a petition for a writ of Habeas Corpus on August 16, 2021. The outcome of the case, as well as Morgan's fate, is unknown.
