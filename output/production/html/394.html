  • Summary of Proceedings: John Whitney petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus to retrieve his daughter, Mary Whitney, from the custody of his late wife's parents, Nathan and Irena Tharpe, which was granted. This may have been because he remarried in 1906. After hearing the evidence, the writ was allowed and Mary remanded to the custody of her father. The court, however, required that John allow Mary to spend at least ten days with her grandparents at least twice a year in order to keep custody.
