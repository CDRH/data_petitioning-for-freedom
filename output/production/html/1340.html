  • Summary of Proceedings: Ubaldino was ordered for deportation on the accusation that he violated the Immigration Act of February 5, 1917, by attempting to enter the U.S. through Canada, of which he was a resident at the time for over ten years. He was also deported from the U.S. to Canada before this petition, upon the ground that Ubaldino has attempted to import a woman for immoral purpose. Ubaldino petitioned for habeas, and ordered a show of cause. Because there was no evidence that Ubandino was native to Italy outside of his father being from the country, he was ordered for deportation to Canada.
