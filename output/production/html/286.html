  • Summary of Proceedings: Charles G. Runker was charged with setting fire to the property of himself and Merwin H. Howes of the value of over $1,300. He was alleged to have burned the building with the intent to defraud the Northern Insurance Company of London. He was arrested by Henry W. Dunn and Patrick Mostyn and then later filed a writ alleging that he was deprived of his liberty and is held without warrant, without writ of detention, copy of commitment, and not a fugitive from justice. His writ was allowed but later the case was dismissed.
