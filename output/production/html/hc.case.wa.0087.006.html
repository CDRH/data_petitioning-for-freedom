Territory of Washington}
County of King} SS

In the District Court holding terms at Seattle

In the matter of the application for a writ of Habeas Corpus by Wong Sam and others} No

The United States of America to E.V. Rugar Greeting

You are hereby commanded to have the bodies of Wong Sam Foke Gee Ah Moke Chin Sam Ah Zee and Wong Hing by you imprisoned and detained as it is said, together with the time and cause of such imprisonment and detention by whatsoever name the said plaintiffs may be called, before Hon Richard A Jones Judge of the Third Judicial District of the said Territory of Washington at his chambers in the Court house in the City of Seattle on the 13 day of April 1888 at the hour of 2 oclock PM of that day to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning the   said Wong Sam Foke Gee Ah Moke Chin Sam Ah Zee and Wong Hing and have you then and there all certificates and papers received or taken by you from said plaintiffs and have also then and there this writ

Witness Hon Richard A Jones Judge of said District Court this 11th day of April 1888

Attest my hand and the seal of said Court the day and year last above written

WE Ledgerwood

Clerk
