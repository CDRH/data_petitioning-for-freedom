  • Summary of Proceedings: Amanda S. Olsen, the petitioner, was the mother of Mable Alfreda Johnson and was awarded custody of her daughter in a previous legal proceeding against her former husband, August Johnson. Yet, August Johnson continued to detain Mable by violence and force. Judge Sutton, therefore, found that Alfreda Johnson was unlawfully deprived of her liberty and issued a writ of habeas corpus on her behalf.
