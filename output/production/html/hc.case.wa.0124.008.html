In re
Application of Daniel Ainsworth for a writ of Habeas Corpus

Order–

 

Territory of Washington County of Spokane SS

I P.W. Dillon Sheriff of Spokane County said Territory

Do hereby certify that I served a copy of the anexed indictment–an its within named Defendant Daniel Ainsworth in the Courthouse in Cheney in Spokane County said Territory on this the 11th day of October 1884 at about the hour of four o'clock of that day. By delivering to W.R. Andrews. The attorney of the said Daniel Ainsworth a copy of said indictment at the above mentioned time and place

Dated this 11th Day of Oct. 1884

P.W. Dillon
Sheriff of Spokane County

Sheriffs Fees $1.20
