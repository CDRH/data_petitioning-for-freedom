In the matter of the Application of Ah Korn for writ of Habeas Corpus

No 4097

And now on this 11th day of Septem A.D. 1884 this cause comes on to be heard before the Judge of this Court at Chambers upon the petition of Applicant and the return of the respondant James H. Woolery and the petitioner appearing in person and by T. J. Hurnes his Attorney and the respondant appearing in person and by J. T. Ronald his attorney and the Court after hearing the argument of Counsel and upon consideration finds that the said Ah Korn is illegally restrained of his liberty by the said respondent in the said city of Seattle as alleged in said petition.

It is therefore considered, ordered and adjudged that the said Ah Korn be discharged from Custody, and that the said respondant pay the costs herein to be taxed for which let execution issue.
