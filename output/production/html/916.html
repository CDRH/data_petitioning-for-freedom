  • Summary of Proceedings: Frank Dolezal committed the offense of disturbing the peace in violation of ordinance 470 of the city of South Omaha. He was found guilty, sentenced to pay a fine, and imprisoned for eight days on hard labor. Dolezal tried to appeal and pay the fine and bond, but the magistrate did not allow the appeal. Finally, the writ of habeas corpus was allowed and he was allowed to leave on bail.
