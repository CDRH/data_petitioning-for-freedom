IN THE DISTRICT COURT,

Of the Third Judicial District of Washington Territory, holding Terms at Seattle, for the Counties of King and Kitsap.

HABEAS CORPUS.

The People of the United States of America, to the Sheriff of King County Greeting:

We command you, that you have the body of Sarah McDunnah by you imprisoned and detained as it is said, together with the time and cause of such imprisonment and detention, by whatsoever name said Sarah McDunnah shall be called or charged, before the HON Richard A. Jones ROGER S. GREENE, Judge of the aforesaid Court at the Court House in the City of Seattle W.T. in June 18th 1888 at 5 30 oclock P.M. of said day to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning the said Sarah McDunnah. And have you then and there this writ.

WITNESS THE HON Richard A. Jones ROGER S. GREENE, Judge of said District Court, and the seal thereof this 18th day of June A.D. 1888

W.E. Ledgerwood JAMES SEAVEY, Clerk.

By H T. Hannon Deputy
