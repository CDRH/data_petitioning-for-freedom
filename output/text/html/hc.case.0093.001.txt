









#6666

In the District Court of the 3rd Judicial District in and for the County of King Territory
of Washington.

Territory of Washington,}
County of King} SS

In the matter of Jimmy Jardine alias Russell Habeas Corpus}

And now comes the said prisoner by his Atty John E. Costello and says that he is at
present unable to procure the services of an Atty for pay and that he is unable to
pay necessary clerk fees upon the filing of this petition for Writ of Habeas Corpus
and asks that the same be filed

Jimmy Jardine alias Russell
by his Atty John E. Costello

Subscribed and sworn to before me
