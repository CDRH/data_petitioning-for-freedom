







In Probate Court 
In the matter of custody of Geneva Departe

Filed Jan 24 
clerk of Probate Court for Whitman



In the Probate Court of Whitman County Washington Territory before WN Ruby Probate
Judge

In the the matter of Amos Gustin and Catherine Gustin adopting Daisy Geneva Departte
minor child of James G. Departte and Elizabeth Departte

I SJ Chadwick guardian ad latern of Daisy Gevena Departte having personaly examined
Amos Gustin and Catherine Gustin under oath and being satisfied that said minor child
Daisy Geneva Departte has been abandoned by her parents and that the above named Amos
Gustin and Catherine Gustin are suitable persons to adopt said child and have the
property and ability to bring up and educate her I hereby give my consent for them
to adopt her

S. J Chadwick
