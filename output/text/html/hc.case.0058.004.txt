








In the District Court of the 3d Judicial District of Washington Territory holding
Terms at Port Townsend

The President  of the United States To James Eaves     Greeting

We command you that you have the bodies Rosa Moulton and Nellie Moulton by you illegally
detained as it is said, together with the cause of said detention, by whatsoever names
said Rosa Moulton and Nellie Moulton shall be called before the Hon R.S. Greene Judge
of of the above Entitled Court at the hour of half past one oclock P.M. of the 14th
day of November 1883 at the Court house in Port Townsend, to do and receive what shall
then and there be considered, concerning the same.
And have you there and then this writ

Witness the Hon RSGreen
Judge of said District Court this 14th day of November 1883

Attesting here and the  of said cause this day
