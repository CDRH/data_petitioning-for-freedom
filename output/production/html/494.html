  • Summary of Proceedings: Lafayette Robinson was being detained for unspecified reasons. Jane Williams, whose relationship to Robinson is unclear, petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus on his behalf, which was granted. In the meantime, Robinson posted a $200 bail and was released. The respondents failed to answer the writ, and so Robinson was released from their custody. Peter Johnstone, the police chief, was ordered to pay Williams' court costs.
