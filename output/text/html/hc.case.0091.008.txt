







In Justice Court Seattle Precinct King C. WT

Before N Soderberg Justice of the Peace

Territory of Washington Plaintiff
vs
Thomas Gilbert Defendant

Commitment

United States of America
Territory of Washington County of King} SS

To any Constable of said County, and to the Keeper of the County Jail of said County=
Greeting:

Whereas at a Justice Court held this day at the office of the undersigned in the City
of Seattle in said County before the undersigned a Justice of the Peace of said County
for the trial for the offence hereinafter stated, the said  was convicted of having
on the 1st day of May 1888 in said County committed the crime of disposing of beer
to an Indian and upon conviction the said Court did adjudge and determine that said
Thomas Gilbert should pay a fine of $100 00 and should be imprisoned in the County
Jail of King County W.T. for the period of 50 days or until the said fine &amp; costs
be paid and whereas the said Thomas Gilbert has made default in the payment of the
said fine and costs: Therefore you the said H.G. Thornton are hereby commanded forthwith
to convey and deliver the said Thomas Gilbert to said Keeper and you, the said Keeper
are hereby commanded to receive the said Thomas Gilbert into your

over



custody on said Jail and have there Keep for the space of 50 Days unless he shall
sooner be thence discharged by due course of law

Dated at Seattle W.T. this 3d day of May 1888

N. Soderberg
Justice of the Peace
Seattle, King County W.T.
