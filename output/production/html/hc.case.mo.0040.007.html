Aug 14' 58 Evidence heard & prisoner dischd of 265

Petition for Habeas Corpus

JW Sharp atty

In the matter of Writ William Edwards

10th of August 1858

"By the Habeas Corpus Act"

To the Jailor of St Louis County

Prisoner discharged

In obedience to the command of the foregoing writ I have the body of the within named defendant Wm Edwards hear at open court and for my return say that he was committed to my custody on the 2 day of June th 1857 on warrant issued by Justice Johnson on charge of being a Runaway Slave which warrant of commitment is hear to arrest & maid a part of this return this 14 day of August 1858

Chas Rodeman Jailor by JL Musick Dept
