Territory Washington County of Spokane

RD Riorden being first duly sworn deposes and says: That he is sheriff of Lincoln County W.T. and keeper of the common jail of said County. That in obedience to the annexed writ of habeas corpus he has the body of the written named Mary E. Teller now in court.

That he restrains said Mary E. Teller of her liberty as such sheriff and keeper of the common jail of said Lincoln by virtue of a commitment given under the hand of the Hon. George Turner Judge of the District Court for said County a copy of which is hereto attached

RD Roridan

Sheriff

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 14th day of July 1887

George Turner

District Judge
