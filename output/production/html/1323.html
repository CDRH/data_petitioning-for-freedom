  • Summary of Proceedings: George Davis was charged with two complaints of forgery and the possession of forged instruments in police court in July of 1898. Upon conviction, Davis appealed to the Nebraska Supreme Court, and the conviction was reversed, and he was ordered a new trial. Judge Cunningham then finds that Davis is deprived of his liberty unlawfully and discharged from custody.
