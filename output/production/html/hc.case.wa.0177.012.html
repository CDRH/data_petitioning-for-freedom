IN THE UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT FOR THE WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON, SOUTHERN DIVISION.

IN THE MATTER OF THE APPLICATION OF TONKA BAJLO, for Writ of Habeas Corpus.

No.1328. ORDER TO SHOW CAUSE.

The above named petitioner having made application to this Court for a Writ of Habeas Corpus addressed to the Hon. Ellis DeBruler, Commissioner of Immigration, in charge of the Immigration Service, at Seattle, in said District, in whose custody the petition shows the petitioner is confined and restrained of her liberty pursuant to certain proceedings looking to her deportation under the Act of Congress of Mar. 26th.1910; and the Court being duly advised, now on motion of Chas.H.Ennis, counsel for petitioner,

IT IS ORDERED that the Hon.Ellis De Bruler, Commissioner of Immigration at Seattle, in said District, and the Inspector or other person in charge of the person of TONKA BAJLO, the petitioner, show cause before this Court at the Court Room in the Federal Building, in the City of Seattle, in said District, on Monday, April 21st. 1913 at 2 p.m. why said application should not be granted and in the meantime, you, the Honorable Ellis DeBruler, Commissioner as aforesaid, as well as the Inspectors or other person in charge of the person of the said TONKA BAJLO, refrain and desist from deporting or removing the said petitioner from said District, or carrying into effect any warrant or order for her deportation which may be held by you or either of you.

 

IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that a certified copy of this Order shall be served upon the said Ellis DeBruler, and the Inspector, or either of them, or any person in charge of the person of the said TONKA BAJLO, on or before April 18,1913.

Done this 17th day of April, A.D.1913.

Edward E Cushman
Judge.
