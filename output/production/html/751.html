  • Summary of Proceedings: In October 1924, Perry Hartness petitioned for a writ of Habeas Corpus on the grounds he was being unlawfully detained of his liberty by Michael L. Endres, Sheriff of Douglas County, The petition alleged that Hartness was placed under arrest on October 11th, 1924, released on a cash bond of $25, to appear in Court on the on the 13th. Hartness stated he was placed in county jail for no known reason, and he has not committed any crime nor violated any law. Judge Stauffer allowed the writ, and the appearance docket states the application was dismissed.
