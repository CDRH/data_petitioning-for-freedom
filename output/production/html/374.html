  • Summary of Proceedings: William Thayer petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus to retrieve eleven-year-old John Medford from Thomas McNair, which was granted. Thayer claimed to be Medford's court-appointed guardian, while McNair claimed that Medford's mother had asked him to take care of the child on her deathbed. After hearing the evidence, Medford was released into Thayer's custody.
