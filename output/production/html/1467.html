  • Summary of Proceedings: The case file is missing so the exact facts of the case are unknown. The Appearance Docket stated that it was ordered that Sadie Walker be discharged from custody on a $500.00 bail with T. E. Dailey listed as the bail surety. On 17 February 1914, the order directing the respondents to release Walker from custody was filed. Sheriff McShane’s Writ Return lists Omaha Chief of Police Henry W. Dunn, Omaha City Jailer Stephan Maloney, and Ella Gibbons as the respondents.
