The State of Missouri

To William Lawler, Greeting

You are commanded to have the body of Eliza Duncan before the Law Commissioners Court of Saint Louis County, on the 14th day of September 1854 at 3 Oclock P.M. together with the time and cause of her detention by you to do and receive what shall then and there be adjudged concerning said Eliza Duncan, who as alleged in the annexed petition is illegally restrained of her liberty by you, and have you then and there this writ with your return thereon

Given under my hand and the seal of the Law Commissioners Court at Saint Louis Sept 14" 1854

Jno W. Colvin

Law Com
