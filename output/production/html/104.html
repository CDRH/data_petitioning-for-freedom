  • Summary of Proceedings: Sulton Shoenbrum claimed that he was unlawfully arrested by John J. Donahue without a warrant. According to Shoenbrum, a man that was referred to as John Doe (Real name was unknown), claimed to be an officer of the state of Pennsylvania that sought to arrest and extradite him back to Pennsylvania to face criminal charges. However, Shoenbrum said that John Doe neglected to have proper extradition papers. The writ was allowed and he was released from custody.
