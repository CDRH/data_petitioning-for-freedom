IN THE DISTRICT COURT,

Of the Third Judicial District of Washington Territory, holding Terms at Seattle, for the County of King.

HABEAS CORPUS

The People of the United States of America to the Sheriff of King County T. H. Hamilton U.S. Marshal Greeting:

We command you, that you have the body of Leo Sun by you imprisoned and detained as it is said, together with the time and cause of such imprisonment and detention, by whatsoever name said Leo Sun shall be called or charged, before the HON. RICHARD A. JONES C. H. Hanford, Judge of the aforesaid Court at the Court House in the city of Seattle on June 8th AD 1889 at the hour of one o'clock P.M. of said day to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning him the said Leo Sun And have you then and there this writ.

Witness the HON. RICHARD A. JONES C. H. Hanford Judge of said District Court, and the seal thereof this 4th day of June A. D. 1889

RM Hopkins Clerk.

By H.T. Hannon Deputy Clerk.
