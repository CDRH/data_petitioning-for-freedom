No 135

Territory of Washington vs. Gus Beudjus & al}

Comes now the Grand Jury and by their foreman into presence of the said Grand Jury present to the Court one indictment against Gus. Bendjus, Robert Strangland, Ben Fredrickson, Peter Peterson, Frank Nelson, Fred Strongburger, John Blending, Andrew Johnson, Leander Brown, John Saxon, John Thompson, Alex Agren, Thomas Jacobs, E. Emel, Andrew Anderson and Charles Burke for the crime of Malicious Mischief which they say they find a true bill in which twelve of their number concur which indictment is received by the Court and by the clerk duly filed in open court in the presence of said Grand Jury

Frank Allyn Judge
