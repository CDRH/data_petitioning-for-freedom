







Territory of Washington}
King County} SS

I, H.G. Thornton to whom the within warrant is specially dirrected, upon oath do say
that I received the same for service on the 21st day of January, 1874 and served the
same on the 22d day of January 1874, by arresting and taking under my custody at Port
Madison, in Kitsap County, Washington Territory, Seo Kim the female person named in
said warrant while she was in the custody of and forcibly restrained of her liberty
by several Chinaman, to one unknown who was considering to abduct and carry the said
Seo Kim as I was informed and believed, and now  and of the Territory of Washington
against her will.
H.G. Thornton

Subscribed &amp;Sworn to before me this 28h day of Jany 1874
L.B. Andrews
Clerk
