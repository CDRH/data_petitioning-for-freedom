"By the Habeas Corpus Act"

The State of Missouri

To Sister M Julia Supr of Vincent Institution...... Greeting:

You are hereby commanded that the body of Caroline Bauer, under your custody detained as it it said, together with the time and cause of her detention, you have before the Honl RE Rombauer one of the Justices of our Circuit Court of the County of St. Louis at Room 1 of said Court at three o clock PM on Saturday 4th day of January AD 1868 to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning the said Caroline Bauer so detained as aforesaid and hereof fact such at your peril

Witness John Lewis Clerk of the Circuit Court of the County of St. Louis with the seal thereof hereto affixed at office in the City of St. Louis this 2nd day of January AD 1868

Jno Lewis Clerk
