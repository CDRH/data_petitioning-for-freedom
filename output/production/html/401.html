  • Summary of Proceedings: Effie Jackson, an unmarried woman from Arkansas, made a complaint of bastardy against Dinkens. He was arrested with a set bail of one thousand dollars. Through her account, Dinkens had relations with her in Arkansas and then left her pregnant. Jackson traveled to Omaha to have Dinkens answer for his actions. Davis, the judge, ordered that he was wrongfully imprisoned and Dinkens was released with no answer to the future of Jackson and her child.
