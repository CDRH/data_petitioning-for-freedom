IN THE UNITED STATE DISTRICT COURT FOR THE WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON, NORTHERN DIVISION.

In the Matter of the Application of MRS. GLADYS FREDRIKA FUSON, for a Writ of Habeas Corpus.}

NO. 3451 PETITION IN FORMA PAUPERIS

Your petitioner herein respecfully shows to the Court that she is a poor person, a native born citizen of the United States; that she is imprisoned in the Seattle, King County, Washington, having been arrested on a verbal order of the respondent by Seattle Police woman at the City of Port Townsend, October 20, 1916; that she has been advised by Counsel, and belives that her imprisonment is in violation of the guarantees of the Constitution of the United States; that she has no property whatever, except an allowance of $12.50 semi-monthly ordered by Judge Boyd Tallman of the Superior Court of King County in her husband's action for divorce; that she has no friends financially able to assist her; that her attorney herein, also knows her finacial condition and that in view thereof told her and her friends, that regardless of the event of this action, that he would not accept from her or from her friends any compensation for his services in this proceeding.

Petitioner further states, that the deposits heretofore made herein were made from allowances of alimony pendente lite above refered to.

WHEREFORE your petitioner prays that she be permitted to prosecute this action as a poor person, under the law and practice of this Court.

P.H. Wilson
Attorney for Petitioner

 

STATE OF WASHINGTON COUNTY OF KING} SS

Gladys Fredrika Fuson
