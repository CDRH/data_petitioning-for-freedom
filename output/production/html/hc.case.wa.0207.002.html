The United States District Court Western District of Washington Northern Division

In the Matter of the Application of Nora Sullivan for a Writ of Habeas Corpus:

4764

Order:

Verified petition, supported by affidavit, has been presented to the Court, charging that the petitioner is deprived and restrained of her liberty contrary to law by the Commissioner of Immigration, and praying that a writ of habeas corpus issue, and that she be produced before the Court and here detention considered and determined, and upon such hearing that she be discharged;

IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED that the Commissioner of Immigration appear before this Court at the court room in the Federal Building on Monday morning, August 18, 1919, at ten o'clock, and show cause why a writ of habeas corpus should not issue.

IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that a copy of this order be forthwith served upon the Commissioner of Immigration.

Dated this fifteenth day of August, 1919.

Jeremiah Neterer

Judge.
