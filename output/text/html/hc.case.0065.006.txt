







In the District Court for the Third Judicial District of Washington Territory holding
terms at Seattle in King County within and for the counties of King, Kitsap and Snohomish

Territory of Washington }
County of King } SS

The President of the United States of America to Lewis V Wyckoff Sheriff of King County
Greeting

We command you that you have the body of Peter Nelson W Smith H Elsner C Whaley G
Mattson Charls Lundblud Dennis Coffy and Jacob Annir by you imprisoned and detained
as it is said together with the time and cause of such imprisonment and detention
before the Hon J.R. Lewis Chief Justice of Washington Territory and Judge of the District
Court aforesaid at the Chambers of said Judge in the City of Seattle county and Territory
aforesaid on the 3d day of May AD 1875. at the hour of one OClock PM of said day to
do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning therein and have
you then 

and there this writ

Seal

Witness the Hon J.R. Lewis Chief Justice of Washington Territory and Judge of said
District Court this 1st day of May AD 1875
Attest my hand and the seal of said court affixed the day and date last above written
L.B. Andrews
Clerk
