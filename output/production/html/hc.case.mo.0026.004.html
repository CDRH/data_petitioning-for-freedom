In obedience to this writ I have here the bodies of the within named John & May Plackett, who were committed by my custody on the 19th Inst. by virtue of a warrant of commitment issued by P. Ferguson Esq a Justice of the Peace for the county of St Louis which said warrant and the desposition accompanying the same one herewith Exhibited and made a part of this my return

Jno. K. Walker Shff

St. Louis 21st Decr 1825

Habeas Corpus for John Plackett & May his wife

To the Sheriff
