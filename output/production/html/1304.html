  • Summary of Proceedings: Joseph Bowers was arrested by Sheriff William Cochrane for the charge of assault and battery, and he was subsequently sentenced by a justice of the peace to 500 days in jail. He filed for a petition of Habeas Corpus on the basis that the justice of the peace was not acting within his jurisdiction when he sentenced the petitioner. Upon hearing the evidence, the judge allowed the petition, but the fate of the petitioner remains unknown.
