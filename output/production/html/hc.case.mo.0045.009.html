Also at the same time and place came J.L. Engart of said City of Cincinnati who being first duly sworn deposeth and saith that he is acquianted with the black man referred to in the foregoing affidavits not said and has known him for the last 12 or 14 years. That said George Washington Kirk is about 22 or 23 years old. is about 5 feet 4 or 5 inches in heighth and rather stout built. Affiant does not remember any special marks by which he can more specifically describe him, Said George Washington Kirk has made his home in the State of Ohio at or near the City of Cincinnati ever since affiant has known him and greater part of the time has lived with his Father Casey Kirk, a black man. He has always been reputed to be free, and affiant has never heard any doubt expressed as to his righ to freedom. And further affiant saith not.

J.L. Engart
