In obedience to the within writ I have the body of Eleck Loan here in open court and for my return say that he was committed to my custody on the 25th day of October 1856 by virtue of a warrant issued by Justice Johnson on charge of being a runaway slave which warrant of commit to is here annexed and made a part of this return

this 1st day of November 1856

R F Logan

Jailor of St Louis County

No 102

October Term 1856

Habeas Corpus in the matter of Eleck Loan

"By the Habeas Corpus Act"

Issued Nov 1st 1856
