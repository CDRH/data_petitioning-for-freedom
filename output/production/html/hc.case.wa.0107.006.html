IN THE DISTRICT COURT of the Second Judicial District of Washington Territory, holding terms at Montesano, for the County of Chehalis.

Territory of Washington Plaintiff. Gus Bendjus &al Defendants} No. 135 JUDGMENT OF DISMISSAL.

And now, on this 29th day of May, A.D., 1888 comes the plaintiff, by his attorney George J. Moody and moves the Court that this cause be dismissed at his costs, and the Court being fully advised in the premises, grants said motion : whereupon it is considered by the Court that this cause be, and the same is hereby, dismissed, and that the defendant have and recover of and from the plaintiff, the costs of this action to be taxed, and that execution issue therefor. and that defendant bonds[?] and each of them be and they are hereby exhonorated

Frank Allyn

J

 

No 135

Territory of Wash vs. Gus Bendjus Etal

JUDGMENT OF DISMISSAL,

Filed May 29th 1888

HM Sutton CLERK.
