UNITED STATES OF AMERICA.

Territory of Washington,

Third Judicial District} SS

To J.H. Woolery, J.H. McGraw and James Welch and H.G. Thornton R.M. Hopkins and C.H. Hanford

In the name of the United States of America, You are hereby commanded to be and appear at the District Court for the Third Judicial District of Washington Territory, at Seattle, for the District aforesaid, at 10 o'clock in the forenoon of the 26th day of November A.D. 1887 then and there to give evidence in a certain cause therein pending, wherein In the matter of the application of Alice McKay for a writ of Habeas Corpus for Mary McKay Plaintiff and []Defendant and to remain in attendance on said Court from day to day thereafter until discharged, and herein fail not at your peril.

Witness on behalf

Witness, the HON. RICHARD A. JONES, Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of Washington Territory, and Judge of said District Court, this 25 day of Nov 1887

WE Lengerwood Clerk

By [] Deputy.
