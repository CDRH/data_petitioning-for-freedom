







In the matter of the application of Han Lee to behalf of Seo Kim for a writ of Habeas
Corpus &amp; warrant of arrest

Now comes the petitioner by D. Higgins his attorney and excepts to the sufficing of
the return to said writ made in this court by the said Ah Chow for the following answers:

1st Because he shows no authority or course for the restraint of the party in custody,
which was examined by him as alleged in petitioners application on file in this court

2d Because he does not state that he has not had the said Seo Kim in his custody and
under his control and that he had not at any time or place transfered the custody
of her to any one else, together with the cause of such transfer

D. Higgins
Petitioner Atty
