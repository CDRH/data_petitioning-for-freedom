







State of Missouri County of St Louis

I Frederick E Baumgarten a phisician of the city of St Louis do hereby certify that
I have this day seen and examined Caroline Bauer wife of John Bauer of the city and
county of St Louis and believe her to suffer of monomania and a proper patient to
be sent to the state lunatic asylum

dated St. Louis December 16 1867
F.E. Baumgarten
