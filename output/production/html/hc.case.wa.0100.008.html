1236

In Justice Court Pierce County Washington Territory, before W. H. Harris, Justice of the Peace.

Territory of Washington vs Ah Sock} 8

To W. H. Harris Esqr:

Justice of the Peace in and for Pierce County.

You will please take notice that the above named defendant Ah Sock, hereby appeals from the Judgment and decision rendered by you in that certain cause wherein the Territory of Washington is plaintiff and Ah Sock is defendant rendered by you on the 18th of August 1884 to the District Court.

Yours Respectfully

John P. Judson

Attorney for Ah Sock.
