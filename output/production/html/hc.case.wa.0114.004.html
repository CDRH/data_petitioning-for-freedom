Inthe United States commissioners Court before Eben Smith, United States commissioner in and for the third Judicial District of Washington Territory at Seattle, in King County.

Territory of Washington}
Third Judical District} SS
King County,}

Whereas William Jamison, Richard Daley (whose real and true name is Peter Daley) and William Winters, have been charged on oath before me, the undersigned United States commissioner in and for the 3rd Judicial District of Washington Territory at Seattle in King County, with the crime of conspiring to defraud the U.S. out of certain of its Public lands as in complaint of William F. Prosser special agent of the General Land Office of the United States, setforth, contrary to the Statutes in such cases made and provided and whereas William Jones, William H. Doyle, Peter Anderson, Fred Williams and Alfred Woods, have been duly subpoenaed as witnesses in the matter of said charge   -2nd- on behalf of the U.S. and whereas to the matter upon examination had this day before me, said named witnesses then and there being present, not having offered sufficient bail as required by me, the said commissioner in the sum of Five hundred each for their and each of their appearance at the next regular term of the District Court of the Third Judicial District of Washington Territory holding terms at Seattle and for the counties of King and Kitsap to begun and holden on the first monday of April next, then and there to testify as witnesses for and on behalf of the U.S. in the matter of the charge aforesaid: were by me then and there ordered to be committed to the custody of the U. S. Marshall in and for Washington Territory for want of surities and until they should be discharged by due course of law. You are therefore hereby commanded in the name and under the authority of the United States of America to receive the said William Jones, William H. Doyle, Peter Anderson, Fred Williams and Alfred Woods, and them safely keep until they shall be delivered by due process of law Given under my hand and seal this 11th day of Feby A. D. 1885.

(signed) Eben Smith. seal.

United States Commissioner as aforesaid.
