  • Summary of Proceedings: Louis W. R. Beaty was arrested March 17, 1910, for mixing garbage with refuse material in the City of Omaha. On March 17, Beaty and attorney, M. O. Cunningham, filed a petition of habeas against Police Force Captain, Henry W. Dunn, and Patrick Mostyn. Beaty argued that no statute exists in Omaha or Nebraska regarding the charge. Judge Lee. S. Estelle denied the writ and gave Beaty 40 days to prepare a bill of exceptions on March 22. Beaty made an appeal and motioned for new trial on March 23. Judge Estelle overruled a new trial, 40 days to prepare appeal was maintained.
