In the Probate Court of Whitman County Washington Territory before WN Ruby Probate Judge

In the the matter of Amos Gustin and Catherine Gustin adopting Daisy Geneva Departte minor child of James G. Departte and Elizabeth Departte

I SJ Chadwick guardian ad latern of Daisy Gevena Departte having personaly examined Amos Gustin and Catherine Gustin under oath and being satisfied that said minor child Daisy Geneva Departte has been abandoned by her parents and that the above named Amos Gustin and Catherine Gustin are suitable persons to adopt said child and have the property and ability to bring up and educate her I hereby give my consent for them to adopt her

S. J Chadwick
