IN THE UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT FOR THE WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON, NORTHERN DIVISION

IN THE MATTER OF THE APPLICATION OF GENEVIEVE WILDS FOR A WRIT OF HABEAS CORPUS}
3895
ORDER

Upon reading the petition of Genevieve Wilds on file herein and the court being fully advised in the premises, now therefore,

IT IS HERBY ORDERED that J.S. McBride, Health Officer of the City of Seattle be and he is hereby directed and ordered to appear in this court on the 18th day of Febuary, 1918, at the hour of 10 2 O'Clock A. P.M., and then and there show cause, if any there be, why a writ of habeas corpus should not be issued herein as prayed for in said petition

Done in open court this 16th day of Febuary, 1918.

Jeremiah Neterer,
Judge.
