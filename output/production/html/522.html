  • Summary of Proceedings: Ardula Porter petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus to regain custody of her daughter, thirteen-year-old Clara Barnes, from her brother-in-law, J. C. Barnes, which was granted. Ardula and Joseph Barnes had gotten divorced without establishing custody of their daughter, Clara, who had been living with her father and his family when he died. According to Ardula, Joseph's brother, J. C., was refusing to allow Ardula to take custody of her daughter. The petition was dismissed during the hearing "without costs to any of the parties, the said child having been surrendered to the petitioner."
