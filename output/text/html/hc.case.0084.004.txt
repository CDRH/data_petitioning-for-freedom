United States of America}
Third Judicial District of Washington Terry} ss

To the Marshal of third Dist of Washington Terry:

Wherein on behalf of the United States has been made complaint in oath to me the undersigned
a Commissioner appointed by the Supreme Court of the Territory of Washington for the
Third Judicial Dist of said Territory to take acknowledgment of bail and affidavits
and also to take deposition of Witnesses on said case, charging the  did on or about
the 20" day of Feby AD 1888 at Port Townsend Jefferson County and Territory of Washington
did unlawfully enter the Territory of the United States, she being then and there
a citizen of the Empire of China and prohibited from entering the Territory of the
United States by Act of Congrees known as the Chinese Restriction Act, and being against
the peace and dignity of the United States and contrary to the Statute in such case
made and provided.

Now therefore you are hereby commanded in the name of the United States of America
to apprehend the said Nan Oy and bring her body forthwith before me or before some
other proper officer authorized by law to arrest imprison and bail for for some such
offence against the United States, wherever found in your District that she may then
and there be dealt with according to law for said offense and have you then and there
this writ

Given under my hand and seal this 20" day of February A.D. 1888
Oliver Wood
U.S. Commissioner
