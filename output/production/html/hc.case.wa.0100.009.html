1236

In Justices Court, Pierce County, Washington Territory before W. H. Harris, Justice of the Peace, in and for Pierce County Washington Territory.

Territory of Washington vs Sam

To W. H. Harris Esqr. Justice of the Peace in and for Pierce County Washington Territory.

You will please take notice that the above named defendant Sam hereby appeals to the District Court from the Judgment and decision rendered by you against him on the 18th day of August 1884 in the above entitled cause.

John P. Judson

Atty for the Defendant Sam
