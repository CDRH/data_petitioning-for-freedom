Case No 1 Poligamy Judgment

And now the said Sarah Elizabeth Seibert is brought to the bar of the Court here, and being asked if she have any legal Cause to show why judgment should not be produced[?]against her. Says[?] nothing unless as she before has said. Whereupon all and singular the premises being seen, and by the Judge of the Court here fully understood. It is considered by the Court that the said Sarah Elizabeth Seibert   be imprisoned in the penitentiary at hard labor for the term of Eighteen months and that she pay the costs of this action. And it appearing to the Court that there is no penitentiary within the Territory or other provided for the reception of prisoner It is Ordered that the said Sarah Elizabeth Seibert be confined in the County Jail of Thurston County till discharged or removed according to law.
