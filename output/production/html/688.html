  • Summary of Proceedings: D. J. Needham’s petition stated that he was being unlawfully held by Omaha Police Chief Henry W. Dunn. He stated that he was being held upon a warrant of the Chief of Police of Milwaukee, Wisconsin upon the charge of wife and child abandonment. He was asking for a writ to be issued because he claimed that he was going to be removed from the Omaha jurisdiction and taken back to Wisconsin without extradition papers. On 13 November 1915, Judge James P. English ordered that the writ be allowed and a writ be issued to Chief Dunn. In the Writ Return filed on 22 November 1915, Sheriff McShane stated that he was unable to deliver the writ because Needham had already left the state.
