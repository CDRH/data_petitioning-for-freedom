  • Summary of Proceedings: Charles H. Gaydou filed a writ of Habeas Corpus on behalf of his son, Charles Gaydou, to receive full custody of said child from Fannie Gaydou and John Marketan. Fannie Gaydou was the mother of Charles Gaydou and the former wife of Charles H. Gaydou. Charles and Fannie Gaydou had annulled their marriage at the time of this case, and Charles Gaydou alleged that Fannie Gaydou had committed adultery against him with John Marketan. Judge Joseph B. Clarkson found Fannie Gaydou to be an unfit person to have the care, custody, education, and maintenance of the said Charles Gaydou and Charles H. Gaydou as a fit guardian for the infant. Accordingly, the final order stated that Charles H. Gaydou was to be given full custody, and Fannie Gaydou was entitled to visit the infant once a week.
