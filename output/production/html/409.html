  • Summary of Proceedings: Fayette I. Foss, attorney for David Zimmerman's company, petitioned on behalf of Zimmerman claiming that the sheriff of Furnas County, Nebraska had deprived him of his liberty. Zimmerman had been arrested on a bench warrant issued by district judge D. T. Welty. Judge Hall granted the petition. After hearing evidence, he ruled that Zimmerman be released on his own recognizance after surrendering a sum of five hundred dollars, which Zimmerman did.
