To the Hon J.R. Lewis Judge within named — respectfully return in obedience to the requirements of this writ that that that I hold here within named Peter Nelson W Smith H Elsner C Whaley G Mattson Charles Lundblad Dennnis Coffy and Jacob Annir in my custody as Deputy Marshal of the United States for the Territory of Washington by virtue of a committment issued to me by D.S. Smith as commissioner for said District and Territory a copy of which said commandment showing his cause of said restraint is hereby annexed marked exhibit "A" and made a part of this return and I also here have the leader of said prisoners before Your Honor

E S Kearney
US Marshal W.T.

By L.V. Wyckoff Depty
