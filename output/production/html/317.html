  • Summary of Proceedings: On December 14, 1921, David Patterson pled guilty to two charges in the Multnomah County Circuit Court. He was sentenced to no more than three years in the state penitentiary but was paroled the same day. On December 15, Patterson was arrested on an outstanding warrant from a Marion County justice of the peace for the same crime to which he had already been convicted. On December 16, he petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, Patterson was released from custody.
