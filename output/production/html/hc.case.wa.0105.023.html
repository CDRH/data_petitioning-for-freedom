906

Territory of Washington}

County of Pierce} SS

To the Sheriff of Pierce Co. Greeting

An inquisition having this day been found by a Coroner's Jury before me stating that one Indian named Scameoch alias John has come to his death by criminal means by the act of a man named James Riley.

You are therefore commanded forthwith to arrest the above named James Riley and take him before the nearest or most accessable Magistrate in this County.

Given under my hand in Steilacoom Pierce Co. WT this 23d day of Augst AD 1861

Daniel Collins County Coroner
