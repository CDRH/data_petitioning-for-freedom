United States of America

Territory of Washington}

3rd Judicial District} SS.

To the Sheriff of Thurston Pierce County or any lawful officer of said District. Greeting:

In the name of the United States of America you are hereby commanded as you have heretofore been commanded to take the body of Wm Corine[?] and him safely keep, so that you have him before the United States District Court for the 3rd Judicial District of Washington Territory to be begun and holden at Olympia in the district aforesaid, on the 2nd Monday Monday of September next ensuing, it being the 8th day of said month 1862, then and there to answer to the Territory of Washington in a charge of Burglary.

Herein fail not and have you then and there this writ.

Witness the Hon. C C Hewitt Chief Justice of the W.T. and Judge second Jud. Dist. this 11th day of April 1862

Andrew Moses Clerk &C
