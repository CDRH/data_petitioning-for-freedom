St. Louis Law Commissioners' Court

August Term 1853, No. 98

Riley, Adam, In the matter of Habeas Corpus

Defendant was a free man of color; born in Indianapolis, IN; no member of his family was ever held in slavery; arrested as a fugitive slave; worked as a fireman on steamboats running between Cincinnati and St. Louis for 3-4 years; demanded to see copy of commitment and evidence against him but was refused
