  • Summary of Proceedings: Ng Key Tung, a 15-year old Chinese immigrant minor, petitioned for a Writ of Habeas Corpus. The petition states Tung had a right to admittance due to his father's status as a merchant. The petition was granted and a writ was issued. However, the immigration hearing was not found to be biased or unjust, and the writ was denied. Ng Key Tung remained in custody to be deported.
