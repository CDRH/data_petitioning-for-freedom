  • Summary of Proceedings: Benjamin Wise was arrested on suspicion of having committed arson, arraigned before the Justice Court of Salem, and jailed to await a grand jury trial. Bail was set at $2,000. Wise paid his bail and was released only to be promptly re-arrested and jailed to answer a second charge related to the arson. Bail was set at $3,000. Wise petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. He contended that the bail was excessive. After hearing the evidence, Wise was remanded to the custody of his jailer.
