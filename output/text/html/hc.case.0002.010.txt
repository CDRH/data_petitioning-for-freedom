







Andrew H Derrick
vs
Mrs M J Whitesides

Habeas Corpus At Chambers Jany 2 1867

And now Mrs. MJ Whitesides 
this day in persuance of the Writ 
appears at this Court produces the 
person of Louisa Jane Derrick 
into Court and after a free
hearing of the Court and  
write the facts and circumstances 
of the case it satisfactorily offering 
that to said Mrs. M J Whitesides 
is exercising no illegal or 
improper restraint over the said 
Louisa Jane Derrick the said 
Mrs. M J Whitesides is discharged 
from the Writ and it further 
appearing to the Court that the 
said Louisa Jane Derrick 
is a minor being about fifteen 
years of age and hence is subject 
to the custody care and control
of her Father the Plaintiff
Andrew H Derrick and after 
a free investigation it appears 
that her said Father is a suitable 
person to have the care custody 
and control of his said daughter 



Louisa Jane Derrick it is accordingly nowhere considered and 
ordered and decreed.

That a A Sidel Sheriff of 
Walla Walla County deliver the 
said Father his daughter Louisa 
Jane Derrick to the end that 
he may exercise that care 
and control over her habits associations and education that he 
has a right to exercise by
lest the laws of nature and 
the laws of the land

And it is further order
that the said Ms. M J 
Whitesides by agents servants 
and  be restraint from 
interfering in any maner
With the said Louisa Jane
Derrick or the right of her 
said Father  care 
and custody.

And it is further ordered 
that the Defendant by the 
Court she has made in this
hearing and the Plaintiff 
by all other Courts to be
taxed by the clerk
Wyche 
Judge
