  • Summary of Proceedings: Emma Rupert petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus claiming that her son Clarence Rupert was being deprived of his liberty by her ex-husband Henry Rupert. She claimed that Henry had abducted Clarence while she was living in Oklahoma. Judge Holmes granted the petition. The next day, Holmes allowed the writ and ordered that Clarence be released into the custody of the petitioner. However, the couple apparently made up over time, as they were living together with Clarence and 3 new younger siblings in Lincoln at the 1910 census.
