  • Summary of Proceedings: Nelson, Smith, Elsner, Whaley, Mattson, Lundblad, Coffey, and Annir were hired by L. E. Gray as mariners aboard the Bark Gem of the Ocean, going from San Francisco, California, to Seattle, Washington, carrying cargo. As Gray did not sign their shipping contracts or agreements in writing or print, Nelson and others demanded that they were entitled to be discharged and paid off in wages of $37.50 each. Gray refused, and Nelson and others were arrested by Sheriff Lewis Wycoff for disobedience. Nelson and others applied for habeas, claiming said imprisonment to be illegal, that there was no proper charge, they weren't examined or held bail, and there wasn't any definite offense. Petition was granted.
