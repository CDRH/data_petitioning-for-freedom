"By the Habeas Corpus Act"

The State of Missouri

To George Hammand Esq- Greeting

You are hereby commanded that the body of Matilda a person of color who has been permitted to sue for her freedom in your custody as is said, detained, under safe and secure conduct, together the day and cause of her capter & detainer by whatsoever name she may be called, you have before the Circuit Court for St Louis County on the 30th day of May 1832 at the Court house ________ in the county of Maries then & there to do submit to and recieve whatsoever shall be considered on that behalf

Witness William C Carr Judge of the 3d Judicial Circuit of the State of Misouri this 23 May 1832

Will. C. Carr
