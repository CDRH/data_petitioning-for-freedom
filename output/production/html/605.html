  • Summary of Proceedings: Link Rothwell claimed to have been unlawfully deprived of his liberty by Frank Krebs under the bases of Ordinance No. 384. This ordinance prohibited interference with buildings, standpipes, hydrants, properties and employees of Union Stock Yards Company in the city of South Omaha. Krebs detained Rothwell, after claiming he was on Union Stock Yard Company property and refused to leave. It was found that he was in fact unlawfully detained and he was released from jail.
