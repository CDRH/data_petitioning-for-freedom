  • Summary of Proceedings: Lee Suey was arrested by an order issued by Immigration Commissioner Henry M. White on the accusation that he was an illegal immigrant from China. Lee Suey petitioned claiming to be a native U.S. citizen born in Oregon, with a father named Lee Long Yuen who is a Chinese merchant. He followed claiming improper trial, illegal conduct, and no due process of law. Writ was dismissed at the behest of the petitioner.
