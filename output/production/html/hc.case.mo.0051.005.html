In obedience to the within writ I have the Body of James A Parker hear at open court and for my return say that he was committed to my custody on the 11 day of September 1858 by virtue of a warent issued by Justice Johnstone on charge of being a runaway slave wich[?] & warent of comittell is hear arrest[?] and maid a Part of this return.

This the 30 day of September 1858.

Chas Rodermann

Jailer

In the matter of James A Parker

"By the Habeas Corpus Act."

Prisoner discharged

Septr 30th 1858

Septr 29. Writ issd Septr 30th 1858 Prisoner dischd.
