Casey Kirk being first duly sworn on his oath states that he is a free man, and is the Father of George Washington Kirk who is now in prison in the City of St. Louis in the State of Missouri. Said George Washington Kirk is a black man is about 22 years of age is rather below medium heighth and is heavy built. has a mark on his left hand caused by a burn which has drawn his fingers crooked (towards the palm of his hand) so that he can not straighten his little finger and the one next to it. He is also lame in the left foot and from some cause unknown to affiant his left ankle is considerably enlarged - and his left foot seems to have shrunk so that it is smaller than his right foot. Said George Washington Kirk is a free Boy, having been manumitted by Major Daniel Gans of the City of Cincinnati on the 12th of February A.D. 1840 by virtue of a power of attorney from Exum Lewis of Hinds County in the State of Mississippi authorizing said Gans to manumit said this affiant and the said George and others members of affiants family. which said power of attorney said deed of manumission are duly recorded in said City of Cincinnati in the County of Hamilton Said George Washington Kirk is the same person referred to in the affidavits of D James Richardson, Henry F. Lector, R.F. Engart and J.L. Engart, taken before Saul S Carpenter of this State and further affiants saith not

Casey Kirk his + mark

Sworn to and subscribed before me this 7th day of August A.D. 1856

Jon Mitchell

Notary Public
