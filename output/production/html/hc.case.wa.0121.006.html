In Justice's Court,
Before J.C. Dorr,
Justice of the Peace.

An order having been this day made by the undersigned one of the Justices of the Peace in and for Columbia County, that Jacob Schuman be held to answer before the next grand jury for the District Court of Columbia County, holding terms at Dayton, upon a charge of selling lager beer in violation of the "Local Option Law", on July 27th in the Precinct of Dayton;

Now therefore, these are to command you the sheriff of said County, in the name of the United States to take the said Jacob Schuman into your custody and him there safely keep until he is legally discharged. And I further order that he be admitted to bail in the sum of five hundred dollars with sufficient surety.

Given under my hand this 11th day of August A.D. 1886

J.C. Dorr
Justice of the Peace
