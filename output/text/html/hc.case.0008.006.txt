







Joseph Wells vs Church et al } In District Court First Judicial District

Territory of Washington County of Walla Walla } ss

William A Church being first duly sworn says that he is one of the defendants above
that Martha Church the other of said defendants is the wife of affiant that said defendant
Martha Church  is now confined to her bed that in December 2 1877 said Martha was
taken down in child birth  and that the said Matty Wells is attending to her mother
and affiant shows that said Matty Wells will be in attendance upon the court on the
4th day of December 1877 at the hour of 3 o'clock PM

x William A Church
Subscribed &amp; sworn to before me this December 3 1877


Clerk
