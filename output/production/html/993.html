  • Summary of Proceedings: On November 7th, 1921, John C. Mischnick submitted a petition on behalf of his four year old son, John Robert. John C. claimed that John Robert was being unlawfully detained by Fred and Minnie Nissen. He explained that around August 1st, 1921, Fred and Minnie visited his residence and requested that John C. let John Robert visit their home, agreeing to bring John Robert back whenever John C. desired. John C. then explained that he went to their house on October 28th to retrieve John Robert but Fred and Minnie refused to release John Robert. The petition was granted and on November 22nd the writ was allowed. The judge found that John Robert was being unlawfully detained and that he should be returned to his father.
