In obedience to the command of the foregoing writ I have here in open Court the Body of Mary Willson and for my return say that she was committed to my custody on the 20th day of April as a runaway Slave on writ of commitment issued by Louis Du Brueil a Justice of the within and for the County of St Louis which writ is hereto anext and made apart of this return this 21st day of April 1854

James Castello

Jailor St Louis Co

No 92

April Term 1854

Habeas Corpus in the Matter of Mary Wilson

"By the Habeas Corpus act"

Carroll atty

Issued April 21st 1854

Jno W. Colvin
