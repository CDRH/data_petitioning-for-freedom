In the District Court of the Second Judicial District Washington Territory, holding terms at Montesano for Chehalis County.

Ex Parte Lorica Hamilton} Ex parte Lorica Hume} In[?] Writs of Habeas Corpus.

And now October 23d 1888 It is hereby stipulated by and between the parties that the Hearing of the Writ of Habeas Corpus, upon the two returns 2 Lorica Hamilton and Lorica Hume, be and one same[?], if both parties are present be continued until the Term of the Court at Vancouver November Term next ensuing, and if the hearing be not then had it may be heard as by the Judge of King[?] County [?] the Chief[?] of the Supreme Court, at Olympia by January 1889.

Elwood Evans of Counsel for Petitioners.

George J. Moody Atty for respondents.

Clerk please file

 

FILED OCT 26 1888

HM Sutton CLERK.

DEPUTY.
