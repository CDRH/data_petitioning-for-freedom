Upon the oral motion of Clay Allen, United States Attorney for the Western District of Washington, attorney for the respondent in the above entitled cause;

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the stay of twenty days granted and directed in an order entered in this cause on the 20th day of July, 1914, be, and the same hereby is extended for an additional period of fourteen days, or to and inclusive of August 21, 1914, and the respondent herein is given to the date last mentioned in which to enter an appeal and the conditions of the petitioner to remain in force until such time and if appeal is prosecuted bond to be given as heretofore stated

Dated this 5th day of August, 1914.

Jeremiah Neterer
