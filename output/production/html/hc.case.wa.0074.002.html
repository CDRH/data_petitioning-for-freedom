UNITED STATES OF AMERICA.

Territory of Washington, County of King, City of Seattle, } ss

To the Marshal or any Police Officer of the City of Seattle, and the Keeper of the Jail of said City of Seattle:

WHEREAS, at a Justice's Court held at my office, in said County, for the trial of Lee Kee for the offense hereinafterstated, the said LEE KEE was convicted of having, on the 21st day of May 1885 in said County and City committed the offense of keeping a place of resort for smoking or inhaling opium and upon conviction, the said court did adjudge and determine that said Lee Kee should pay a fine of fifty Dollars and twenty 20 70/100 Dollars costs, and stand committed until the same be paid; and, whereas, the said Lee Kee has made default in the payment of the same. Therefore, you, the said Marshal or Police Officer, and said Keeper are hereby commanded in name of the United Stated forthwith to convey and deliver the said Lee Kee to the said keeper; and you, the said keeper, are hereby commanded to receive the said Lee Kee into your custody in said jail, and him there keep at hard labor for the space of 30 days, unless said fine and costs be sooner paid, or he shall thence be discharged by due course of law.

Dated this 22 day of May 1885

George G. Lyon SEAL. Justice of the Peace.
