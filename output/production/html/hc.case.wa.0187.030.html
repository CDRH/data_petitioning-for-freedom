UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT, WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON, NORTHERN DIVISION

I the Matter of the Application of MARIE ROBINA GRAYSON, alias Marie Robina, for a Writ of Habeas Corpus.

No. 2780 BILL OF EXCEPTIONS

Irons

This cause coming on for hearing on the 20th day of August, 1914, the time for the filing and settlement of the bill of exceptions having been extended until the 21st day of August, 1914, I, Jeremiah Neterer, Judge of the United States District Court for the Western District of Washington, now on this 20th day of August, 1914, and within the term of this court during which the trial of the above entitled cause was held and within the time to which the settling of the bill of exceptions was extended DO HEREBY CERTIFY that this cause was tried before me without a jury; and I further CERTIFY that on this day came on for settlement and certification the proposed bill of exceptions of the respondent, counsel appearing for both parties, and agreeing that said proposed bill of exceptions and the exhibits therein set forth or referred to or attached be settled and certified as a true and correct bill of exceptions in said cause;

And I further CERTIFY that having duly settled, and hereby settling and allowing, the foregoing bill of exceptions in the above entitled action, do hereby certify the same and do hereby certify that this bill of exceptions, together with the exhibits marked "Respondent's Exhibits -1-   One and Two", and the exhibits made a part of the records of the hearings, set forth in respondent's exhibits one and two, therein set forth, or referred to, and hereto attached, contain all the evidence, exhibits and other material facts, matters and proceedings in said cause not already a apart of the record herein.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF the undersigned has hereunto set his hand with his title of office at Seattle in the Western District of Washington, Northern Division, United States of America, this 20th day of August, A. D. 1914

Jeremiah Neterer
District Judge of the Untedt District of Washington.

August 20-1914
We hereby consent to the entry of the above order & the order allowing the appeal.
Noah Shakespeare
Louis A. Merrick
Attorneys for Petitioner
