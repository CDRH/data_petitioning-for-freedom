  • Summary of Proceedings: Ralph O. Urban was charged with the crime of forgery by the C. F. Adams Company. It was argued that the company charged him in bad faith and for the sole purpose of private gain. William Crooker also argued that he held an executive warrant for the arrest of Urban and that Urban was a fugitive from justice in the state of Colorado. Both charges were deemed unlawful and after the case finished in the Supreme Court of Nebraska, Urban was released.
