  • Summary of Proceedings: Special Constable W. H. Harris said Hutchinson was arrested at Port Blakely and committed to his custody by virtue of an arrest warrant. The warrant by Justice Campbell stated that Hutchinson embezzled money ($240) from W. H. Harris. Hutchinson's attorneys filed a petition and Judge Greene granted it and ordered Hutchinson to be released. After this, an execution was issued in King County to take the defendant's property (2 small horses, 1-4 spring wagon and 1 set of double harness) to be sold, however Mrs. Lena Hutchinson claimed to own the property so Sheriff McGraw could not take it.
