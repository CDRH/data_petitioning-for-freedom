







Office of the Sheriff of King County W.T.} SS.

I William Cochrane Sheriff of King County W.T. do hereby certify that on the 23d day
of May A.D. 1887 I received the writ of Habeas corpus a true copy of which is hereto
annexed; and personally served the same on the same day on Ella Cline, she being the
person named in said writ, by delivering to and leaving with her personally in Seattle
King County W.T. said writ of Habeas Corpus.

Dated at Seattle King Co W.T. this 24th day of May 1887
William Cochrane
Sheriff

By M. Gadden
Dep.
