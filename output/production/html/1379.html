  • Summary of Proceedings: Rhoda McNamira alleged that he was unlawfully restrained of his liberty by Martin White and that no complaint had been brought against him, nor had he received a trial upon his charge. White charged McNamira with grand larceny and said the complaint was filed and a bond was issued for $600 and never paid and thus explains why McNamira is imprisoned. Judge Slabaugh decided that McNamira was unlawfully held. However the fate of the bound party is unknown.
