In the District Court at Montesano, September Term A.D. 1888

Comes now William Hume Locrea J Hume in answer to the annexed writ of Habeas Corpus and makes anser[?] of the same as follows

That she holds in her custody the said Hilma Lundvall and has so held her since the fifteenth day of July A.D. 1887 under and by orders of edicts[?] of adoption between said Hume and the Orphan Home of the Presbyterian Church located at Portland in the State of Oregon and that he restrains said Hilma Ludvall of her liberty in no other manner

Leuvca J. Hume

 

FILED SEP 19 1888

HM Sutton CLERK.

By [] DEPUTY.
