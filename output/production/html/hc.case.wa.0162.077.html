Territory of Washington
County of Spokane} ss

In the District Court, Fourth Judicial District holding terms at Spokane Falls.

The Territory of Washington
vs.
Ray Raymond

I, W.S. Murphy being duly sworn on my oath depose and say that I am now and for the past five years have been a resident of the precinct of Rockford in Spokane County Washington Territory. That I have had casual knowledge that there was such a case as that above named now since the homicide out of which it grew. That I am well acquainted with the people residing in said Rockford precinct, and have personal acquaintance with the greater share of them.

That I am engaged   in the mercantile business in said precinct and at the town of Rockford and have opportunity to hear the opinions and sentiments of the people of said precinct expressed and in a general way known the same. That I have not heard any expressions of hostility or of prejudice against the said defendant Ray Raymond by the residents of said precinct. That if there was prejudice and ill will [?] against her there I should have been most aware to have heard it. That from my best information the alleged facts relating to the homicide out of which said action grew, all not generally known at said precinct known to the people of said precinct and that they generally have not formed an opinion as to the guilt or innocence of said defendant, and that the people of said precinct   are not prejudiced against said Ray Raymond in said action.

Applicant further saith that there are about eight hundred people living in said Rockford precinct and that affiant verily believes that a large number of good unprejudiced and legally qualified jurors can be summoned and secured in said precinct to serve as jurors in said action. And further applicant saith not.

W.S. Murphy

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 10th day of December A.D. 1886

S.C. Hyde
Notary Public
