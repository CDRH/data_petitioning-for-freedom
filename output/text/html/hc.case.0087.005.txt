







Territory of Washington
County of King} SS

In the District Court holding terms at Seattle

In the matter of the application of Wong Sam Foke Gee Ah Moke Chin Sam Ah Zee and
Wong Hing for writ of Habeas Corpus } No 5911

Now on this 13th day of April A.D. 1888 this cause comes on to be heard on the Demurrer
of Petitioners to the return of E.V. Rugar to the writ of Habeas Corpus on file herein
Petitioners being present in person and by their attorney Richard Osborne Esq and
Respondent present by Chas F. Manday Esq assistant US Attorney after hearing the argument
of respective counsel and being fully advised in the premises the Court sustains said
Demurrers and orders that Petitioners be discharged from custody

RA Jones
Judge
