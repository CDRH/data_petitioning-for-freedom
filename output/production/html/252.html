  • Summary of Proceedings: Lemuel Dobbs petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus claiming that his daughter, Ura Dobbs, was deprived of her liberty by Floyd Raney, T. N. Brittenham, and Levi Grouch. The men were her deceased mother's relatives, who the girl had gone to visit on December 22, 1918, and had not returned. Judge Holmes granted the petition. According to the appearance docket, Sheriff Ress was unable to locate any of the respondents. The petition outcome and fate of the bound party are unknown.
