  • Summary of Proceedings: On June 4, 1918, Enola Crater was sentenced to 60 days in Jail on the charge of drunkenness in the Omaha Police Court. On July 5, Crater filed for habeas against Doulgas County sheriff, charging that the police judge had exceeded jurisdictional authority and that this charge of such nature for Crater. Crater's petition was granted, a writ issued, and sustained on July 6; Crater was discharged from custody.
