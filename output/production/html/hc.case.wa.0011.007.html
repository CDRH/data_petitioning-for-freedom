Territory of Washington
County of Walla Walla }

In Justice Court before
OP Lacy Justice of the Peace

Territory of Washington
vs
Adaline Oneil Deft }

Charged with being an Habitual Drunkard and a disorderly person

Now this cause coming in to be heard this 25th day of July 1876 J B Brooks[?] J G Justice John Lucas and S McCally[?] were sworn on the law of the territory and the charge being fully sustained by the evidence the said Defendant was duly convicted as a vagrant and was required to give bonds in the sum of one hundred dollars for her good bevahior for three months and in default of bonds was committed to Jail for the term of three months or until discharged by due course of law

O P Lacy

Justice of the Peace
