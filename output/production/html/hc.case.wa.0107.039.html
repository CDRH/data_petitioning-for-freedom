United States of America

Territory of Washington, Second Judicial District.}

To A T. Harris, Fred Colbert, and Wm Church In the Name of the United States of America, you are hereby required to appear at the Court House in Montesano, on the 15th day of May, 1888, at 9 o'clock in the forenoon to give evidence in a certain cause, wherein Territory of Washington Plantiff, and Gus Bendjus Et al Defendant, on the part of Plantiff

Witness Frank Allyn, Esquire, Judge of the District Court of the Secon Judicial District of Washington Territory, this 28th day of April, A.D. 1888.

HM Sutton
Clerk.

 

Territory of Wash. vs Gus Bendjus Et al. Subpoena. Returned and filed this 7th day of May A.D. 1888. HM Sutton, clerk.

Viddette Print, Montsano, W. T.

Territory of Washington, County of Pacific} I hereby certify that the within subpoena came into my hands on the 3d day of May A.D. 1888, and I served the same on the within named witnesses, to-wit: A.T. Harris, at Ft. Canby, Pacific in W.T. on the 4th day of May 1888. and, Fred Colbert, at Ilwaco in said county on the same day, (was returned, not in county)

by exhibiting and reading the same to each of them. J.H. Turner, Sheriff of Pacific County
