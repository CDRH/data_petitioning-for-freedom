No.137. Territory of Wash vs. Gus Bendjus.
Judgement of Dismissal.
Filed May 29th 1888
HM Sutton
Clerk.
Entered on page 500

 

In the District Court of the Second Judicial District of Washington Territory, holding terms at Montesano, for the County of Chehalis.

Territory of Washington. Plantiff. Gus Bendjus et al Defendants. No. 137 Judgment of Dismissal.

And now, on this 29th day of May, A.D. 1888 comes the plaintiff, by his attorney, George J. Moody, and moves the Court that this cause be dismissed at his costs, and the Court being fully advised in the premises, grants said motion; whereupon it is considered by the Court that this cause be, and the same is hereby, dismissed, and that the defendant have and recover of and from the plaintiff, the costs of this action to be taxed, and that execution issue therefor, and that the bonds of said defendants and of each of them be and are hereby exonerated.

Frank Allyn
