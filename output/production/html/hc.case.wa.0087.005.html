In the District Court holding terms at Seattle

In the matter of the application of Wong Sam Foke Gee Ah Moke Chin Sam Ah Zee and Wong Hing for writ of Habeas Corpus } No 5911

Now on this 13th day of April A.D. 1888 this cause comes on to be heard on the Demurrer of Petitioners to the return of E.V. Rugar to the writ of Habeas Corpus on file herein Petitioners being present in person and by their attorney Richard Osborne Esq and Respondent present by Chas F.
