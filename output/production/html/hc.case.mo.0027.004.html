The State of Missouri

To Mary Ann Scheiber, and Henry Steinberg

You are hereby commanded to bring before the Judge of the St Louis Circuit Court at the Circuit Court Room on the 13th May Inst at 3 o'clock the body of a child named Edward Scheiber, who is illegally detained by you as it is said, and herein jail not under the penalties that may fall thereon.

Witness Stephen Rice Clerk of our said Court with the seal thereof, here to affixed at City of St Louis this May 12th A. D. 1862

Stephen Rice

Clerk
