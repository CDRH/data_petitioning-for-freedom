  • Summary of Proceedings: Joseph Augé petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus to retrieve his stepson, Amadee Quevereux, from Joseph Poupenez, which was granted. Augé accused Poupenez of illegally indenturing Quevereux; Poupenez countered that he held Quevereux by virtue of an indenture of apprenticeship agreed to by Quevereux's mother. After hearing the evidence, Quevereux was released.
