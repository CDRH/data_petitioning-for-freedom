  • Summary of Proceedings: On May 15, 1922, Louis Loukas filed a second habeas petition. Arrested under the charge of wife desertion and of being a fugitive from justice from Butler County, Kansas, Loukas argued that he had never been in or lived in the state of Kansas. A writ was issued, sustained, and Loukas was released on May 15, 1922.
