







In Justice's Court Seattle Precinct King County W.T. Before N. Soderberg Justice of
the Peace

Territory of Washington Plaintiff vs. Thomas Gilbert Defendant } Commitment

United States of America

Territory of Washington}
County of King} ss

To any constable in said county or the Keeper of the County Jail of said county Greeting

Whereas at a Justice Court held this day at the office of the undersigned in the City
of Seattle in said county before the undersigned a Justice of the Peace of said County
for the trial of  for the offense hereinafter stated the said  was convicted of having
on the 1st day of May 1888 in said county committed the crime of disposing of beer
to an Indian and upon conviction the said court did adjudge and determine that the
said Thomas Gilber



should pay a fine of $100 and $- costs and should be imprisoned in the County Jail
of King County W.T. for the period of 50 days or until the said fine and costs be
paid and Whereas the said Thomas Gilbert has made default in the payment of said fine
and costs. Therefore, you the said H.G. Thornton are hereby commanded forthwith to
convey and deliver the said Thomas Gilbert to said Keeper and you the said Keeper
are hereby commanded to receive the said Thomas Gilbert into your custody in said
Jail and him there keep for the space of 50 days unless he shall sooner be thence
discharged by due course of law

Dated at Seattle W.T. this 3 day of May 1888

N Soderberg
Justice of the Peace Seattle Precinct King County W.T.
