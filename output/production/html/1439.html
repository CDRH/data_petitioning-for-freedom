  • Summary of Proceedings: J. H. Rosseter sued C. W. Zaueker for the recovery of a few hundred dollars in the Recorder's Court for the City of Salem, Oregon. The city recorder ordered the arrest of Zaueker and set bail at $240. Zaueker petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus in the Marion County Circuit Court claiming that his detention was illegal, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, Zaueker was released and Rosseter ordered to pay his fees.
