Mary Ann ONeil Deft}            


Action for The
Crime of Vagrancy
Convicted 5 day of
Nov. 1886


Record of Conviction
This is to certify that Mary Ann
ONeil was convicted of the crime 
of Vagrancy on the 9th day of 
November 1886. as being an Habitual 
Drunkard and in default of Bonds
in the sum of 50.00 Dollars for her 
good behavior was committed to 
the County Jail for 30 days or 
until said Bond is filed and 
legally discharged from custody.

Given under my hand this 
9th day of November 1886
D. M. Lambert J. P.
