Habeas Corpus in matter of Chas. Dorsey

Capt. J.S. Dales

Have had the man on my boat for the last 3 yrs he lives in Allegheny town City Allegheny Co. Pa have always known him as a free man, shipped him to come to this port. he had hired with one on the Herald & was coming down with his dunnage when the officer took him. Know him by name of Charles Dorsey- has been up the Mo. River, from Pittsburgh here, has been at Louisville Ky.

Mr. Lanun have known 4 years about first knew him at Pittsburgh. have had him with me on 3 different steam boats as an Engineer. I hired him to go on the Herald. has always passed a free man, to the best of my knowledge he is free

Mr. Blagg - have known him some where in the neighborhood of 6 yrs. has been on perhaps 1/2 doz. or more boats with me. was barkeeper on boats so acquanted with him first on S.B. Cape May, running from Pittsburgh to Nashville. He made several trips on sd boat to Nashville. He was a fireman. made his home at Allegheny town he has sent money to his wife once or twice to Allegheny Town

April 23 1855
