In the District Court for the third Judicial District of Washington Territory holding terms at Seattle for the counties of King Kitsap and Snohomish

To the Sheriff or any constable of King County Washington Territory

Greeting

Whereas

An affidavit has been placed on file in the Clerk's Office of the aforesaid court in the city of Seattle county of King and Territory of Washington, wherof it [?] appears that the body of Seo Kim a Chinese woman is unlawfully detained and held in the cutody of an Ah Chow a Chinese man and against the will of the said woman Seo Kim and for the purpose of unlawfully transporting and removing her the said woman out of the jurisdiction of this court and out of this territory of Washington

Now therefore this is the command you and you are hereby commanded to take the body of the said Chinese woman Seo Kim and her safely keep and detain in your custody until the further order of the Hon Court herein Herein paid not and have you then and there this writ

Witness the Hon O Jacobs Judge of said Dist Court and the seal thereof this 21st day of Jan AD 1874

L.B. Andrews
Clerk
