No 137

In the District Court at Montesano

Territory of Washington
vs.
Gus Bendjus & al

The Grand Jury of Chehalis County having returned into this Court an indictment charging the defendants in said above named cause with having committed the crime of riot - and in appearing to the court that the defendants are not in custody or under recognizance to appear and answer in this court it is ordered that the Clerk of this court issue a warrant for the arrest of said defendants forthwith.

Dated this 16th day of September A.D. 1887

Frank Allyn, Judge  

Filed on the 16th day of Sept 1887 H.M. Sutton Clerk
