  • Summary of Proceedings: On September 8, 1923, Albert Hall was arrested. On September 10, he petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus. The respondents replied to the writ the next day and stated that they arrested Albert because he was allegedly a fugitive from justice from Cherokee County, Kansas. On September 15, 1923, the hearing for the writ was denied and Albert was remanded to the custody of the respondents.
