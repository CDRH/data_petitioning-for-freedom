  • Summary of Proceedings: Edward Fuller was arrested for threatening John Seibert in writing and for being an "accessory to Polygamy" and was committed to jail by a justice of the peace when he could not post a $1000 bail. Fuller maintained that these were not crimes and petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. The outcome of the hearing and fate of the bound party are unknown.
