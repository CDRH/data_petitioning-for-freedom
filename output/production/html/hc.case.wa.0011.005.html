Territory of Washington
County of Walla Walla }

In Justice Court before
OP Lacy Justice of the Peace

Territory of Washington
vs
Adaline Oneil Deft }

Defendant charged with
being a habitual Drunkard
and disorderly person

Complaint filed June 15th 1877 Warrant issued and the defendant arrested and arraigned and pleads guilty and John Lucas and J P Justice being first duly sworn on the part of the Territory and it appearing to the court from the evidence of the witnesses and the confession of the defendant that the defendant Adaline Oneil is an habitual Drunkard and a disorderly person the Court did adjudge and determine that the said Adaline Oneil was a Vagrant and that the said Adaline Oneil gave bonds in the sum of one hundred dollars for her good behavior for the term of three months and that in default thereof that the said Adaline Oneil be committed to the Jail of Walla Walla County for [?]   [?] [?] to you the bonds as required she the said Adaline Oneil was Committed to Jail for three months

Given under my hand this 15th day of June 1877

O P Lacy
Justice of the Peace

Territory of Washington

Adaline Oneil
Certificate of Conviction

Filed June 15th 1877

A Reeves Ayers
Clerk
