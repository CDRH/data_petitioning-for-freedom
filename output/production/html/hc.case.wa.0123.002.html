In the matter of the application of T.S. Roadman for Habeas Corpus Petition Filed Aug. 15, 1883 A Reeves Ayres, Clerk By A.W. Sarpe Dep.

 

In the District Court of Washington Territory and for the First Judicial District charged of holding terms at Cheney for the County of Spokane

In the matter of the application of T.S. Roadman for writ of Habeas Corpus. Order granting writ

On reading and filing the petition of W.C. Jones of Cheney Spokane County W.T. one of the attorneys for T.S. Roadman duly signed and verified by him whereby it appears that the said T.S. Roadman is illegally imprisoned and restrained of his liberty by P.W. Dillon Sheriff of Spokane County. The at the town of Cheney Spokane County in the Territory of Washington and stating wherein the illegally consists[?] from which it appears to be that a writ of Habeas Corpus ought to issue.

It is ordered that a writ of Habeas Corpus issue such under the seal of the District court of the First Judicial District Court of the Territory of Washington in and for the court of Spokane directed to the said sheriff commanding him to have the   body of the said T.S. Roadman before me in the court room of the said court on the 15th day of August 1883 at 7 o'clock P.M. of that day to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning the said T.S. Roadman together with the time and cause of his detention and that he then and there have the said writ.

Dated on the 15th day of August 1883

SC Wingard

No. 430
In the matter of the application of T.S. Roadman for writ of Habeas Corpus

Order granting writ

Filed Aug. 15, 1883
A Reeves Ayres, clerk
