I JH McGraw, Sheriff of King County W.T. do hereby certify that I received a writ
of Habeas Corpus, of which the annexed is a true copy, on the 20th day of November
1885, and that I personally served said writ on George J Finleyson, he being the 
person to whom said writ is directed by delivering to and leaving with him the said
George J. Finleyson in said King County on the said 20th day of November 1885, said
writ

Dated at Seattle this 21st day of November 1885
JH McGraw
Sheriff
