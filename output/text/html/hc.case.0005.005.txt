







Territory of Washington County of Walla Walla} In Justice Court before O. P. Lacy
Justice of the Peace

Territory of Washington v. Adaline O'Niell deft} Charged with being an habitual drunkard
and disorderly person

Defendant arraiged and pleads 
guilty to said charge wherefor 
the court did adjudge the Deft a 
vagrant and the said court did order
the Deft to give bonds in the sum 
of one hundred dollars for her 
good behavior for the term of 
three months and in default thereof
she was committed to the Jail of 
Walla Walla County for three months.

March 29th 1876
O P Lacy
Justice of the Peace
