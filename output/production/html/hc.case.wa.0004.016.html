In the District Court First Judicial District of W. T.

[?]

County of Walla Walla } ss

To W.H. Barret and Isabella Barret

Whereas an application has been made by Charles Gahres and Martha Gahres Guardians of Ella Dora Cravens a minor pursuant to Statue to the Hon Joseph. R Lewis Judge of the First District Court First Judicial District of Washington Territory and an order granted for a writ of Habeas Corpus. [?] the said W.H. Barret and Isabella Barret to appear and produce the body of Ella Dora Cravens and show cause [?] the said Ella Dora Cravens should not be be returned to [?] Charles Gahres and Martha Gahres.

Therefore in the name of the United States I am now commanded to appear before the Hon Joseph R. Lewis Judge at a Court to be held in Chambers in the Court house in the city of Walla   Walla Court of Walla Walla and Territory of Washington on Monday September 1st 1873 at 11 oclock A.M. then and there to produce the body of the said Ella Dora Cravens and show cause why the said minor child is withheld from the care and custody of [?] and for costs in the proceedings and abide such further order of the Court or may be made in selection thereto

Witness this now
Joseph R Lewis Judge
Aforesaid and the Jud Ct [?] Court this the 30th day of August AD 1873

Wm H Andrews

Clerk
