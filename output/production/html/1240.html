  • Summary of Proceedings: A complaint was made on October 3 by Rob Blich against Nick Glavich of assault and battery that happened October 2. Glavich was found guilty in the Police Court and fined $50, costs, and sentenced to 30 days hard labor. On October 17, Glavich and attorney, Louis Berka, filed petition of habeas against the sheriff, charging that his punishment is unlawful - that a Police Judge is unauthorized to pass such a judgment. On October 18, Glavich’s petition and writ was upheld and he was released from custody.
