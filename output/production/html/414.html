  • Summary of Proceedings: John Commons, Superintendent and Special Disbursing Agent of the Omaha Nation, was tasked with evicting Thomas Barada from land that belonged to Howard Provost, an Omaha man, under the Dawes Act. Aware of Commons' plans, Barada accused him of preparing to breach the peace in the Thurston County Court. Commons was arrested and jailed when he refused to pay a $200 peace bond and soon petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, Commons was released.
