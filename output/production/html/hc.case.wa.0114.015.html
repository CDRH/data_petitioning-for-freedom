Territory of Washington}
Third Judicial District} ss.

United States of America
vs.
William Jamieson, Michael Daly and William Winters.
Complaint for Conspiracy.

William F. Prosser, being first duly sworn deposes and says that he is a citizen of the United States, over twenty-one years of age and is a special agent of the General Land Office of the United States.

That on the 31st day of January 1885, at Seattle in King County in the Third Judicial District of Washington Territory William Jamieson, Michael Daly alias Michael Healy and William Winters then and there being did unlawfully conspire together to defraud the United States out of certain of its public lands situated in Chehalis County Washington Territory, to wit: Sections 2, 4-6-8-10--18- 20- and 30, in Township Twenty, North of Range Seven West of the Williamette Meridian, by hiring divers and sundry persons to wit; Edward Taylor, William H. Doyle and twenty others whose true names are to affiant unknown to unlawfully and fraudulently enter said lands at the United States Land Office at Olympia, Washington Territory under the provisions of the act of Congress approved the 3rd day of June 1878, entitled "An Act for the slesale of timber lands in the States of California, Oregon, Nevada and in Washington Territory". and did then and there unlawfully and fraudulently proceed to carry into affect the conspiracy, and did   purpose aforesaid, and did then and ther unlawfully and fraudulently proceed[?] and hire the said Edward Taylor, William H. Doyle and twenty other persons to go into the land office at Olympia aforesaid, and falsely swear to the sworn statements required by said act of Congress and the rules of said land office of applicants to enter lands under said act, and to file in said land office their said sworn statements demand applications to enter portions of said above described public lands, contrary to the statute in such cases made and provided, and against the peace and dignity of the United States.

WILLIAM F. PROSSER.

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 8th day of February, A.D. 1885.

Eben Smith, United States, Commissioner, in and for 3rd Judicial District Washington Territory.
