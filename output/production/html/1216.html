  • Summary of Proceedings: On March 23, 1922, Joe Vacarro filed for habeas against Chief of Police, Michael Dempsey, Chief of Detectives, C. H. Van Deusen, and Detective, John J. Pazanowski. In his petition, Vacarro explains that on March 22, 1922, he was arrested without complaint or warrant, and that a written complaint must be brought a district, court, or police judge, required by Sec. 8940 of the Rev. Statutes of Nebraska, 1915. Vacarro further suspected that the respondents are holding him at the sole request of an employee of the Savage Tires Company. A writ was issued but the case was dismissed for want of prosecution on January 21, 1914.
