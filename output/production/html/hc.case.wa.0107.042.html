In the District Court of the Second Judicial District, for Washington Territory

Territory of Wasington
County of Chehalis}

Charles Theasly, being first duly sworn on oath says that he was bailiff to the Grand Jury at the last September term of the district Court holding terms at Montesano, that as such bailiff he was instructed by the said Grand Jury to bring John I Medcalf before them for examination as a witness that he did to bring the said John I Medcalf and he remained in the Grand Jury room about fifteen minutes, that the day he was before said Grand Jury, was the day before said Grand Jury adjourned and were discharged by the court.

Charles Theasly

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 12th day of October AD 1887. Lewis B Bignold
Notary Public
of Washington Territory

 

Affidavit of Charles Theasly Bailiff to Grand Jury
