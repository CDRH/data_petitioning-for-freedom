United States of America

Territory of Washington

Third Judicial District } SS

To S E Gray Master of the Bark Gem of the Ocean

You are hereby summoned to appear before me JR Lewis Judge of the District Court of the 3d Judicial Distrist of said Territory held at Seattle in and for the countries of King Kitsap and Snohomish at my chambers in the City of Seattle and said County of King on the 3d day of May AD 1875, at the hour of 1 oclock PM to show cause if any you have, why admiralty prosess should not issue out of the District Court aforesaid, against the above named Bark whereof you are Master, her tackle apparel and furniture to answer the demands of Peter Nelson W Smith H Elsner C Whaly G Mattson Charles Lunblad Dennis Coffy and Jacob Annir late mariners on board said Bark for their wages as such mariners earned on the voyage from the Port of San Fransisco to the Port of Seattle between the 8th day of April and the [] day of May AD 1875.

Given under my hand this 3 day of May AD 1875

JR Lewis

Served the above summons on the above named Gray by reading the same to him and by delivering to him a true & correct copy of the same at Seattle in Dis Court WT

May 3 1875

Dated May 3 1875

LV Wycoff
Dept U.S. Marshall
W.T.

Dist Court 3 Dis

In Admiralty

Peter Nelson et al
