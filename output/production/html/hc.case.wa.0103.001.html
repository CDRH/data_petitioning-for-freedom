#1854
Ex "B"

A Campbell J.P.

United States of America vs. Prospero Depelippes Felip Perell} Criminal Warrant

To the Sheriff or any Constable of said County.

Whereas S. L. Humphrey Master of the Bark Elderado has this day complained in writing under oath to the undersigned one of the Justice of the Peace in and for the said County that Prospero Depelippes and Felip Perell are Seaman under written contract and belong to the Bark Eldorado and now loading in the port of Tacoma and that they have willfully deserted said ship without fulfilling said contract.

Therefore, in the name of the United States you are commanded forthwith to apprehend the said Prospero Depelippes and Felip Perell and bring them before me to be dealt with according to law.

Given under my hand this 29th day of June A.D. 1886

Alex Campbell

Justice of the Peace.
