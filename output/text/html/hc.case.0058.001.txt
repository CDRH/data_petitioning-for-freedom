







In the District Court of the third Judicial district of Washington Territory holding
terms at Port Townsend

In the matter of the application of Robert Moulton for a writ of Habeas Corpus

It appearing  ing  to the Court by the pleadings and  herein made and filed that the
said Rosa Moulton and Nellie Moulton are the minor children of said Robert Moulton,
and that they are unlawfully detained by the said James Eaus the respondent herein
and the said minor children being now in the custody of the Court. It is therefore
considered and adjudged by the court that said children be taken from the custody
of the said James Eaves and restorted to the custody and control of said Robert Moulton
and that the said James Eaus pay the costs in this case taxed at        dollars and
that execution issue therefore 
Greene
