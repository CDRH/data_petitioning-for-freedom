State of Ohio Hamilton County } S.S.

James Richardson of the City of Cincinnati in the County and State aforesaid being first duly sworn on his oath saith: I am acquainted with a black man named George Washington Kirk, who is now said to be in jail in the City of St. Louis in the State of Missouri. And have known him ever since the year 1844. I am a practicing physician and was the family physician of Kasey Kirk, father of the said George Washington Kirk from the year 1844 to the year 1853 inclusive. The said George Washington Kirk is a negro is probably about five feet and a half in height and rather disposed to be corpulent. Has a kind of rolling walk as though he were lame. Has no special mark as affiant remembers or has noticed. Said Geo Washington Kirk has during the period affiant has known him always been received and reputed to be a free man person, and affiant has never heard any doubt expressed of his right to freedom should suppose him to be about twenty-two years of age. He always lived with his father whilst I knew him (from 1844 to 1853) and further affiant saith not.

Jas Richardson
