  • Summary of Proceedings: On August 13th, 1921, Loren Laughlin submitted a petition for a writ of habeas corpus on behalf of his clients Thomas Wyatt Robinson and Bessie Kraus. The petition alleged that they were being unlawfully detained in the Wymore city jail by William Lytle (City Marshal) and a John Doe. The petition alleged that Thomas and Bessie had not been presented with any copy of commitment, warrant, or other cause of detention although they had asked for them. The petition was granted, and the writ allowed. The judge ordered that Thomas and Bessie be released from police custody.
