  • Summary of Proceedings: On August 7th, 1922, Gertie Folden filed a petition for a writ of habeas corpus on behalf of her minor daughter Adean. She named her ex-husband and Adean's father, Homer Folden, as respondent. She explained that she divorced Homer in October 1921 and was granted custody of Adean. On July 22nd, she claimed Homer obtained possession of Adean through deception and had since refused to return Adean, thus she was being unlawfuuly detained by Homer. The petiton was granted, and the writ allowed. Adean was to be returned to the custody of her mother.
