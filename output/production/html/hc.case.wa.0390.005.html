Territory of Washington County of Walla Walla

To James McAuliff Sheriff of Walla Walla County you are hereby commanded to have the body of the within named John Doe before me at the Court House in Walla Walla at 3 o'clock of the afternoon of this the 13th day of October 1869 together with the cause of his detention of any you have

Witness my hand the day and Year above written

James K. Kennedy
Judge &c.
