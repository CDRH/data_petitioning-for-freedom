Territory of Washington County of Walla Walla } SS

To the Hon Jas K Kennedy Judge of the District Court of the 1st judicial District W. T.

As per writ of Habeas Corpus served on me as Sheriff of Walla Walla County W. T. by R. P. Reynolds Clerk of the District Court of the 1st Judicial District W. T. to show cause why one John Doe was restrained in the jail of Walla Walla County W. T.

1st That the said John Doe is a fugitive from justice from Umatilla County Oregon

2nd That the said John Doe broke jail from Umatilla County Oregon some time in the winter of 1868 or 1869

3rd That in or about the 5th or 6th day of September 1869 I filed an affidavit with Charles W. Frush, Justice of the Peace in and for Walla Walla County that the said John Doe was a fugitive from justice from said Umatilla County Oregon on which the said justice issued a warrant for the arrest of the said John Doe   and in persuance of said warrant the said John Doe was arrested by me as Sheriff of Walla Walla County in or about the 5th or 6th day of September 1869

4th And in the arrest being made of his own free will acknowledged that he did broke jail from Umatilla County in the winter of 1868 & 1869

5th That the said John Doe was committed to the County jail of Walla Walla County W. T. to await a Requisition from the Governor of Washington Territory. Said commitment was until the 12th day of October 1869

6th that as there was no Requisition arrived by request of [] Thompson Sheriff of Umatilla County Oregon on the 13th day of October 1869 I made a further affidavit for the said John Doe commitment until the 31st day of October 1869 which was granted by the above named Chas W. Frush Justice of the Peace

7th That the said John Doe was confined in the County jail   of Umatilla County Oregon on the charge of Grand Larceny to await the acton of the Grand Jury of said County

8th Reference is made to the papers in the case which is now on file in the office of C. W. Frush Justice of the Peace

James McAuliff

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 13th day of Oct. 1869

R P Reynolds
Clerk
