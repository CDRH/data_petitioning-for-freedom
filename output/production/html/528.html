  • Summary of Proceedings: On August 9, 1918, George W. and Elise M. Hahne filed for habeas against Elsie's parents, Fred E. and Mary A. Sawtell for custody over their minor children, George W. and Dale D. Hahne. In the petition the Hahne's claimed that the Sawtells agreed to maintain care of children during certain parts of the year in Hastings, Adams County, Nebraska. Furthermore, full custody and care remained in hands of the Hahnes in Omaha and that the petition comes forth after the Sawtells refused to give the children back. The petition was granted, but the cause was later dismissed by the Hahnes on August 27, 1918. Census records indicate that the family continued to share custody, as the boys appear in both the Hahnes' household in the 1920 census on January 7, 1920, and in the Sawtells' house in Hastings on January 9-10, 1920.
