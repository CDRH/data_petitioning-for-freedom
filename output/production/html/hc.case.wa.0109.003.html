United States of America

In the District Court of the 2d Judicial Dist for the Territory of Washington

The United States of America

To Jesse Martin of the County of Thurston Territory of Washington Greeting:

We command you that you have the body of Mary M. Martin, by you imprisoned and detained, before the Hon. J.E. Wyche Judge of the 2d Judicial District of the Territory of Washington, at the Court room of the District Court of the U.S. for said District in Olympia on the 23d day of March A.D. 1868 at 12'oclock noon of that day, to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning the said Mary M. Martin. And you are hereby commanded and forbidden from taking the said Mary M Martin minor child, out of the Jurisdiction of this Court until the matters and things mentioned in the petion[?] for this writ may be heard and determined

Witness the Hon. J.E. Wyche Judge of the Dist Court of the U.S. in and for the 2d Dist of Washington Territory this 2d day of March A.D. 1868. and my private seal (the seal of the Court being in the possession of R.H. Hewitt late Clerk &c)

Francis Henry
Clerk
