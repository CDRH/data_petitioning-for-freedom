James Glispen sworn and testified

Am sheriff of Spokane County met her first saw defendant last Wednesday in Cal Turners office - has been in my custody since- she delivered herself up to me in Cal Turners office she did not state say anything to me when I took her into custody

In consequence of a conversation between myself and Cal Turner - I went to his (Turners) office with him and I took her Mary Teller into my custody to answer to the charge of killing Frank Teller she was in my persona charge say 1 1/2 hours - she did not say anything to me about this killing while my charge I know Dr Waterhouse and Mr. Stoltz Know where the defendant was only from reading[?] where defent[?] resides know of the killing last tuesday a week ago May 31st 1887

On last Wednesday I saw the body of a Man supposed to have been killed about 1 1/2 miles West of Deep Creek Falls Spokane County WT heard Mr Stoltz testimony it was the same man he spoke of - From what Mr. Turner had told me and from   this duty[?] into custody I found the corner Dr Penfield and together Mr[?] [?] there to investigate the circumstances concerning this murder of Frank Teller Shown Diagram and says substantially correct - made measurements - from front gate to door 2 ft 3 inches - from pool of blood that ran from his head to the gate was 12 feet and 3 inches saw the body before moved - head by where blood was from feet to front door about 7 feet did not measure that - 12 feet from house to where his head lay body to butt end of gun about 2 feet shot[?] from gun say 12 to 18 inches Mr Stoltz then when I was then was present at the examination by Waterhouse and coroner - I assisted I took off his pants and some other clothes saw no mark of a bullet [?] where it would come in in clothing the heart saw no bullet holes in the clothes Gun laying on the grass both barrells cocked about as indicated in Diagram took gun into my custody identified gun took shot identified club - saw ax there cannot say whether   this one is same or not - blood spots round wound the body Door and and a pool of blood M towards the corner of the house 5 feet [?] in from the other - no blood between the body and the gate head cut on one side skull all broke in the other were small[?] spots on the body just little red spots spot on the shoulder near the nipples head all [?] up could take hold of it and hear it [?] under the heads left side of head all [?] - on the other side a cut

I did not examine the clothes with reference to bullet holes - wounds on the body might have been made by a spent bullet am not expert enough to say effect of spent bull if not going through clothing - blood on knees partly dried off not near as much now as then I got it was handled by several people board is in the same condition When I got it Wednesday morning first I saw gun - blood was dry cannot see it as plainly now as when I first got it

Jas Glispen

 

Sworn and subscribed to before me this 8th day of June 1887

Jno J Booge
