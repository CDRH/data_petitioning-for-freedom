In the District Court holding Terms at Montesano

The Territory of Washington vs. Gus Bendjus Robert Stronglaud Pete Peterson Ben Fredrickson Frank Nelson Fred Stromburger John Blending Andrew Johnson Leander Brown John Saxon John Thompson Alex Agren Thomas Jacobs E. Emil Andrew Anderson Charles Burke } Warrant

The President of the United States To the Sheriff of Chehalis County Greeting:

Whereas, Gus Bendjus, Robert Stronglaud,

 

Pete Peterson, Ben Fredrickson, Frank Nelson, Fred Strongburger, John Blending, Andrew Johnson, Leander Brown, John Saxon, John Tompson, Alex Agren, Thomas Jacobs, E. Emel, Andrew Anderson, Charles Burke having been duly indicted in the District Court of the Second Judicial District of Washington Territory, holding Terms at Montesano of the crime of Malicious Mischeif all of which appears to be of record

Now this is to command you, the said Sheriff, to take the said Gus Bendjus, Robert Strongland, Pete Peterson, Ben Fredrickson, Frank Nelson, Fred Strongburger, John Blending, Andrew Johnson, Leander Brown, John Saxon, John Thompson, Alex Agren, Thomas Jacobs, E. Emel, Andrew Anderson, Charles Burke and them the said Gus Bendjus, Robert Strangland, Pete Peterson, Ben Fredrickson, Frank Nelson, Fred Strongburger, John Blending, Andrew Johnson, Leander Brown, John Saxon, John Thompson, Alex Agren, Thomas Jacobs, E. Emel, Andrew Anderson, Charles Burke safely keep and have them forthwith in this Court there to answer to said charge, and abide such further order as the Court may make in the premises Herein fail not

 

Witness the Honorable Frank Allyn Judge of the said District Court and the Seal of said Court this day of 19th A.D. 1887

HM Sutton Clerk.

Territory of Washington}

County of Chehalis}

I hereby certify that I received the within Warrant on the 20th day of September AD 1887 and served the same on the 20th day of September AD 1887 by arresting and taking into custody the following within named defendants Gus Bendjus, Robert Strongland, whose true name is Robert Straugland, Ben Fredrickson, Frank Nelson, Andrew Johnson, Leander Brown, John Thompson, Alex Agren and Thomas Jacobs and court not then being in session. I did on the 19th day of October AD 1887 accept bail from the following named persons to wit Gus Bendjus, Robert Strangland, Ben Fredrickson, Frank Nelson, Andrew Johnson, Leander Brown, Alex Agren and Thomas Jacobs Conditioned[?] That they appear at the next regular term of the District Court to be held for Chehalis County, and from term to term thereafter or until duly cleared by due process of law. On accepting of said bail I discharged said parties from custody, and I have been unable after due and diligent search to find any of the others of the defendants named in the within warrant

Dated this 14th day of November AD 1887

WS Bush Sheriff
of Chehalis County
