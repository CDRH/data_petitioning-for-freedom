  • Summary of Proceedings: William Bridwell (also spelled Birdwell) petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus claiming that Louis C. Otto and William T. B. Ireland had wrongfully deprived him of his liberty. Judge Hall granted the petition. Upon delivering the order, it was discovered that Bridwell had already been released from custody. Weeks later, Judge Tibbetts dismissed the cause at the respondents' costs.
