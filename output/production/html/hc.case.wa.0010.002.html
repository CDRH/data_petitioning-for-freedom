In the District Court of the Territory of Washington, and for the First Judicial District thereof, holding terms at Walla Walla, for the Counties of Walla Walla and Franklin, in said Territory

In the matter of the United States of America

To the sheriff of Walla Walla County Territory of Washington County

In having been known to me by the application of H.J. Mann that he is the father of Bessie Mann a child four years old and that Cynthia Mann has the illegal custody of said child in said county and is about to absent from said Territory therewith and wont do so unless this warrant issue. Now therefore you are hereby commanded to immediately arrest and bring before me the said child Before and to serve a copy of said complaint and affadavit in this case and a copy of this writ upon said Cynthia Mann

And you the said Cynthia Mann are commanded to appear before me with the said child and show cause if any you have why the said child before shall not be immediately given into the care and custody of her said father

William G Langford
Judge
