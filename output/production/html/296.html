  • Summary of Proceedings: On January 27th, 1913, John Jakobowski filed a petition for a writ of habeas corpus, claiming that he was being unlawfully detained by Sheriff John Schiek. The petition revealed that John was being detained under a warrant after the Commissioners of Insanity found that he should be committed to the Nebraska Hospital for the Insane at Lincoln. But, the superintendent of the Nebraska Hospital for the Insane refused to receive John.
