Recalled by Pros

W.D. Gillespie

I reached the body something less than one minute after the shot Dr Morgan Came between 10 & 15 minutes after I reached the body,

The Body was not lying same when he arrived I got assisting and moved it back in the Hall before he came Hall was about four feet wide his head was about a foot from outer door

W.D. Gillespie
