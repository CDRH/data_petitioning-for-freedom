John H Stiel makes oath that the foregoing petition concerning the illegal detention of James Allison Parker - a free man of color, is true as he hereof believes

Said Stiel has known the said Parker for the last five or six years in the State of Illinois & knows the mother of said Parker to be a free woman and said James Allision Parker to be not a slave but a free man

John H. Stiel

Duly sworn before me on this 29th day of Septr 1858

H. Dusenbury
