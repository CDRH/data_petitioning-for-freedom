IN THE UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT FOR THE WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON, NORTHERN DIVISION.

IN THE MATTER OF THE APPLICATION OF GENEVIVE WILDS FOR A WRIT OF HABEAS CORPUS} No. 39895 ORDER.

This matter coming on regularly for hearing and it appearing to the court that proceedings have been commenced in the Juvenile court in relation to the matters herein involved and that the best interests of all parties concerned that the matter be heard in the Juvenile court, now therfore, upon motion of the attorney for petitioner, this proceeding in this court is hereby dismissed withought prejudice.

Done in open court this 26th day of Febuary, 1918

Jeremiah Neterer
Judge.
