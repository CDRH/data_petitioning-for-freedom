State of Missouri County of St. Louis } In Circuit Court Hm Judge Rombauer presiding

Petition for a writ of habeas corpus

Petitioner states by Wm Bush that on the 26th of December 1867 she Caroline Bauer was arrested by a police officer (at the instigation of John Bauer her husband who has for more than 4 months last past deserted her; ) and brought to St. Vincent insane asylum a private institution situated cnr[?] 9th-Warien street in city of St. Louis

Petitioner by Wm Bush further states: that she Carolina Bauer is there now illegaly restraint and confined against her will; petitioner by Wm Bush further states that she Mrs. Bauer has about 6 children the youngest of which is about one year old from which she has been forcibly separated and for which she has all ways properly provided. Petitioner by Wm. Bush futher states that there is a divorce pending between her - her husband; and that he has prevented two certificates from 2 persons purporting to be doctors a true copy of which is here with filed pretending that she is a monomaniac, which is the only authority by which sister Julia Superior of said asylum claims to hold and confine her.

Petitioner by Wm Bush further states that said arrest was illegal and against the statute law in such cases provided in that there has not been a judicial examination in the probate or county court of St. Louis to inquire in her sanity - or insanity as is required by statute. Petitioner by Wm Bush therefore prays this honorable court to grant a writ of Habeas Corpus to inquire her cause of confinement. Petitioner by Wm Bush further states that there has not been made any application to any court or officer superior   to the one now applied to; Petitioner by Wm Bush further states that said Mrs Bauer is not insane which he is able to prove by witnesses in trial

Petitioner by Wm Bush therefore prays the court to liberate her from confinement if facts appear as set forth in this petition and to make such further order as the Court may deem proper

Caroline Bauer by Wm Bush her Atty

The above named Wm Bush being duly sworn says that the facts stated in above petition are true according to his best knowlege and belief

Wm Bush

Sworn to and subscribed before me Jany 2nd 68

J Lewis Clk
