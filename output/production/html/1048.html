  • Summary of Proceedings: Rittmaster was taken into custody due to the oral order of Hon. Melville R. Hopewell, District Court Judge. Judge Hopewell did this based on claims that Rittmaster is a fugitive of justice from the state of Colorado for obtaining money under false pretenses. Rittmaster claimed that no affidavit was filed that had charged Rittmaster with being a fugitive of justice or committing any criminal offense. Additonally, Rittmaster claimed that despite being innocent he had been treated cruelly and inhumanly in close confinement, denied bail, and imprisoned without evidence. Judge Hopewell and Judge George W. Doane found that Rittmaster was unlawfully detained and he was released from custody.
