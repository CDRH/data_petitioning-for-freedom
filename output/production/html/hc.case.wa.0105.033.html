Territory of Washington,}

Third Judicial District} SS.

To the Sheriff or any Constable in the County of Snohomish in said District, or any other Sheriff of said District:

Whereas William H. Waterman has this day complained under oath to the undersigned Chief Justice of the Territory of Washington that on the Thirty first day of October 1864, or thereabouts, as the County of Snohomish in said District that James Riley Frank Dolan James Widdows Peter Vaz and Samuel Moore Job Field & John Gardner did feloniously kill and murder one Charles, and two other Indians of the Snohomish tribe of Indians, whose names are unknown. Therefore in the name of the United States, you are commanded forthwith to apprehend the said James Riley

and bring them before me, to be dealt with according to law Given under my hand, at Steilacoom, in said Third District, this tenth day of November, 1864.

Witness the seal of our District Court for said District, holding Terms at Steilacoom in said District, on the day and date last above written

J H Munson Clerk

C.C. Hewitt C.J. and Judge of 3d Judl Dist W.T.
