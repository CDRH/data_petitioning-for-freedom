







[WAL-2342]

The  George F. Thomas Sheriff of Walla Walla County, Washington Territory to the writ
of Habeas Corpus hereto answered

In obedience to the writ of Habeas Corpus hereto command &amp; do hereby certify and whereto
the Hon S.G. Winegard Judge of the First Judicial District Washington Territory that
before the coming of said writ to me on the 29th day of March 1876 at Walla Walla
Washington Territory the said Mary A O'Neil mentioned in said writ was placed in my
custody by virtue of a Commitment of said  Judicial Court by O. P. Lacy one of the
Justice of the Peace in and for Walla Walla County this under my Sentence of said
Court for being a Habitual drunkard and disorderly person and ordered to the Committeed
to the County Jail of Walla Walla County for the period of one month until she should
give bond in the sum of one hundred dollars for her good wherein and that the said
Mary A. O'Neil is now in my custody by under of said Commitment a



copy of which is hereto annexed and which I certify and I have here her body before
the said Hon S.G. Wingard 'Judge' as by the said writ I have Commanded

George F. Thomas 
Shff W. W. Co.
