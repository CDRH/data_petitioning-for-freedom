Territory of Washington vs. Gus Bendjus et al.

This cause coming on to be heard on this 15th day of October, before Hon. Frank Allyn, Judge of the Second Judicial District of Washington Territory for the application of defendants for release from confinement in the county jail in Thurston county , Washington Territory, and the court having heard the arguments of counsel for both partities hereto, and being fully advised in the presmises , it is hereby

Ordered that said application of said defendants to be released is hereby refused and said defendants Gus Bendjus, Robert Strangland, Alex Agren, and Thomas Jacobs are hereby remanded to the custody of the Sheriff of Chehalis conty to be confined at Olympia, Thurston county, Washington Territory, for safe-keeping until they can be tried at Montesano, Chehalis County, unless before that time released by bond or due process of law. All of which defts by their attnys severally[?] except & such exception is entered[?].

Frank Allyn
Judge of said District  

Territory vs Gus Benjus et al.

Filed on the 21st day of October 1887.

HM Sutton
Clerk

Entered three times.
