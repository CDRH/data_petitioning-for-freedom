SUBPOENA

In the Justice Court of Columbia County, W.T.

Before J.C. Dorr, Justice of the Peace

Territory of Washington, Plaintiff vs Jacob Schuman Defendant

Filed July 27 1886

FEES Serving 1 $40 Mileage, 2 miles $20 $60

Territory of Washington, County of Columbia. } SS

I. J H Hosler sheriff do hereby certify that I served the within Subpoena on the within named witness by reading the same to him in Columbia County, W.T., on the 28th day of July 1886.

J H Hosler Sheriff Columbia County WP

 

Territory of Washington, County of Columbia. } SS

To George V. Crossler

In the name of the United States: You are hereby required to appear before the undersigned, one of the Justices of Peace in and for said county, on the 29th day of July 1886, at 10 o'clock in the fore noon, at his office in the Precinct of [] to give evidence in a certain cause, then and there to be tried, between the Plaintiff, and Jacob Schuman Defendant, on the part of the Territory Given under my hand this 27th day of July 1886.

J.C. Dorr
Justice of the Peace
