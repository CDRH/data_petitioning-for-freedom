William Finney being duly sworn on His oath saith that some time two or three weeks ago he saw the girl now present tied to a bedpost and Mr Placket beating her over the head with a broom stick and also pushing or punching her in the side with the end of it—some short time after that he saw the girl down and Mr Plackett either kicking or stomping her. he is not sure which—a few minutes before he had seen the girl with irons on and he thinks had them on then—

Sworn to and subscribed before me 19th December 1825 P Ferguson J.P.

Wm Finney
