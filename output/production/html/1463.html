  • Summary of Proceedings: On July 16, 1924, Mike Jaska was arrested for the unlawful possession of still, mash, and intoxicating liquor. On August 2, he was found guilty and Judge Frank M. Dineen sentenced him to 30 days in the Douglas County Jail along with a $500 fine. On December 22, Mike petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus and cited the 1913 Nebraska statutes 9138, 9144, and 9199. According to the appearance docket, the hearing was continued and then his writ was denied. He was then remanded to the custody of the respondents.
