In the District Court Judicial District of W.T.
Territory of Washington
County of Walla Walla } ss

W. H. Barret and Isabella Barret

Whereas an application has been made by Charles Gahres and Martha Gahres Guardians of Ella Dora Cravens a minor persuant to statute to the Hon. Joseph R. Lewis Judge of the [?] District Court, First Judicial District of Washington Teritory and an order granted for a Writ of Habeas Corpus commanding the said W.H. Barret and Isabella Barret to appear and produce the body of Ella Dora Cravens and show cause why the said Ella Dora Cravens should not be returned to guardians Charles Gahres and Martha J. Gahres

Therefore in the name of the United States You are commanded to appear before the Hon Joseph R. Lewis Judge at a Court to be held at Chambers in the Court House in the city of Walla Walla County of   Walla Walla and Teritory of Washington on Monday September 1st 1873 at 10 oclock AM then and then to produce the body of the said Ella Dora Cravens and show cause why the said minor child is withheld from the care and custody of affiants and for costs in this proceedings and would such further order of the court on any [?] in [?] threats

Witness the Hon Joseph R Lewis Judge [?]and the lead of Disct Court [?] the 29th day of August A.D. 1873

Wm H Andrews[?]
