  • Summary of Proceedings: In August 1883, a petition for a writ of Habeas Corpus was filed on behalf of J.S. Roadman on the basis of him being unlawfully restrained of his liberty by P.W. Dillon, Sheriff of Spokane County. The petition alleged the complaint upon which Roadman was arrested did not charge him with the commission of any crime, and the evidence did not prove Roadman guilty of any crime. Judge S.C. Wiugand authorized the petition, yet the outcome of the writ and the fate of Roadman is unknown.
