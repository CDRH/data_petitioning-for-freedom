  • Summary of Proceedings: Joseph Ranzer was arrested and jailed upon an accusation of murdering his wife. He petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. His jailer, Gabriel Darlington, responded that Ranzer had already been "taken from [his] custody" by Louis du Breuil, a justice of the peace who had produced a warrant for Ranzer. Ranzer's fate is unknown.
