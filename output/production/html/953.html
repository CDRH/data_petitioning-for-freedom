  • Summary of Proceedings: George A. Tarrant filed a petition to receive custody of his daughter, Eva, from his former wife, Lila Daniels, and her current husband, Ed Daniels. George Tarrant alleged that his wife left him in 1898 with their three children, the youngest being Eva at 13 and the others around 17 and 18, to marry Ed Daniels and that he did not know her or the children's whereabouts until recently. George found that they were residing in Washington County, Nebraska. He stated that Eva is being abused in every way, and it was known in the neighborhood that the Daniels lived in that they were unfit to care for children. The outcome of the case is unclear.
