  • Summary of Proceedings: On December 13, 1918, Mrs. Alice Stanely filed habeas, with attorney George C. Porter, against husband Edward Stanely for custody of minor child, Lorina Stanely. Alice charged that her husband had deserted her and their three children on May 5, 1916, and has been having a relationship and living with a woman of color by the name of Mrs. Lulu Walton in Omaha. On April 15, 1918, Edward obtained their daughter Lorina Stanely, prompting Alice to file for custody. On January 30, 1920, the case was dismissed for want of prosecution. Fate of Lorina is unknown.
