Imperial Chinese Consulate General
San Francisco, April 6 1889

To whom it may concern

I, the undersigned, his Imperial Chinese Majesty Consul General do hereby certify
that Lee Sing Wot whose photograph is here attached is a merchant member



of the firms of Zuong Lin Nening &amp; Co. 733 Commercial St. of San Francisco, and that
he was former a merchant at Victoria B.C. and that he is lawfully married to the lady
whose photograph is here attached, and the lady is at present living in Victoria,
and that they both belong to the exempt class and is entitled to come to the United
States of  their own free will and accord, according to he treaty between the United
States and China of 1880

Liang Ying Tsan
Consul General
L Sellinger 1006 1/2 Wash St.
T Jackson 710 Dupont St.
