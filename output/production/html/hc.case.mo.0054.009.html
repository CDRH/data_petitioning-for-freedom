Personally appeared before me, Jerome Mudd a Notary Public in and for said County, Simon P. Turley and Jane Turley his wife, who being duly sworn according to Law state that they have recently received the annexed letter, marked A, dated St Louis; June 1st 1835; that they are well acquainted with the hand writing of Geo W. Turley, and that said letter is in his handring; that they are the parents of said Geo W. Turley, who was born on 1st Oct 1829 in Alexandria, formerly part of the District of Columbia, but now belonging to the State of Virginia; that he is free born, they his parents, being both free at the time of his birth; the said deponents further state that they left Virginia about five years ago, and have been residing about two years in Cincinnati; that said G.W. Turley is of a dark complexion, straight and tall, being about six feet high; the deponents further state that said Geo W. Turley has been employed in the capacity of cook on Steam Boats trading below the Falls of Ohio, and that he has been absent from this City rather more than a year

Simon P. Turley

Witness Jeromy Mudd
