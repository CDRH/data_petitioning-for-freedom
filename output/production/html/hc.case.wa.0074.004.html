In the District Court of the Third Judicial District of Washington Territory, holding terms at Seattle. To the Hon Roger S Greene Judge of said Court

I hereby make return as follows to the writ of Habeas Corpus, in re Lee Kee, and say that I hold the said Lee Kee as prisoner in the City Jail of Seattle by virtue of a commitment issued by Geo G. Lyon a Justice of the Peace of the city Court of City[?] in and for the Precinct of Seattle, a copy of which is hereto annexed and made a part of this return but nevertheless I have the body of said Lee Kee in Court pursuant to you Honor's writ.

J H Woolery Chief of Police of the City of Seattle

Subscribed and sworn before me this 27th day of May AD 1885 by Geo G. Lyon Justice of the Peace
