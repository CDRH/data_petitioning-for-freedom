Sister Mary Ignatious of the Sacred Heart Mother Superior of the House of the Good Shepherd for return to the within writ states that she has under her charge the said Frances Vanhorn. That said Frances Vanhorn is a minor and under the age of 17 and will be 18 years of age on 11[?] November[?] next that said Frances Vanhorn was committed to said House of the Good Shephard by consent of her father who still lives. And in Obedience to the mandate of the within writ the body of said Frances Vanhorn is here brought into Court

St Louis August 25. 1865

Sister Mary of the Sacred Heart Superior

Sworn to and subscribed before me this 20th day of August

Testimony whereof I hereto set my [?] and affix the seal of the St Louis Land Court at Office the date aforesaid

Robert White Clerk
