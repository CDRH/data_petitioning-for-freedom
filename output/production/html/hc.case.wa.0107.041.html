Tacoma W.T. Oct 15th 1887

In obedience to the annexed writ, I do hereby return to this Hon Court that heretofore, on the 28th day of Sept 1887. WH Bush esq Sheriff of Chehalis County delivered to me for safe keeping in the jail of Thurston County, the following named persons viz: Gus Bengus, Robert Strangeland, Ben Fredrickson, Frank Nelson, Andrew Johnson, Leander Brown, John Thompson, Alex Green, and Thomas Jacobs. He the said sheriff gave me a written order to hold the said prisoners in the jale of Thurston County, also delivered their papers proporting to be three copies of indictments against all of the above named prisoners, which papers I attach to, and return with this writ, I also herewith have the bodies of all of said persons in this court, as in this said writ directed

William Billings
Sheriff of Thurston County
W.T.
