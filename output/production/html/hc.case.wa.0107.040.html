In the district court holding terms at Olympia

Territory of Washington, v.s. Gus Bendjus, et al.

In the matter of the application on Petition of certain persons for a writ of Habeas Corpus

Whereas it appears by the petition of Ben Frederickson duly verified, that certain persons are ilegally imprisoned by William Billings, Sheriff of thurston County, Washington Territory, presented to me this day.

Now, in the Name of the United States of America: to William Billings, Sheriff of Thurston County, W.T., greeting:

We command you to have the bodies of Gus Bendjus, Robert Stranghauet, Ben Fredrickson, Frank Nelson, Andrew Johnson, Leander Brown, John Thompson, Alex Green and Thomas Jacobs, by you detained and imprisoned, as in said petetion alleged, before the Judge of the second Judicial district at his chambers at the City of Tacoma at the Court house in said city, together with the cause or causes of their detention and imprisonment, to do a and receive what shall be ordered concerning them, and have you then and there this Writ, with your return attached thereto, on the 13" day of October 1887, at the hour of ten o clock A.M. Witness the Honorable Frank Allyn, Judge of the Second Judicial District of Washington Territory, this 6" day of October 1887.

H Reeves Ayres
Clerk.

The clerk is hereby directed to issue the foregoing Writ at once, and delivered to said Sheriff without delay. returnable at Tacoma, Thursday 13" Oct. 1887

At Chambers Oct. 6" 1887.

Frank Allyn Judge
