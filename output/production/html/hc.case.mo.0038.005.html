State of Missouri

County of St. Louis } Ss

William Greyon, personally appeared before me Liberty Waite a Justice of the Peace within & for said county & being duly sworn says he has taken up as a runaway slave in the County of St. Louis & State of Missouri a negro man about 37 years old, who goes by the name of Charles Dorsey & this affiant believes the said Charles Dorsey is a runaway slave and prays his commitment may be had for his safe keeping.

William Greyon

Sworn to & subscribed before me this 20th day of March 1855

Liberty Waite

Justice Peace
