The State of Missouri

To James A Felps Jailor of Saint Louis County, Greeting:

You are commanded to have the body of Charles Dorsey before the Honorable Law Commissioners Court of Saint Louis County on this 22nd 24th day of April, A.D. 1855, at five O'clock P.M. together with the time and cause of his detention by you to do and receive what shall then and there be adjudged concerning said Charles Dorsey, who as alleged in the annexed petition is illegally restrained of his liberty by you, and have you then and there this writ with your return thereon.

Given under my hand and the seal of said Court at office in Saint Louis this 2 3rd day of April 1855

John W. Colvin

Law Com
