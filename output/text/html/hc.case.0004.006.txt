







At a regular term of the Circuit Court of the State of Oregon for the County of Union
began and held at the Court House in LeGrande Oregon on the first day of November
AD 1869 there was present here JG Wilson Judge AC Craig Sheriff and ES McCoreras County
Clerk

Due proclamation of the Opening of Court having been made. The following among other
business was transacted on the fifth Judicial day of said term

Martha J. Craven
vs
WH Craven

Now this cause coming on to be heard Plaintiff appeared by RA Prince her attorney
and the Defendant although duly served with process appeared not, but made default,
and the Prosecuting Attorney appearing in behalf of the State. Now therefore due consideration
of the allegations in the complaint and the evidence submitted to the Court and on
Motion of Said Plaintiffs attorney it is ordered and adjudged that the marriage contract
existing between the plaintiff and defendant be dissolved 



on account of habitual gross drunkeness on the part of the defendant contracted since
the marriage contract existing between the plaintiff and defendant, and continuing
for two years next prior to the commencement of this suit, and on account further
of cruel and inhumane treatment and personal indignities making life burthensome by
the Defendant to the Plaintiff and it appearing further by the allegations and proof
that these parties have one child by the name of Ella Dora Craven of the age of three
years. Therefore on motion of said plffs attorney it is further ordered and decreed
that the said plaintiff Martha have the custody and education of said Etty Dora and
that the defendant William Harry Craven pay the costs and disbursements herein to
be received made and expended to be herein taxed and that execution issue therefore.

Signed Joseph G. Wilson
Judge
