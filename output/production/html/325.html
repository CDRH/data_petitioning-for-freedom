  • Summary of Proceedings: In 1922, John N. Baldwin, the petitioner, filed for a writ of Habeas Corpus for Bert H. Palmer, the bound party. Palmer's petition claimed that he was being held for the purpose of extorting money from him. Judge Leslie filed an order for Palmer to be brought to court, and then the record stops. In 1924 the case was dismissed for want of prosecution, according to the appearance docket.
