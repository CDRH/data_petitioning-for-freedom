







In the District Court of the 3rd Judicial District of Washington Territory holding
terms at Seattle in King County

In the matter of the application of Jennie Miller Powers for a writ of Habeas Corpus}
Order granting writ

Upon reading and filing the petition of Jennie Miller Powers only signed and verifiied
by her wherein it appears that Mary Alice Powers an infant child of said Jennie Miller
Powers is illegally restrained confined and detained from the rightful custody of
said Jennie Miller Powers in King County Washington Territory and stating wehrein
the alleged illegality consists from which it appears to me that a writ of Habeas
Corpus ought to issue, it is ordered that a writ of Habeas Corpus issue out of and
under the seal of the District Court of the 3rd Judicial District of Washington Territory
directed to the said Mary Elizabeth Miller and to J RJ Miller her husband commanding
then and each of them to have the body of the said Mary Alice Powers before me at
my Chambers in the Court House of said King County on the 10h day of April at 10 oclock
AM of that day to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning
said Mary Alice Powers the minor child of said petitioner Jennie Miller Powers together
with the time and cause of her detention and that

you have then and there the said writ

RAJones
Judge

Dated
April 9th 1888
