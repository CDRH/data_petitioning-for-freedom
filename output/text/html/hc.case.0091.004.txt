







IN JUSTICE'S COURT, SEATTLE PRECINCT, KING COUNTY, W.T.

Before N. Soderberg, Justice of the Peace.

Territory of Washington, Plaintiff, vs. Catharine Gilbert Thomas Gilbert Defendant.
) COMMITMENT.

UNITED STATES OF AMERICA.

Territory of Washington, )
County of King. ) ss.

TO ANY CONSTABLE OF SAID COUNTY, and to the Keeper of the County Jail of said County--Greeting:

WHEREAS, at a Justice's Court held this day at the office of the undersigned, in the
city of Seattle, in said County, before the undersigned, a Justice of the peace of
said County, for the trial of Thomas &amp; Catharine Gilbert for the offense hereinafter
stated, the said Catharine Gilbert was convicted of having on the 28th day of April
1888, in said County committed, the crime of Disposing of beer to an Indian and upon
conviction the said Court did adjudge and determine that said Thomas &amp; Catharine Gilbert
Catharine Gilbert should pay a fine of $100 00 and $  costs, and should be imprisoned
in the County Jail of King County, W.T., for the period of 50 days, or until the said
fine and costs be paid, AND WHEREAS, the said Thomas &amp; Catharine Gilbert has made
default in the payment of the said fine and costs; THEREFORE, you, the said H.G. Thornton
are hereby commanded forthwith to convey and deliver the said Thomas &amp; Catharine Gilbert
to said Keeper, and you the said Keeper are hereby commanded to receive the said Thomas
&amp; Catharine Gilbert into your custody in said Jail and him there keep for the space
of 50 days unelss he shall sooner be thence discharged by due course of law.

Dated at Seattle, W. T. this 22d day of May 1888

N Soderberg

Justice of the Peace, Seattle, King County, W.T.
