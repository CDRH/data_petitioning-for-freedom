Return to

I Wm Billings to whom the writ of Habeas Corpus in the above matter is directed hereby make return there to as follows. The said writ was served on me the 27th day of Aug 1877, and I now return the same to this court, and herewith return the body of the said E.B Fuller in to court, said Fuller is held by me by authority of a commitment Issued by Galen F. James, a Justice of the Peace for said Thurston County, the said commitment is herewith returned in to this Court. The said E.B. Fuller has been confined in the Jail of Thurston County since the 13th day of June 1877.

William Billings Sheriff of Thurston County W.T. & Keeper of the Jail of said County
