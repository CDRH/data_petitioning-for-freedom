  • Summary of Proceedings: In March 1884, the Chehalis County Probate Court sentenced G. M. Savage to jail in Thurston County for unspecified causes. Savage petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, claiming that the probate court lacked jurisdiction. Upon hearing the evidence, Savage was released from custody. Two months later, the complainant, a woman named Lirstina Carney, alleged that the sheriff, William Billings, had illegally charged her for holding Savage. It is unknown if this dispute was resolved.
