County of Saint Louis

The State of Missouri

To James A. Felps Esquire, Jailor of Saint Louis County, Greeting:

You are commanded to have the body of George Bailey before the Honorable Law Commissioner Court of Saint Louis County forthwith together with the time and cause of his detention by you, to do and receive what shall then and there be adjudged concerning the said George Bailey who as alleged in the annexed petition is illegally restrained of his liberty by now and have you then and there this writ with your petitioner thereon

Given under my hand and the seal of said Court at office in Saint Louis this 9th day of August 1856

Matthew Williams Clerk
