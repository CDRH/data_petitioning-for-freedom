IN THE UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT FOR THE WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON, NORTHERN DIVISION.

In the Matter of the Application of MRS. GLADYS FREDRIKA FUSON, for a Writ of Habeas Corpus.}

No. 3451 ORDER

Upon reading and filing the Affidavit and Petition of Gladys Fredrika Fuson, the application for habeas corpus herein, for permission to prosecute said proceding in forma pauperis, and having been duly advised in the premises,

ORDERED that the petition be granted, and that all of the procedings heretofore had may be entered by the Clerk if this Court as in forma pauperis, and that any deposits made by the petitioner in this action be return to her by the Clerk

DONE IN OPEN COURT this 2nd day of November AD 1916

Edward E Cushman
Judge
