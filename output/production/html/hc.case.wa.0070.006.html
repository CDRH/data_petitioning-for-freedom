IN THE JUSTICE COURT, Of Seattle Precinct, of King County, Washington Territory, Before S. B. Vrooman, Justice Peace and Police Justice.

THE CITY OF SEATTLE VS. Ah Min Defendant, CRIMINAL COMPLAINT.

TERRITORY OF WASHINGTON, COUNTY OF KING ss

Personally appeared before me this 10th day of Sept A. D. 1884 Thos Ross who first being duly sworn on his oath says: That on or about the 10th day of Sept 1884 at the City of Seattle, in said King County, W. T., one Ah Sin committed the offense of Playing at Dice as follows, to-wit: Playing a [?] game at Dice for money All of which is contrary to the form, force, and effect of Section 1 of Division 2 of Ordinance No 551 of the said City of Seattle, and against the peace and dignity of the City of Seattle.

Said complainant therefore prays that a warrant may be issued for the arrest of the said Ah Sin and that he may be dealt with according to law and the ordinances of said City.

Thomas Ross Subscribed and sworn to before me this 10th day of Sept A. D. 1884 Sidney B Vrooman Justice of the Peace and Police Justice of the City of Seattle.
