  • Summary of Proceedings: Lucia Martinez filed for a writ of habeas corpus on behalf of her two daughter, Clara A and Johana Martinez who were being held by one of the defendants, Thomas Barman. The hearing was postponed to August 30 1871 so the defendants had time to collect their evidence and witnesses. Before the hearing took place, Martinez approached the judge and requested the dismissal of the case, and both parties agreed to drop the case. By order of Judge Alsop Clara and Johana were to stay in the custody of the sheriff until the August hearing had ended.
