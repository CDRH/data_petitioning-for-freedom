Charge of vagrancy being a habitual 
drunkard. William Gleiss Prosecuting Witness 

Complaint filed Jany 9th, 1885. Warrant 
issued since time to T. J  Robinson Marshall
of Walla Walla City W.T. for service who 
returned the same June 9 having the Deft 
under arrest who was to drunk to have a 
hearing and the matter was postponed 
until she could get sobered up June 11th
1885 Defendant brought into court and 
confessed guilt and plead guilty under the 
court being notified in the matter by her 
own confession and in the testimony of T. J.
Robinson and William Gleiss who were serving 
as witnesses that the Deft was a habitual drunk 
and and it is here now ordered and adjudged by the court the Defendant Mrs Mary Oneil

by refused to give a bond in the sum of 
fifty dollars for good behaior for the term of 
three months and in default thereof that the
