  • Summary of Proceedings: A. B. Burks was being jailed for unspecified reasons. He petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, the writ was allowed and Burks released from custody. Since Burks had submitted his petition to the state district court, however, he had been detained on federal charges. The state district court found that the federal court should have "awaited the determination of this action" before commencing its proceedings, confirming that Burks should be released.
