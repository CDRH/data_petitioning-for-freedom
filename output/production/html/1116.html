  • Summary of Proceedings: Minnite Tarlton, by way of Fred L. Smith, alleged that she was arrested for no reason. When the court responded, they said that there existed no person by the name of Tarlton, but instead there was a Mabel Whitecomb. Whitecomb was arrested for prostitution, vagrancy, and suspicious character by Martin White and Daniel W. Iler. Smith filed for a writ of habeas corpus by which Tarlton, alias Whitecomb, was released from prison.
