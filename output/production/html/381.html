  • Summary of Proceedings: William O'Mara was sent to the St. Louis City Workhouse due to his inability to pay a $100 fine assessed on a misdemeanor conviction. He filed a writ of habeas corpus challenging his detention as well as that of a woman named Margaret O'Mara, which was granted. The petition was ultimately withdrawn--perhaps because, according to workhouse superintendent, William Lingo, Margaret O'Mara was never sent to the workhouse.
