  • Summary of Proceedings: Mrs. Carmen Lynne, by attorney John M. Macfarland filed for habeas on July 20, 1918, against city health commissioner, Ernest C. Manning. The petition charged that Manning had arrested Lynne on Tuesday, July 16th, without warrant or complaint, and had placed her unlawfully in a detention home. The petition was granted, but the writ was denied on July 24th.
