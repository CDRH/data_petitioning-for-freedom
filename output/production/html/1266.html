  • Summary of Proceedings: Percival Tully petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus to retrieve his thirteen-year-old daughter, Alice Tully, from George Fisher, which was granted. Percival accused George of illegally detaining Alice; George responded that Percival had given him custody, which Percival denied. After hearing the evidence, Alice was released into the custody of her father.
