In the District Court at Montesano September Term A.D. 1888

Comes now Lovcey O. Hamilton in answer to the annexed writ of Habeas Corpus and makes return of the same as follows That she holds in her custody the said Ida Tundvall and has so held her since the fifteenth day of July A.D. 1887 under and by virtue of ortects[?] of adoption between said Lovcey O Hamilton and the Orphans House of the Presbyterian Church, located at Portland in the State of Oregon and that she restrains said Ida Lundvall of her liberty in no other manner.

Levicy O. Hamilton

 

FILED

SEP 19 1888

HM Sutton CLERK.

BY [] DEPUTY.
