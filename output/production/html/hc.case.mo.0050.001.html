Law Commissioners' Court

October Term 1854, No. 110

Moore, Rebecca A., In the matter of Habeas Corpus

Arrested under suspicion of being a runaway slave; petitioner was a free person of color; was at one time property of James Wilson of Pinkneyville, Mississippi; Wilson relocated to Cincinnati and sold her to Dr. Noah S. Webb of Pinkneyville; remained his property until his death; emancipated by his will
