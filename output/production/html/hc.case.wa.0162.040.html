In the District Court of the Territory of Washington, and for the Fourth Judicial District thereof, holding terms at Cheney Spokane Falls, Spokane County, in said Territory.

To the Sheriff of Spokane County Greeting:

In the Name of the United States, You are hereby commanded to summon J. Donaldson 30 miles to be and appear before the 4th Jud. Dist Court W.T. at the Court House, in Cheney Spokane Falls in said County, on the Instate day of December A.D. 1886 at o'clock M. to there give evidence in the above entitled cause on the part of said Plaintiff.

Witness the Hon. George Turner, Associate Justice of the Supreme Court of Washington Territory, and Judge of said Court, and the Seal thereof affixed this 2 day of Dec. A.D. 1886.

D.A. Clement Clerk

By Chas B. Johnston Deputy.
