  • Summary of Proceedings: Dolly petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus to secure her release from John Young, which was granted. Dolly accused John of assaulting and kidnapping her in St. Louis and then removing her to the Northwest Territory where he enslaved her for six months. Young pleaded not guilty and paid a recognizance agreeing that he would not remove Dolly from the court's jurisdiction. Her fate is unknown.
