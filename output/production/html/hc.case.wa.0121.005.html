In the District Court of the Territory of Washington and for the First Judicial District thereof holding terms at Walla Walla Dayton, for the county of Walla Walla and Franklin Columbia in said Territory

In the matter of the application of Jacob Schuman for a writ of Habeas Corpus}

In the name of the United States of American Greeting:

To J.H. Hosler Sheriff of Columbia County Washington Territory,

You are hereby commanded to have the body of Jacob Schuman by you imprisoned and detained, as it is said, together with the time and cause of such detention and imprisonment before the Hon Tom G. Langford Judge of the above court and associate Justice of the Supreme Court of said Territory at Drakes Hall in the County Court House at at Dayton; County and Territory aforesaid at the hour of 10 o'clock A.M. of the 31st day of August 1886 there to do   and receive what shall be ordered concerning said Jacob Schuman and have then and there this writ.

Dated this 25th day of August 1886

Tom G. Langford Judge of the above named District Court and associate Justice of the Supreme Court of Washington Territory
