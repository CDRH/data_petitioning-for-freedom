Am sheriff of Spokane County met her first saw defendant last Wednesday in Cal Turners
office - has been in my custody since- she delivered herself up to me in Cal Turners
office  she did not state say anything to me when I took her into custody

In consequence of a conversation between myself and Cal Turner - I went to his (Turner)
office with him and I took her Mary Teller into my custody to answer to the charge
of killing Frank Teller she was in my persona charge say 1 1/2 hours - she did not
say anything to me about this killing while my charge   I know Dr Waterhouse and Mr.
Stoltz   Know where the defendant was only from  day when defent reside know of the
