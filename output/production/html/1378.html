  • Summary of Proceedings: On November 10th, Governor McKelvie of Nebraska issued an arrest warrant for James Cummings at the request of the Governor of Minnesota due to allegations of Cummings being a fugitive from justice. Cummings was then places into the custody of the Sheriff of Yellow Medicine County, Minnesota, G. O. Homme. Cummings and his attorney then filed a writ of Habeas Corpus stating that it was granted without due process of law. The petition was heard but eventually denied on November 12th, 1919.
