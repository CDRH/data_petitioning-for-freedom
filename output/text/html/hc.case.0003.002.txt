







Territory of Washington
County of Walla Walla

To E. Delaney
Marshall of Walla Walla City

Whereas an application has been made pursuant to Statue to the Hon J. E. Wyche Judge
of the U.S. District Court 1st Judicial District of said Territory and an order granted
for a Writ of Habeas Corpus for the body of Ah Chon Chinaman who it is alledged is
unlawfully restrained of his liberty

Therfore in the name of the United States you are Commanded to bring the body of the
said Ah Chon Chinaman with the cause of his detentions forthwith before the Hon J.
E. Wyche Judge at Chambers in the City of Walla Walla County and Territory aforesaid
to do and receive what shall be ordered concerning him and have then &amp; there this
writ

Witness the Hon J.E. Wyche Judge aforesaid and the Seal of the Court this the 2nd
day of April 1869
BJ Wyche
Clerk
