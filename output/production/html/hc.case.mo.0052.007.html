The State of Missouri

To the Keeper of the common Jail of the County of St Louis Greeting

You are hereby commanded to receive the body of the above named Adam Riley into your common jail, and detain him in custody until he is called for by his master and all reasonable charges paid or until he be therein discharged in due course of Law

Given under my hand this 4th day of July 1853

Peter W Johnstone
