State of Missouri County of St. Louis } ss "By the Habeas Corpus Act".

The State of Missouri to Charles G. Ward.

You are hereby commanded that the body of Martha Edwards, under your custody detained, as it is said, under safe and secure conduct, together with the day and cause of her being detained by you, and by whatsoever name the said Martha Edwards may be known, you have before me Henry Dusenbury, Law Commissioner of St. Louis County, at the Courthouse in the City of St. Louis in said County, this, the twenty fourth day of August 1858, at 3 o'clock P.M. to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning the said Martha Edwards, so detained as aforesaid and here of fail not at your peril.

Witness my signature this 24th day of August AD 1858.

H. Dusenbury

Law Commissioner
