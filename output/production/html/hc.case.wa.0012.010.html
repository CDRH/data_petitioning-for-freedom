In the Probate Court of Whitman County Washington Territory before WN Ruby Probate Judge

In the matter of Amos Gustin and Catherine Gustin adopting Daisy Geneva Departte minor child of James G Departte and Elizabeth Dapartte

The above named Amos Gustin and Catherine Gustin allege that they are inhabitants of Washington Territory and now residing in Colfax Whitman County Washington Territory that during all the time hereafter mentioned they were and now are Husband and wife, that they are the owners of Two Hundred Acres of land in Nez Perce County Idaho Territory worth three thousand dollars that they have personal property worth two thousand dollars and that they and each ask leave to adopt Daisy Geneva Departte a minor child not theirs by birth and for a change of the name of said child that the present name of said child is Daisy Geneva Departte that she was born February 9th 1885 that on March 1st 1885 the mother of said child to wit Elizabeth Departte became insane and it became necessary to remove her to an insane asylum at which time the father of said child to wit James Departte placed said child   then three weeks old in the care and custody of your petitioners where she has since that time remained That four months latter to wit July 1st 1885 said Elizabeth Departte was cured of said insanity and your petitioners and each of them at that time requested said James G Departte and Elizabeth Departte and each of them to take said child in their custody and maintain and support her- that they and each of them refused to take said child and refused to support her or in anyways provide for her and then and there abandoned said child and left her with your petitioners and moved to Utah Territory and have not since said time furnished said child any support or paid or agreed to pay for her support or in any way to provide for her that said James Departte and Elizabeth Departte have no property and that said Elizabeth Departte is partially insane and for a part of the last two years has been in an insane ansylum for treatment and that said James G Departte is now residing in Utah Territory and are unfit persons to have the custody of said child that your petitioners and each of them ask leave to adopt said child and have her named changed to Daisy Geneva Gustin

Wherefore your petitioners Amos Gustin and Catherine Gustin and each of them ask an order delcaring that from this date   to January 24th 1888 said child Daisy Geneva Departte to all legal issues and purposes be the child of your petitioners and that the name of said child be changed from Daisy Geneva Departe to Daisy Geneva Gustin and for such other relief as may seen seen just

Amos Gustin

Catherine Gustin
