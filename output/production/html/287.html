  • Summary of Proceedings: George F. Moore was detained for the alleged crime of being a fugitive from justice in Nebraska, the alleged crime was accepting a bribe as a public officer of the State of Wyoming in Newcastle on August 1st of 1921. In her petition on his behalf, Winifred Moore stated that he was not physical present in Wyoming and could not have been guilty as claimed. Judge A. C. Troupe granted the petition, but the outcome of the proceeding is unknown.
