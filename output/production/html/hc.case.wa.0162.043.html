Territory of Washington County of Spokane} ss

I, Thomas C. Griffith, being first duly sworn on my oath state: I am one of the attorneys of Ray Raymond and am a citizen of the United States of lawful age. I make this application for a writ of Habeas Corpus in her behalf and at her request; I have read the foregoing petition for a writ of Habeas Corpus know the contents thereof and I believe said Petition to be true.

Thomas C. Griffith

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 5th day of July 1886.

George Turner Notary Public Dist. Judge Honorable Justice of Wash. Terr.

 

In the matter of the application of Ray Raymond for a Writ of Habeas Corpus Petition

Filed July 6th 1886 D.A. Clement Clerk
