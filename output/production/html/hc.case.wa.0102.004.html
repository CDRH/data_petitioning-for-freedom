Sheriffs Office}

County of Pierce}SS

I do hereby return to the honorable Judge of the District Court for Pierce County, that before the coming to me of the within writ, the said Mary Arlington was committed to my custody, and is detained by virtue of a commitment a copy of which is hereto annexed the original of which I also nevertheless; I have the body of the said Mary Arlington before you at the time and place within mentioned as I am within commanded

Lewis Byrd

Sheriff Pierce County W.T.

Dated Tacoma W.T. Jan 28h 1885}
