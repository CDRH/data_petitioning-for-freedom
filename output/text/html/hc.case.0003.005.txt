







In the matter of the petition of Ah Chon a Chinaman for a writ of Habeas Corpus

And now upon command  of the complaint filed herein it is Ordered that the writ  be
issued to E Delany as Marshall of Walla Walla City requiring him to present the body
of the said Ah Chon before me at the Court House at 2PM on the 2 day of April 1869
and that the said writ be delivered to the sheriff &amp; be   according to law

Walla Walla W.T.
April 2 1869
JE Wyche
Judge
