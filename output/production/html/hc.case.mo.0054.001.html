No 141

June Term 1853

In the matter of George W Turley upon a writ of Habeas Corpus

"By the Habeas Corpus Act"

Issd June 18th 1853

Relblo[?] forthwith

Main in fee Book cost fee 73

June 18th 1853 Discharged Blo2 R1757

The State of Missouri

To James Castello, Jailor of the County of St Louis Greeting

You are commanded to have the body of George W Turley before the Law Commissioners Court of St Louis County on the 11 day of June AD 1853 at 3 o'clock PM forthwith together with the time and cause of his detention by you to do and receive what shall then and there be adjudged concerning the said George W Turley who as alleged in the petition hereto annexed is restrained of his liberty by you. And have you then and there this writ with your return thereon.

Given under my and the seal of the Law Commissioners Court of the County of St Louis this 18th day of June 1853

John W Colvin Law Com

Retd forthwith to day
