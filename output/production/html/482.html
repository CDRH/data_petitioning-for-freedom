  • Summary of Proceedings: Irwin A. Medlar claimed that he was unlawfully detained by John F. Boyd and Patrick Horigan. He was charged with being a fugitive from justice after being arrested in Omaha. It was said that Medlar had been charged with a "crime of seduction" in Harvey County, Kansas, but he claimed that he was only there for work and did not commit any crime. The judge determined that he was in fact unlawfully detained and was released from custody.
