The State of Missouri

To John Hempstead Jailor of St Louis County

You are hereby commanded to bring before the one John W Colvin County Law Commissioners of St Louis the Body of Rebecca Ann Moore a free woman of color who is illegally detained by you as it is said together with the day and cause of her detention and herein fail out under the penalty that may fall thereon

Witness my hand and seal this 24th October AD 1854

Jno W, Colvin Law Com
