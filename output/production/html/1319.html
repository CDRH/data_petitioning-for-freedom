  • Summary of Proceedings: The husband of Emma E. Howard, Clark W. Howard absented himself and deserted her, and they remained separate. She was took care of their child, Lillian Violet, but without her consent, Clark stole the child. Emma claimed that he had vicious habits and not a suitable person to care for the child. Then he placed the child in the care of Julia Moore, Mother of Mary Michael, mother superior of St. James Orphanage in Benson in Omaha. Emma proved her character and ability to take care of her daughter, and Lillian was released from custody.
