In the District Court for the Third Judicial District Court holding terms at Seattle Washington Territory

In the matter of the application of Sarah McDunnoh for discharge from imprisonment} No 6006

The return made to the writ of Habeas Corpus issued herein and the application of said Sarah McDunnoh for discharge from imprisonment having been duly heard and considered.

It is now and here ordered that said application be denied and said Sarah McDunnoh be & is hereby remanded to the custody of the Chief of Police who is also Keeper of the City Jail of the City of Seattle King County Washington Territory as such Keeper of said Jail

RA Jones

Judge

Dated May 26, 1888
