  • Summary of Proceedings: Leroy Craig petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus claiming that he was being illegally detained by James L. Greene. Although not mentioned in case files, Greene was the Superintendent of the Nebraska Hospital for the Insane. Judge Frost granted the petition. Greene submitted a demurrer to the Court, which was overruled. Frost allowed the writ and ordered that Craig be released from custody.
