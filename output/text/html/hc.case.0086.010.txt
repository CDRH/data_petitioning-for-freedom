







Territory of Washington
3d Judicial District} SS

In the District Court holding terms at Seattle in King County.

In the matter of the application of Jennie Miller Powers for  Writ of Habeas Corpus
vs.
Mary Elizabeth Miller and R.J. Miller her husband
} No. 5902

This matter having been adjorned from time to time, Now on now on this 12th day of
May, 1888, the same comes on to be heard upon the allegations and proofs of the parties
and the arguments of counsel, J.M. Hall, Eben Smith and J.T. Littell, Esquires appearing
for the applicant and Jennie Miller Powers and Messrs Lewis &amp; Gilman appearing for
the respondents Mary Elizabeth Miller and R.J. Miller, her husband, and the Court
being freely advised in the premises it is now by the court held considered and adjudged
that the infant Mary Alice Powers be delivered into the care and custody of her mother,
towit: the said Jennie Miller Powers and that said Jennie Miller Powers hereafter
have the exclusive care, control and custody of the said infant the said Mary Alice
Powers. And now the said


Mary Alice Powers is in open Court delievered into the custody of the said Jennie
Miller Powers. And it is now further considered and adjudged by the Court now here
that the applicant herein the said Jennie Miller Powers do have and recover of and
from the respondents herein the said Mary Elizabeth Miller and herR.J. Miller her
husband or either of them the costs and disbursements of this proceeding taxed at
fifty four dollars and ten cents and that Execution issue therefor.
