The State of Missouri

To the Keeper of the Common Jail of St Louis County Greeting:

Whereas complaint has been made before me that one Henry Freeman a Negro is a Runaway Slave as set forth in the complaint of Alfred B. Holton. There are therefore to command you to receive the body of the said Henry Freeman in to your common Jail and him safely keep until he be taken hence by his master and all reasonable charges paid, or until he be otherwise discharged according to Law.

Given under my hand this 20th day of July 1853

Peter W Johnston

Justice
