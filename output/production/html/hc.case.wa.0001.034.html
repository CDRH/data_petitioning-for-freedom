Territory of Washington County of Spokane } SS

In District Court of 4th Judicial District Holding Terms at Cheney

Mary E Teller plaintiff vs Franklin Teller defendant

Territory of Washington County of Spokane } SS

Mary E Teller herself first duly sworn on her oath states that she is the plaintiff in the above entitled cause that she has read the complaint in the above cause and knows the property therein mentioned to belong to defendant: that she is informed and believes that defendant is about to dispose of said personal property and to sell the improvements on said land and relinquish his homestead entry thereon so as to enable the purchase of said improvements to file on the said: and that she believes that said defendant will do so unless restrained therefore by the order of this court

Mary E Teller

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 7th day of August 1886

Frank Graves

Notary Public

Wash Ter

 

Mary E Teller vs Franklin Teller affadavit

Filed August 10th 1886

DA Clement

Clerk
