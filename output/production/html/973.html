  • Summary of Proceedings: Estella Lyell of Eagle, Nebraska petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus to retrieve Samuel Prey, her nephew, from the custody of Martha Prey, his grandmother, which was granted. She claimed that Martha was unable to care for Samuel due to her advanced age. The custody of the boy was in question due to his parents' divorce. After hearing the evidence, the writ was denied and Samuel remanded to the custody of Martha. However, the 1920 census shows a now 18-year-old Samuel living with his mother, Nellie, in Eagle, Nebraska.
