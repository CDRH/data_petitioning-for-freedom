  • Summary of Proceedings: The petitioner, John W Harding, was charged with accessory to murder Peter Gallager. The petitioner filed for habeas corpus on the basis that he was more than 20 miles away from the place where Gallager was killed. However, when he came to court to testify, he was arrested for withholding knowledge about Gallager’s murder and refusing to pay bail at his appearance. The petition and writ were granted on the basis that there was not sufficient grounds for confinement, and the petitioner was released from custody. The county was required to pay the $132.45 in court fees.
