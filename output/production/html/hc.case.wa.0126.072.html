Territory of Washington County of Lincoln } ss

To Frank J. Beardon, Henry Beardon, O.J. Rogers, R.C. Welch, H. Portch, John Jump, E. A. Portch.

In the name of the United States you are hereby required to appear before the undersigned one of the Justice of the Peace Acting Coroner in and for the said county on the 25th day of December A.D. 1884 at 11 o'clock AM at the house of A.L. Beardon to give evidence in the cause of death of A.L. Beardon.

I give under my hand and seal at this 24th day of December A.D. 1884
J.W. Carpenter
Justice of the Peace
Acting Coroner

 

Subpoena for witness in Coroners inquest served the within John Jump could not be found served on the rest.

J Friedlander
Deputy Sheriff
Lincoln Co W.T.
Dec 24 84

Filed March 5th/85
Kate W. Feuerbach
Clerk
