In the District Court of the 3rd Judicial District in and for King County Territory of Washington.

In the matter of Frank OBrian Petition for Writ of Habeas Corpus}

Order granting writ

On reading and filing the petition of Frank OBrien duly signed and verified by him hereby it appears that he is illegally restrained and imprisoned in the County Jail of said County and stating wherein the illegality consists from which it appears to me that a writ of Habeas Corpus ought to issue

It is ordered that a writ of Habeas Corpus issue out of and under the seal of the District Court of the 3rd Judicial District in and for the County of King directed to said Sherif Cochrane commanding him to have the body of said OBrien before me in the Court room of said Court on the 2nd day of March 1889 at 7-30 oclock of that day to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning the said OBrien together with the time and cause of his detention and that he have then and there the said writ

Dated on the 2nd day of March AD 1889
