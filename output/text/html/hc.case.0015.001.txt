St. Louis Circuit Court
Case Files - Civil
February Term 1868, No. 8456

Bauer, Caroline, in the matter of Habeas Corpus

Caroline Bauer by her attorney petitioned court to inquire into her imprisonment &amp;
sanity; her husband John James Bauer had her committed to St. Vincent's Institution
for the insane located on 9th street; sister Mary Julia Superioress of institution;
divorce case between Caroline &amp; John pending also spelled Jean Jacques Bauer; doctor
certificates of insanity included

Plaintiff Attorney: Bush, William; Gottschalk, Louis
