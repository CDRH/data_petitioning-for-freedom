In the District Court of the Second Judicial District of Washington Territory holding Terms at Montesano for Chehalis County, W.T.

Territory of Washington } #135 vs. Gus Bendjus Robert Strangland} Indictment Ben Fredrickson, Pete Peterson, Frank Nelson, Fred Strongburger, John Blending Andrew Johnson, Leander Brown John Saxon, John Thompson Alex Agren Thomas Jacobs. E Emel. Andrew Anderson and Charles Burke

Defendants Gus Beudjus, Robert Stranglaud, Ben Fredrickson, Pete Peterson, Frank Nelson, Fred Strongburger, John Blending, Andrew Johnson, Leander Brown, John Saxon, John Tompson, Alex Agren, Thomas Jacobs, E Emil Andrew Anderson and Charles Burke are accused by the grand jury of Chehalis County Washington Territory by this indictment of the crime of Malicious Mischief committed as follows, to wit:

the said, Gus Bendjus, Robert Stronglaud, Ben Fredrickson, Pete Peterson, Frank Nelson, Fred Strongburger, John Blending, Andrew Johnson, Leander Brown, John Saxon, John Thompson, Alex Agren

II II   Thomas Jacobs, E Emil, Andrew Anderson and Charles Burke on the 9th day of September A.D. 1889, and in the right term, in the said County of Chehalis in the said Second Judicial District, in the Territory of Washington, then and there being one Pound net of the value of $650.00, the property of one Wilson Graham, then & there being and situate in the said County and Territory, thereby maliciously intending to injure and destroy such Pound net did willfully, maliciously, unlawfully, feloneously and with malace towards and against said Wilson Graham cut, tear, pull down injure and destroy to the great damage of the said Wilson Graham

Contrary to the statute in such case made and provided and against the peace & dignity of the Territory of Washington

George J. Moody
Prosecuting Attorney
Second Judicial District.
Washington Territory
