1236

Territory of Washington}

County of Pierce} SS.

To the Seriff or any Constable of said County-

Whereas John Morris has this day complained in writing under oath to the undersigned one of the Justices of the Peace in and for said County. That on the night of the 15th day of August A.D. 1884 at the City of Tacoma in said County

The three following named Chinamen Sam Lock & Jim were guilty of smoking and inhaling opium.

Therefore in the name of the United States You are commanded to apprehend forthwith the same Sam Lock & Jim and being then before me to be delt with according to law.

Given under my hand this 16th day of August AD 1884

WH Harris

Justice of the Peace
