State of Missouri County of St Louis } SS

"By the Habeas Corpus Act."

The State of Missouri

To the Jailor of St. Louis County _ Greeting:

You are hereby commanded to have the body of James Allison Parker, alledged to be unlawfully detained by you, and imprisoned in the Jail of said County, together with the cause of such imprisonment and detention, before the undersigned at the Law Commissioners Court Room in the City of St Louis in said County State, tomorrow the 30th day of September 1858 at 2 o'clock P.M. to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning the said James Allison Parker, the person so imprisoned and detained and have you then and there this writ.

Witness my hand this 29th day of September AD 1858

H Dusenbury

Law Commissioner
