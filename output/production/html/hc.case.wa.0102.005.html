Territory of Washington}

County of Pierce} SS

M. Brotton being duly sworn on his oath says that Mary Arlington and Tim Johnson at the town of Tacoma in the said County of Pierce Territory of Washington before the filing of this complaint, visited a house used by one Harry Arlington as once for the purpose of a resort for the purpose of smoking opium by other persons and that then and there being the same defendants Mary Arlington, and Tim Johnson did smoke and inhale opium contrary to the form of the statute in such case made and from did and against the peace in dignity of the Territory of Washington and complaint prays that a warrant of arrest may be issue for said defendant and that they be dealt with according to Law

M. Brotton

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 23rd day of January A.D. 1885

A. E. Lawrence

Justice of the Peace
