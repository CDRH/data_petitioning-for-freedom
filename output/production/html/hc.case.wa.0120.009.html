In the matter of the
application of
L.T. Savacool
for writ of habeas
Corpus

Now comes the above named L.T Savacool, applicant & plaintiff and excepts to the sufficiency of the[?] return of J.H. Hosler Sheriff of Columbia County on the grounds the said Commitment marked exhibit 'B' and a part of said return, does not purport to have been issued by J.H. Hosler the keeper of the Common jail of Columbia County such authorize or empowered him to said prison the said plaintiff now does said Commitment authorize this keeper or the Common jail of Garfield County deliver or turn over the said plaintiff of the said J.H. Hosler

As to the order purported to have been made by S.C. Wingard, and which is made apart J.H. Hosler return, the said plaintiff and applicant denies this he has any knowledge or information sufficent from a belief as to whether any such was ever made or not.

Plaintiff and applicant alleges that the said Justice of the Peace W.S. Newland had no legal or probable cause, to Commit him to answer the charge of forgery or any other crime or offense   And the evidence before said Justice of the Peace was wholly insufficient, and did not show or tend to show the said plaintiff had committed the crime of forging or any other crime or offense wherefrom plaintiff says as in his petition herein.

L.T Savacool

Territory of Washington
County of Columbia } SS

L.T. Savacool hereby being duly sworn says that he is the plaintiff and applicant named in the foregoing answer by J.H. Hosler return that he knows the contents of the foregoing answer and believes it to by him

L.T. Savacool

Subscribed & sworn to before me this 23rd day of February 1886

JW Jessel
Clerk
