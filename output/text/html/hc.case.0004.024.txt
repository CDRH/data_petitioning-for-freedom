







Territory of Washington
County of Walla Walla 

In the District of the 1st Judicial District 

Charles Gahres &amp; Martha J Garhes Plffs
vs
W H Barrett Isabella Barrett Defts

The Defendants for answer to Plaintiffs complaint ans 1st that on the 12th day July
1871 at the city of Walla Walla in an action brought in this court herein Charles
Gahres &amp; Martha J Gahres was Plaintiffs and W.H. Barrett &amp; Isabella Sutton now Isabella
Barrett Defendants herein was defendants in said action. For the same cause of action
as that alledged in this cause and said defendants renounce Judgement against said
Plaintiffs in said cause duly given upon the merits and these defendants was by the
said Judgement awarded the care and custody of the 



said child Etta Dora Cravens and that the said Plaintiffs pay the cost of said action.
Wherefore Defendants pray Judgement that Plaintiffs proceedings be dismissed and that
they have and recover there costs in this proceedings 
W.A. Gorge &amp; B. L. Sharpstein Attys for Defts
