  • Summary of Proceedings: Katie, Dora, and Lillie Sitzman were being detained by the State Board of Control at the Institution for Feeble Minded Youth for unspecified reasons. Their father, Charles Sitzman, petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus to retrieve them, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, the children were remanded to the custody of the respondents and their father ordered to pay the court costs.
