  • Summary of Proceedings: The chief of police ordered the sheriff and constable of King County to arrest Charles Lind after two saloon keepers alleged that Charles was indebted to them. Charles was apprehended by sheriff John F. Sheenan and constable H. G. Thornton. On August 24, 1885, Charles petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus and it was delivered to the respondents the same day. Charles’ fate is unknown.
