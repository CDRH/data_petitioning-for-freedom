  • Summary of Proceedings: Notary Public J. S. Bishop subpoenaed Louis M. Cohn. When Cohn refused to provide information, Bishop charged him with contempt of court and ordered that he be jailed. Cohn petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus claiming that Sheriff Samuel McClay was depriving him of his liberty. Judge Hall granted the petition. After hearing evidence, Hall remanded Cohn to the custody of McClay. Case files for hc.case.ne.0551 reveal that Cohn appealed this decision to the Supreme Court.
