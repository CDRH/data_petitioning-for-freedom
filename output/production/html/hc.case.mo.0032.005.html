In obedience to the command of the coms foregoin writ I have here at open c court the Body of Joseph Adams an for my return say that he was commed[?] to my custoday on the 11th day of May 1854 on Oath made by George Borge & Beck as a runaway slave committed by Justice Johnston paper at the Sherriff office

James Castello

Jailor St. Louis Coy

No 158

June Term 1854

Habeas Corpus in the matter of Joseph Adams "By the Habeas Corpus act"

Issd June 17th 1854

Petition fee book $1.85

June 17th 1854 Discharged 2h 608
