In the District Court holding terms at Olympia.

In the matter of the application of William Jones William H. Doyle, et al. for a writ of habeas corpus

To John B. Allen, U.S. Pros. Att'y and to C. H. Hanford, assistant U.S. Pros. Att'y for Washington Territory.

You and each of you will please take notice that a Writ of habeas corpus has issued to the U.S. Marshall Jesse W. George in charge of the U.S. Penitentiary McNeils Island, Pierce County, W.T. commanding him to bring the bodies-and each of them-of the above named parties before the Hon. John P. Hoyt, associate Justice of the Supreme Court and Judge of the 2nd. Judicial district of W.T. that they and each of them may be discharged and restored to their liberty.

And you will please appear and then show cause to the contrary if you can.

Said writ is made returnable at ---o, clock, on the ----- day of March 1885. at the court room at Olympia, W.T.

Olympia W.T. March, --- 1885.

Attorneys for petitioner

Invoice[?] by copy admitted this -- day of March 1885

Assistant U.S. Attorney for Washington Tery

 

US District Court Olympia
