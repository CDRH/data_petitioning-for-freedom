St. Louis Law Commissioners' Court

April Term 1855, No. 91

Blackburn, William,

In the matter of

Habeas Corpus

Defendant arrested as a fugitive slave; petition states that defendant "says he is a free man, but has no evidence of his freedom"; witness, Sarah Broadwater, testified defendant is a free man and that he is her son-in-law; other witness was captain of steamboat Michigan and that the defendant worked on the boat as a cabin boy and was a free man
