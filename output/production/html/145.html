  • Summary of Proceedings: William E. Conely was arrested by John Briggs on December 4. On December 11, Ethel Conely, wife to Conely, with attorney William R. Patrick, filed Habeas against John Briggs, William P. Corrigan, and Michael Cochoran. She charged that they had no order of commitment to hold Conely. Arrest was said to have followed a complaint made by Gilbert Bolen and son, former employer of Conley, who claimed that Conley had beaten Gilbert Bolen unconscious and robbed him of his watch and money. A writ was issued and sustained, and Conley was discharged from custody December 12.
