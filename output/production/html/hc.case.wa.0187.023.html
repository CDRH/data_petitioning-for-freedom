UNITED STATES OF AMERICA. DISTRICT COURT FOR THE WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON, NORTHERN DIVISION.

In the matter of the petition of Marie Robina Grayson, for a Writ of Habeas Corpus. 2780

N. G.

The petitioner above named having heretofore at the date of the issuance of the Writ of Habeas Corpus herein, filed her bond for her appearance herein, in the penal sum of $2000.00, with J. L. Lyen, and B. P. Castner, as sureties, which bond has heretofore been approved and is now on file, and thereafter hearing having been had in said matter, and the petitioner discharged, in accordance with her prayer upon said Writ of Habeas Corpus, and thereupon the United States having appealed from such Order of Discharge and Judgment, to the United States Circuit Court of Appeals for the Ninth Judicial Circuit, and the petitioner having in accordance with the Order of said Court, allowing such appeal filed her bond for her appearance to abide the Judgment of the said United States Circuit Court of Appeals, for the Ninth Judicial Circuit, with sureties satisfactory to the Clerk of this Court, as provided in such Order allowing such appeal

NOW THEREFORE the said bond of the said petitioner heretofore filed in said cause in the said penal sum of $2000.00, with the said J. L. Lyen & B. P. Castner as sureties having been fully satisfied the same is hereby discharged, and the sureties thereon exonerated from any liability thereon or thereunder.

Done in open Court this 25th day of August, 1914.

Jeremiah Neterer
U. S. District Judge.
