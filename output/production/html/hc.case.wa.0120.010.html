In this District Court of Columbia County Washington Territory, holding terms at Dayton

Before William G. Langford Judge of this where Court and associate Justices of the Supreme Court of the Territory Washington

Territory Washington County of Columbia } SS

The United States of America

To J.H. Hosler Greeting: We command you, that you have the body of L.T. Savacool, by you imprisoned and detained, as in is said, to this with the time and course of such imprisonment and detention by whatsoever name said L.T. Savacool shall be called or charged before William G. Langford Judge of the District of Columbia County, Washington Territory, holding terms at Dayton, of this Chambers in the clerks office in Dayton Columbia County Washington Territory on the 23rd day of February 1886 on the hour of 9 O'clock A.M. of said day, to do and receive what shall there and then he Considered Concerning the said L.T. Savacool and have you there and then this writ.

Witness the Hon. William G. Langford Justice of the District Court of Columbia   County, holding terms at Dayton and Associate Justice of the Subpoena Court of said Territory of Washington this 19th day of February 1886.

Attest my hand and the seal of said District Court affixed this the day and year above written

JW Jessel
Clerk District Court

Filed Feb 23 1886
JW Jessel
Clerk
