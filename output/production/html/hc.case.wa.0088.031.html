MAMIE MCKAY, called and questioned.

Q. [Ronald] Where do you live? A. Old Tacoma.

Q. What is your name? A. Nellie McKay.

Q. Where is your mama? A. She is sitting down over there.

Q. Do you live with your mama? A. Yes sir.

Q. How does your mama treat you? A. She is kind.

Q. Who buys your clothing for you? A. Mama.

Q. Does your mama abuse you? A. Yes, she never licks me though.

Q. She scolds you does she? A. No.

Q. Does she talk cross to you or does she talk kindly? A. She talks kind.

Ronald: We rest.
