In the matter of the application of Edward B Fuller for writ of Habeas Corpus.
Writ

To William Billings, greeting.

In the name of the United States of America, we command you that you have the body of Edward B Fuller, by you imprisoned and detained as it is said together, with the time, and cause of such imprisonment and detention by what ever name said Edward B Fuller shall be called or charged before Honorable Roger S Greene Judge of the above Court, in the office of the Clerk of said Court, in Olympia in said County on the 26th 27th day of August, 1877 at two oclock pm of that day, to do and receive what   shall then, and there be considered concerning the said Edward B Fuller, and have you then and there this writ.

Witness the Honorable Roger S Greene Judge of the said District Court at Olympia this in said County this 27 day of August 1877.
