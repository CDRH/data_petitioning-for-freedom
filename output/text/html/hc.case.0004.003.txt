







Territory of Washington
County of Walla Walla
In the District court of the first Judicial District

Charles Gahres and Martha J. Gahres Plffs
v.
W.H. Barret and Isabella Sutton Deft

Whereas the Defendants in the above Entitled action has appealed to the Supreme Court
of the Territory of Washington from an order entered against them in said District
court on the 12th day of July 1871 awarding the custody of a minor child of Plantiff
named Ella Dora Cravens to Defts and also from a judgement entered at some time to
wit July 12th 1871 by said District Court in said action for sum of Sixty five dollars
as cost of said suit and every part thereof of said order and judgement Now therefore
in consideration of the presence and of such



appeal we the undersigned  Charles R Gahres of Broken City Oregon as principal and
Wm Kahlhauff of the City and country of Walla Walla Territory of Washington or  on
behalf of the appellants do hereby jointly and severally undertake  and promise that
the said appellant will pay all damages and costs  which may be awarded against them
on the appeal not exceeding one hundred and thirty dollars to which amount we ackowledge
ourselves jointly and severally bound  In  whereof we have hereinto   and seal this
13th day of July 1871

Charles R Gahres
Wm Kahlhauff
