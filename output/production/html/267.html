  • Summary of Proceedings: Arrested and jailed on an unknown charge, Frank Skipton petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus claiming Sheriff Trompen was depriving him of his liberty. Judge Frost granted the petition. Skipton later amended his petition, which Judge Frost also accepted. After hearing evidence, Judge Frost denied the writ and ordered that Skipton be remanded into custody. Skipton then filed an application to be admitted to bail, which Frost granted and fixed at 500 dollars. It is unknown whether Skipton followed through and posted bail.
