  • Summary of Proceedings: Frank McCartney was being detained at the Nebraska State Hospital for the Insane. He petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, the writ was allowed and McCartney released from custody. The hospital superintendent appealed to the state supreme court, which overturned the decision of the lower court. Consequently, the writ was denied and McCartney remanded to the custody of the hospital superintendent.
