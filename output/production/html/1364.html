  • Summary of Proceedings: Arrested on the charge of horse stealing, Henry Smith first petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus claiming that Sheriff Trompen was unlawfully jailing him. Later, Smith amended the petition to name the sheriff of Nemaha County, J. F. Cole, as an additional respondent. Judge Cornish granted the petition. After hearing evidence, Judge Holmes allowed the writ and order that Smith be released from custody.
