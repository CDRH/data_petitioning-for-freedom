SUBPOENA

Territory of Washington
County of Spokane} ss

TO Jimmy Barnes F. Palmtag, Frankie Sutsinger Zoe Howard. In the name of the United States you are hereby required to appear before the undersigned, one of the Justices of the Peace in and for the said County, on the 26 day of June 1886, at 10 o'clock A.M. at his office in Spokane Falls W.T. to give evidence in a certain cause then and there to be tried between the Territory of Washington Plaintiff and Ray Raymond Defendant

On the part of the Defendant

Given under my hand this 28th day of June 1886

J.J.L. Peel
Justice of the Peace

 

Territory of Washington

S. B. Leininger
Constable
