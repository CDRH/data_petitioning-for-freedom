Francis N Lisle sworn says-

I reside in this city - I know the prisoner Edwards eleven years ago - then a boy of about 17 years old - was engaged in the employment of the U States government - he was then and there reputed as a free boy - never heard him to be claimed as a slave - He was not then the servant of anybody

Prisoner Discharged

Heard Augt 14th 1858 & discharged
