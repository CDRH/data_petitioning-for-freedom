  • Summary of Proceedings: A.C. Hallam, agent for the U.S. under the Secretary of Navy and manager and operator of the Puget Sound Navy Yard Hotel at the time, maintained a dance hall without paying for a city license of $150.00 per year. Hallam was arrested by Chief of Police Watrous and petitioned claiming that the police had no jurisdiction and the hotel is a part of the U.S. government property, making it not subject to taxation. Petition was discharged.
