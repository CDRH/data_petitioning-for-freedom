In the matter of the application} of Mariah Kristina Peterson} for a writ of Habeas Corpus } Order for writ

On reading and filing the petition of Maria Kristina Peterson, duly signed and verified by her, whereby it appears that her minor child Hilma Lundvall is illegally detained and restrained of her liberty by William Hume and Levicy Hume at their residence in Chehalis County Washington Territory, and stating wherein the illegality consists from which it appears to me that a writ of Habeas Corpus ought to issue.

It is ordered that a writ of habeas corpus issue out of and under the seal of the District Court of the Second Judicial District of the Territory of Washington, in and for Chehalis County, directed to the said William Hume and Levicy Hume his wife commanding them to have the body of the said Hilma Lundvall before me in the court room of said Court forthwith

to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning the said Hilma Lundvall, together with the time and cause of her detention, and that they have then and there this said writ.

Frank Allyn J

Dated Sept 17 1888

No 187

In the matter of the application of Maria Kristina Peterson for writs of Habeas Corpus

Order granting writ for Hilma Lundvall

FILED 17th day of Sept 1888, and entered on Page 529 of Journal No.
