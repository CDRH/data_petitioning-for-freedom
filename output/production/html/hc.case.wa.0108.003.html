GEO. D. BARNARD & CO. ST. LOUIS.

In the matter of the application of Maria K. Peterson for writs of Habeas Corpus

This matter being called up in open Court upon the return of the writs served upon Levicey Hume and Levicey O. Hamilton the same is continued until the Fourth Monday of October next to be heard at Kalama in open Court and it is further ordered that the mother of the children alleged to be confined have the privilege of seeing them at her own pleasure and convenience on all proper occassions & under proper restrictions

Dated Sept 20 1888

Frank Allyn

Judge
