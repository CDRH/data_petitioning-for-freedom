State of Missouri County of S. Louis } to wit By the Habeas Corpus Act.

The State of Missouri

To the Jailor of St. Louis County: Greeting:

You are hereby commanded to have the body of Elizabeth Vincent, alias Elizabeth Allen, alleded to be unlawfully detained by you, and imprisoned in the Jail of said County, together with the cause of such imprisonment and detention before me the undersigned at the Law Commissioner Court Room in the City of St Louis & State of Missouri on on morrow the 14th day of May A.D. 1859 at 4 O'Clock P.M. to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning the said Elizabeth Vincent, alias Allen, the person so imprisoned and detained and have you then and there this writ

Witness my hand this 13h day of May A.D. 1859

H Dusenbury

Law Commissioner of St Louis County
