  • Summary of Proceedings: Harry Porter was serving a sentence in the Douglas County jail for an unspecified charge. On February 6, 1915, the day he was to be released, he was served with an arrest warrant and compelled to remain in jail. After a few weeks passed without a hearing, Porter petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus. Then, in mid-March, he withdrew his petition. For more details, see related petition hc.case.ne.1508.
