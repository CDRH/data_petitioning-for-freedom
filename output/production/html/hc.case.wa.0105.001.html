Know all men by these presents that we Carl Gorich John Wamsley and Thos Tolentine[?] as principals and [] as surties are held and firmly bound unto the Territory of Washington in the sum of two hundred Dollars each for the payment of which are jointly and severally bind ourselves our heirs executors and adminstrators

Sealed with our seals. Dated this 28th day of August AD 1861

The conditions of this obligation is such that if the said Carl Gorich John Wamsley and Thos Tolentine[?] will appear at the next term of the District Court in and for the Second Judicial District of the Territory of Washington to be seen and held on the first Monday in September lurst[?] at Olympia WT on the first day thereof and remain in attendance from day to day until discharged by due process[?] of law from further attendance and shall give testimony in a certain cause then and there to be tried wherein the Territory of Washington is plaintiff and James Riley defendant on a complaint for an assault with intent to kill on John McLuod and for Malicious Mahem then this obligation to be null and void otherwise to remain in full force and effect
