In the District Court of the 3d Judicial District of Washington Territory held at Seattle in and for the counties of King, Kistap and Snohomish.

In the matter of the application of Peter Nelson, W Smith H Elsner C Whaley G Mattson Charles Lundblad Dennis Coffrey & Jacob Annir for a writ of Habeas Corpus } Order granting writ

When reading and filing the petition of Peter Nelson, W Smith H Elsner C Whaley G Mattson Charles Lundblad Dennis Coffrey & Jacob Annir duly signed and verified by them whereof it appears that they are illegally imprisoned and restrained of their liberty by Louis V Wyskoff Sheriff of said County of King in the County jail of King County aforesaid, and stating wherein the alleged illegality consists from which it appears to me that a writ of Habeas Corpus ought to issue.

It is ordered that a writ of Habeas Corpus issue out of and under the seal of the District Court aforesaid, directed to the said Louis V Wyskoff commanding him to have the bodies of the said Peter Nelson, W Smith H Elsner C Whaley G Mattson Charles Lundblad Dennis Coffrey & Jacob Annir before me at my chambers in the City of Seattle and County of King aforesaid on the 3 day of May AD 1875 at 1 oClock PM of that day, to do and recieve what shall then and there be considered concerning them together with the time and cause of there detention, and that he have then and there said writ

Dated May 1 AD 1875

J. R Lewis
Judge
