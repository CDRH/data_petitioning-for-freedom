  • Summary of Proceedings: Ellis Harrison petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus to retrieve Margaret and Grace Harrison from the custody of Eileen Harrison, which was granted. At the hearing, Ellis withdrew his petition and was ordered to pay court costs. Eventually, Margaret and Grace ended up with their mother. Although nothing in the court documentation explicitly states the relationships between these parties, the 1920 census makes clear that this was a child custody case.
