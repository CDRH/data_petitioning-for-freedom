In the District Court of the third Judicial District of the Territory of Washington holding terms at Seattle in and for the Counties of King, Kitsap and Snohomish

King County SS.

To the Keeper of the County Jail of King County and to Frederick Minick Marshall of the City of Seattle in said County:

We command you and each of you that the bodies of Ah Sing and Ah Yoet in your custody detained, as it is said, together with the day and cause of their caption and detention, by whatsoever name the said Ah Sing and Ah Yoet may be sworn or called, you safely have before the Honorable Orange Jacobs Judge of our said Court on Saturday the 25th day of October A.D. 1873 at 9 1/2 oclock in the forenoon to abide such order as the said Judge shall make in this behalf

Witness the Honorable Orange Jacobs Judge of our said District Court of Seattle

(Over)

this 25th day of October A.D. 1873.

L.B. Andrews

Clerk
