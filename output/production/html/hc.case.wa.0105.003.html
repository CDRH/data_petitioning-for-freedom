Territory of Washington}

County of Pierce} SS

To the U.S. Marshal or his Deputy, Greeting.

You are hereby commanded in the name of the United States of America to take the bodies of James Riley and Thomas Horn, as you have herebefore been commanded if to be found in your District, and them safely keep, so that you have them before the United States District Court of Pierce County at the next May term thereof. that is to say the 18th day of May AD 1868. then and there to answer to two indictments viz– "Assault with intent to kill", and "mallicious mahem. and have you then and there this writ with your doings endorsed thereon

Witness the Hon C.B. Darwin Associate Justice of Washington Territory and Judge of the 3rd Judicial District this the 16th day of April AD 1868.

Joseph L. Allison Clerk
