Territory of Washington}

County of Pierce} S

To the Sheriff or any Constable of Said County

Whereas Mowgatt Lo has complained in writing under Oath to the undersigned one of the Justices of the Peace in and for said County. That on the night of the 28th of February 1861 at Steilacoom in said County James Riley did in a rude and insolent manner unlawfully strike one A Loe and on same night did in and angry manner unlawfully push and thrust defendant and likewise he the said James Riley on same night did in a crowd of two or more persons exibit a pistol and bowie knife in a threatening manner and furthermore that said Riley did take steal and carry away one Silver Watch from the Hotel Kept by this deponant and of the value of 13 dollars and pray that he the said Riley be dealt with according to law

Therefore in the name of the United States You are commanded to forthwith apprehend the said James Riley and bring him before me to be dealt with according to law

Given under my hand this the 1st day of March AD 1861

A B Rabbesen J.P.
