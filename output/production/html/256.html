  • Summary of Proceedings: Clara A. Lilly, the petitioner, filed for a writ of Habeas Corpus to release her children, Carl A. Herbert, Bessie V. Herbert, and Oscar H. Herbert, from the Nebraska Children's Home Society. The Home Society did not permit her to see her children, who were taken after a complaint that Lilly was unable to care for them due to sickness for a temporary period. After their removal, Lilly re-married, her current husband being David S. Lilly, and claimed she now has a good and sufficient home for the children. The case ends with a stipulation of dismissal without prejudice. The fate of the Herbert children is unknown.
