Territory of Washington, County of Columbia } SS

To A.L. McCauley

In the name of the United States: You are hereby required to appear before the undersigned, one of the Justices of the Peace in and for said county, on the 29th day of July 1886, at 10 o'clock in the fore noon, at his office in the Precinct of Brooklyn to give evidence in a certain cause, then and there to be tried, between the Territory of Washington, Plaintiff, and Jacob Schuman Defendant, on the part of The Territory

Given under my hand this 27th day of July 1886.

J.C. Dorr
Justice of the Peace
