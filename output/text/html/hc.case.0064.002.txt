









Territory of Washington
County of King SS.

Territory of Washington
v
Alexander S Hughes David Hughes James Boyd David Hart and Joseph Brannan

Defendants except to Sheriff return and say that notwithstanding he claims to hold
them under said commitment the same is illegal.

For that these defendants never threatened to destroy to injure and destroy the property
of  of  Josiah Gellerson and Geroge Gellerson as in any way on the 5th day of May
or at any other time upon which they were convicted and upon which said commitment
was issued

Alex S. Hughes
David D. Hughes
David Hart
James Boyd
Joseph Brannon



Subscribed and Sworn to before me this 15th day of May 1875 }
WH White
Notary Public
W.T.
