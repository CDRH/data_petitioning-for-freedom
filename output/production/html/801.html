  • Summary of Proceedings: On April 5, 1924 Edward Ryan filed for a petition of a Writ of Habeas Corpus on behalf of Eva Ryan. The petition alleged that Eva Ryan was being detained in a Douglas County jail without a warrant on a pretended complaint from Kansas City Missouri. The petition also states that Eva Ryan is a resident of Nebraska and was not present in the state of Missouri when the alleged crime is believed to have taken place. After allegedly being deceived into signing a waiver of extradition to Missouri, the petition for a Writ of Habeas Corpus was filed. On April 9, 1924 the application was granted and Eva Ryan was discharged.
