  • Summary of Proceedings: Patrick McDonald, a thirteen year old boy, was arrested, accused of being a vagrant, and sent to the St. Louis House of Refuge. Bail was set at $100. He petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, contending that minors who had parents and homes could not be vagrants, which was granted. Michael McDonald, likely his father, witnessed the petition. After hearing the evidence, Patrick was remanded to the custody of F. S. W. Gleason, superintendent of the House of Refuge. N.B. This case file contains two versions of the habeas corpus petition.
