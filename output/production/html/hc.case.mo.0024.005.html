Alexis Amelin, to whom the writ of Habeas Corpus, hereto attached, was directed, commanding him to have the bodies of Catherine, Julie, Joseph and Helen, persons of colour, before this honorable court, together with the cause of their caption and detainer, for return to said writ certifies to the court here that the said Catherine Julie and Helen, were committed to the custody and care of him the said Amelin, at Herculaneum in the county of Jefferson, some time in the month of April - last by Alexander Scott Master of the Steam boat General Brown as the agent of said Scott, for the purpose of bringing the said Catherine Julie and Helen to S Louis and to make return of their bodies before the Honorable Robert Wash am of the Judges of the Supreme Court of the state of Missouri in obedience to a writ of Habeas Corpus, issued by said Judge directs to said Scott, that said Amelin brought the said Catherine Julie and Helen to S Louis as agent of said Scott, and on his return found that the said Judge had gone to St Charles to attent a session of the Supreme Court; that said Amelin committed the said Catherine Julie & Helen to the custody of John K Walker Esqr Jailor of S Louis County for safe keeping, until the said Judge should return to his chambers, the said Catherine Julie & Helen were not taken out of the custody of said Amelin, by order of said Judge, but were left with him as before the return by said Judge. That said Catherine Julie and Helen are now in the actual custody of the said John K Walker and are detained by him at my request until they shall be claimed and taken away from me by my principal or his authorities agent that I the said Amelin hold the said Julie Helen and Catherine in my custody in no other character then as the agent of said Scott & that they are not detained by me for any criminal or supposed criminal metter, & that they are now in the custody of said Walker, ready to be produced to this honorable Court by him on said writ. And the said Ameline saith that Joseph, mentioned in said writ never was, nor is he now in my custody or constraint, or detained by me in any manner whatsoever, but is informed and believes that Pierre Chouteau Senior, who claims said Joseph as his slave, has sent him up the Missouri river.

A Amelin Sworn to in open Court May 26th 1826 A Gamble Clerk
