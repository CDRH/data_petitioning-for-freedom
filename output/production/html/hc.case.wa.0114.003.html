Robinson being sworn says that he is one of the Attorneys for the above named Petitioners. that he has read the foregoing petition And knows fully the contents thereof, and knows the same to be true, except as to matters theirin stated upon information and belief and as to those, believes the same to be true. Joseph W Robinson
