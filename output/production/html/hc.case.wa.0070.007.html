In the District Court holding Terms at Seattle WT before the Hon Roger S Greene Judge thereof at Chambers

In the matter of the applicant of Ah Korn Ah Min and Ah Sin for writ of Habeas Corpus

The Territory of Washington County of King

1 To the Hon Roger S Greene Judge of the above named Court your petitioner Ah Korn Ah Min and Ah Sin come now and respectively represent that they are restrained of their liberty by James H Woolery Chief of Police in the City of Seattle in the Territory of Washington.

2 That the Court of their said restrained according to the best of their knowledge and belief is a trial and conviction under the respective Complaints hereto attached of against each of them respectively had before S B Vrooman City Justice of the Peace from said City of Seattle as appear upon said complaints   that said section I of Division 2 of Ordinance No 551 of said City of Seattle is void and of no affect for the reason that the same is in conflict with the general laws of the Territory of Washington and for the reason that the city Commission[?] Counsil[?] of [?] City had no power or authority to enact the same in manner and from as the name appears.

3rd that they each make this application for themself respectively in their own proper[?] person and also that the writ of Habeas Corpus issue and that they be discharged from their custody in the reason that therein said restraint is illegal.

T J Hurnes Atty for Applicants

The Territory of Washington County of King

Ah Korn Ah Sin and Ah Min being first duly sworn each for himself on his oath says that they are the applicants herin that they have heard the foregoing   petition and that they know the contents thereof and that the same is true as they believe

Witness E A Gardner ([?]) ([?]) ([?]) A [?] Ah Korn Ah Min Ah Lin

Signed
