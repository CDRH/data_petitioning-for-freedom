Sept 16-1914

Friday, July 3, 1914.

In re Marie Robina Grayson, Habeas Corpus. No. 2780. HEARING

Now on this day this cause comes on for hearing, Geo. P. Fishburne, Asst. U.S. Attorney, appearing for the Government, and Noah Shakespeare, Esq., and L. A. Merrick, Esq., appearing for the Petitioner, this being the return day, it is stipulated by and between counsel in open Court that the return herein may be signed by Geo. P. Fishburne, attorney for Respondent, and that such hearing will be deemed a sufficient verification thereof. It is further stipulated that a general denial may be filed later in the day as to the affirmative matter set up in the reply. Thereupon the matter is submitted upon the record, argued and taken under advisement. Respondent's exhibit, Record of proceedings before Immigration Commissioner is admitted.
