No. 118393 ORDER

In open Court this 20th day of October 1916 the above entitled matter coming on for argument and hearing on the application of the Defendant for an order to show cause heretofore issued here in for an allowance to the defendant for suit money and alimony pendete lite in this action, Defendant appearing by P.H.Wilson her Attorney and the Defendant H.W.Fuson appearing in person and also by his attorney T.R.Horner, the Court having considered the matter and being duly advised, having heard the argument of Counsel and the statement of the Paintiff,

IT IS THERFORE ORDERED BY THE COURT, that the Plaintiff H.W.Fuson immediately pay into Court or the registry thereof the sum of Four Dollars as suit money for expenses already incurred and also the further sum of Twelve & 50/100 Dollars as alimony pendent lite, and also the sum of Twelve & 50/100 Dollars upon his next paydayto wit Oct 25" 1916 and the further sum of Twelve Dollars & 50/100 semi montly thereafter, during the pendendency of this action as and for the support of the Defendant during the pendency of said action.

By The Court,

Boyd J Tallman
Judge
