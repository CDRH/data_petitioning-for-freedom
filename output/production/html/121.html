  • Summary of Proceedings: Leonard Collins, the petitioner, commit an act of larceny against William Hemmings. He allegedly stole one shotgun, shovel, wagon cover, and two blankets. Collins filed for a writ of Habeas Corpus on the grounds that he needed to support his wife and children. He argued that because of his detainment, they were entering severe poverty. The Judge allowed for the writ to be heard, but whether Collins was released or not is unknown.
