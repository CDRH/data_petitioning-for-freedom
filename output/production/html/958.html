  • Summary of Proceedings: Albert Schiessl claimed that Wilma Schiessl, his 2-year-old daughter, was unlawfully detained by Gussie Maloney. Gussie was the wife of Albert and the mother of Wilma. Albert claimed that Gussie left him for another man and that she was "morally unfit" to take care of the child properly. His wish was that Wilma be placed under his custody and not Gussie's. The writ was allowed and he was released from jail.
