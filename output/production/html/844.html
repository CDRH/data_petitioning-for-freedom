  • Summary of Proceedings: On October 27th, John Eral Tompkins, a minor, was arrested. Tompkins father, Willett E. Tompkins and attorney, Edward F. Bradley, filed petition of Habeas against Chief of Police and arresting officer, J. J. Donahue. Tompkin's father argued that his son was at home with him at the time of the arrest and that they have no authority to detain him. Judge Lee. S. Estelle granted the petition, but fate of the boy is unknown.
