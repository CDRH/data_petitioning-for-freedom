UNITED STATES OF AMERICA.

Territory of Washington

Third Judicial District} SS

To S.J. Murphy JA Farrell

In the name of the United States of America, You are hereby commanded to be and appear at the District Court for the Third Judicial District of Washington Territory, at Seattle, for the District aforesaid at 10 oclock in the forenoon of the 26th day of November A.D. 1887 [] then and there to give evidence in a certain cause pending wherein In the matter of the application of Alice McKay for a writ of Habeas Corpus for Mary McKay Plaintiff [] Defendant and to remain in attendance on said Court from day to day thereafter until discharged, and herein fail not at your peril.

Witness of behalf Deft

Witness, The HON. RICHARD A. JONES, Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of Washington Territroy, and Judge of said District Court, this 25 day of Nov 1887

W E Ledgewood, Clerk.

By [] Deputy.
