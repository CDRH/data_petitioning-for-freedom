Territory of Wash. vs L.T. Savacool

Commitment

 

(B)

Territory of Washington County of Garfield } SS

To the Sheriff, or any constable, and the keeper of the jail of said County

Whereas L.T. Savacool of Garfield County was brought before the undersigned, one of the justices of the Peace in and for said County, on the 18th day of February 1886, charged on the oath of C.A. McCabe and Jas Robinson with having on the 8th day of Feb. 1886 in said county committed the offence of uttering and publishing a forgery, and in the progress of the examination it appearing to the undersigned that the said L.T. Savacool was guilty of the offence of forgery as charged. And whereas the said L.T. Savacool has failed to give bail in the sum of $1000.00 for his appearance to answer at the next term of the District Court as required by me, Therefore in the name of the United States you the keeper of the jail of said County are commanded to receive the said L.T. Savacool into your custody in the said jail, and him there safely keep until he be discharged by due coursed Law.

Given under my hand this 13th day of Feb. 1886, W.S. Newland Justice of the Peace
