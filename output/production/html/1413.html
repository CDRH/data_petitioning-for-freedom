  • Summary of Proceedings: On August 28, 1914, Hazel May Mangen was committed to the Nebraska Institution for the Feeble Minded. Six years later, on November 1, 1920, Peter Mangen, possibly her father, filed a petition for a writ of habeus corpus against Douglas County's juvenile officer, A.H. Vosburg. A writ and return was filed on November 3, and Mangen also subpeonaed a Dr. Young and Miss Anthony to testify in the case. On November 4, 1920, judge A.C. Troup denied the application and remanded custody of Hazel May to the institution.
