  • Summary of Proceedings: The Board of Insanity for Kearney County, Nebraska had Scott Brainard committed to the Nebraska State Hospital for the Insane. He petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, the court determined that the board had improperly sentenced Brainard, although the rest of the "proceedings in relation thereto were regular." The court remanded Brainard into the custody of the state hospital but gave the board forty-eight hours to re-sentence him, otherwise he would be discharged.
