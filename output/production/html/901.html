  • Summary of Proceedings: John Thomas was convicted of "applying to his own use and benefit property of the United States"--specifically a gray military horse. He was ordered to pay a $1,000 fine and committed to jail in the interim. Thomas petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. His fate is unknown. N.B. The surviving case file contains a response to the writ of habeas corpus but lacks the initial petition.
