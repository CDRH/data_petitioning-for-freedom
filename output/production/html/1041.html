  • Summary of Proceedings: Frank Krebs and Pat Daughtery were detained by John McGraw and charged by J. F. Miller with assault. They petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus arguing that the justice of the peace exceeded his jurisdiction by trying to commit them to the custody of the sheriff and fine them $10 each immediately. The writ was granted but upon hearing the evidence, judge C. H. Hanford ordered that Krebs and Daughtery would spend sixteen days in jail.
