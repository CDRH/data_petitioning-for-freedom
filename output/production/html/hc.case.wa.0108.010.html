BELFAST LINEN. FROM GEO. D. BARNARD & CO. ST. LOUIS
(Copy.)

In the District Court of the Second Judicial District of Washington Territory holding Terms at Montesano in and for the County of Chehalis.

Territory of Washington}
County of Chehalis} SS.

To Levicy. O. Hamilton, Greeting

In the name of the United States of America, We command you to have the body of Ida Lundvall by you detained and restrained of her liberty, as it is said together with the time and cause of said detention and restraint by whatsoever name the said Ida Lundvall shall be called or charged before Frank Allyn Judge of the Second Judicial District of Washington Territory at the Court Room of the District Court in the City of Montesano in Chehalis County in said Territory, forthwith, to do and receive what shall then and therebe considered concerning the said Ida Lundvall and have you then and there this writ. Witness Hon Frank Allyn Judge of the said District Court at the Court room in the City of Montesano in Chehalis County this 17th day of September AD 1888. Attest my hand and the seal of the said Court the day and year last above written

H.M. Sutton Clerk

District Court Chehalis County Washington Territory
