In The DISTRICT COURT of the SECOND JUDICIAL DISTRICT

OF WASHINGTON TERRITORY,

Holding Terms at Montesano, in, and for the County of Chehalis.

The TERRITORY of WASHINGTON, PLAINTIFF. VS. Andrew Johnson DEFENDANT.} INDICTMENT.

We Andrew Johnson as Principal, and Charles H Byles and T J Shamley as Sureties, acknowledge ourselves to owe and be indebted to the Territory of Washington in the just and full sum of Four Hundred Dollars, lawful money of the United States of America, to be levied of our Goods and Chattels, Lands and Tenements, upon the following conditions:

WHEREAS, The said Andrew Johnson has been duly indicted by the Grand Jury of Chehalis County, in the Second Judicial District of Washington Territory, for the following crimes of to wit on three separate indictments one for riot and two for malicious mishief, in said County, and is now under arrest upon a warrant duly issued upon each of said indictments

Now, therefore, it the said Andrew Johnson shall be and appear in his proper person at the next regular term of said Court, to be holden in and for said Chehalis County, and from term to term thereafter, and answer the above mentioned indictments and shall obey all orders and processes of said Court, made and issued in that behalf, and shall keep the peace and be of good behavior toward all the people of Washington Territory, then this recognizance to be void, otherwise to remain in full force and virtue.

Witnessed and approved by me this 19th day of October A.D. 1887

WH Bush Sheriff.

Andrew Johnson
Charles N Byles
T J Shamley
