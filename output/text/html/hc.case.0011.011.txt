






Territory of Washington
County of Walla Walla 

In Justice Court before 
J.  D. Lauman Justice of the Peace 

Territory of Washington 
-vs-     
Mary Oneal  SS} 

Charge of Vagrancy
being a habitual drunkard

T. J. Robinson Marshall of Walla Walla City 
arresting and prosecuting witness
Defendant arrested March 7 1886 as it says in
complaint filed March 8 1886 and warrant 
issued. to T. J. Robinson Marshall who returned
the same into Court this March 8th 1886 with 
the defendant under arrest defendant admitted to charge and plead guilty. T. J. Robinson
sworn when the court being satisfied in 
the matter it is here now ordered &amp; adjudged by the court that the defendant be 
required to give bail in the sum of fifty
dollars for her good behavior for the term of 
three months and that in default thereof that 
she be committed to the County Jail for the 
term of three months or until she shall be 
thence discharged by due process of law.

J D Lauman
Justice of the Peace
