Territory of Washington County of Garfield } SS

I, J.H. Hosler the person to whom the annexed writ of Habeas Corpus is directed for return to the same State as follows, First, that I am now and during all the time for the two years past have been Sheriff of Columbia County Washington Territory and the Keeper of the common jail of said County, that on the 18th day of April 1884 the County of Garfield in said Territory had no jail or other place for the safe keeping of prisoners and that said County of Garfield has at no time since had such jail or place, that on the 18th day of April 1884 the Honorable S.C. Wingard Judge of the District Court of Garfield County Washington Territory holding terms at Pomeroy in said County made an order, that all persons bound over to wait trial before the District Court in said County of Garfield and who are unable to furnish bonds be confined in the County jail of said Columbia County, A true copy of which order is hereby annexed and   marked "A" and made a part of this return, that on the 13th day of February 1886 L.T. Savacool was brought before W.S. Newland, one of the Justices of the Peace in and for said County of Garfield charged on the oath of C.A. McCabe and Jas Robinson with having on the 8th day of February 1886, in said County committed the offence of uttering and publishing a forgery and in having appeared to the said W.S. Newland Justice of the Peace as aforesaid on the examination of the said L.T. Savacool then and there by him made that the said offence or crime had been committed and that there was probable cause to believe the said L.T. Savacool guility thereof, ordered that the said L.T. Savacool enter into recognizance in the sum of One Thousand Dollars for his appearance to answer at the next term of said District Court, and that in default of said recognizance that he be committed to jail until he should be thence discharged by due process of Law, that the said L.T. Savacool failed to enter into said recognizance whereupon   the said Justice of the Peace issued his commitment in due form of law (a true copy of which is hereinto annexed marked "B" and made a part of this return) and delivered the same to W.E. Wilson, Sheriff of said County of Garfield who by virtue of said commitment on the 13th day of February 1886, delivered the said L.T. Savacool into my custody in said jail, since which time and for the causes aforesaid I have as said keeper of said jail imprisoned and detained the said L.T. Savacool in my custody in said jail,

Dated this 23rd day of February 1886 J.H. Hosler

J.H. Hosler being duly sworn on oath says, he is the person who made the foregoing return that he has read the same and believes it to be true JH Hosler

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 23rd day of February 1886
