IN THE DISTRICT COURT OF THE UNITED STATES FOR THE WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON NORTHERN DIVISION.

****

IN THE MATTER OF THE APPLICATION OF CELIA BERG O'NEILL, alias Celia Berg, for a Writ of Habeas Corpus ))

No. 2963

ORDER DIRECTING ISSUANCE OF WRIT OF HABEAS CORPUS ETC.

*****

The petition of Celia Berg O'Neill, alias Celia Berg, having been today presented to the court in open court praying that a Writ of Habeas Corpus issue to Henry M. White, Commissioner of Immigration, and alleging that the said Henry M. White unlawfully detains the said Celia Berg O'Neill and deprives her of her liberty within the district and division aforesaid, and the court having duly examined such petition, it is now, after full consideration.

ORDERED that a Writ of Habeas Cornus in due form of law do forthwith issue out of and under the seal of this court to Henry M. White, Commissioner of Immigration, directing, commanding and requiring the said Henry M.White to produce before this court at a regular session thereof, appointed to be holden at the Federal Building in the City of Seattle, in said district on the 24th day of February,1915, at the opening of said court on said day, the body of said Celia Berg O'Neill, alias Celia Berg, and to then and there return said Writ with a full cause of the detention of said Celia Berg O'Neill fully set forth.

 

It is further ORDERED that at the same time and place the said Henry M. White, Commissioner of Immigration, do certify to this court all the proceedings had before Albert H. Joy and Thomas W. Lynch, as Immigration Inspectors, and before the said Henry M. White, or any other officer of the Immigration Service in such district, subsequent to the 12th day of October 1914, respecting the deportation of the said Celia Berg O'Neill alias, Celia Berg, to the end that such record having been inspected such order may be made herein as of right and justice ought to be made.

Done in open Court this 17th day of February, 1915.

Edward E Cushman
Judge.
