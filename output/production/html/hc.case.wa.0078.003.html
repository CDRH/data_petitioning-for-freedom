To the Hon. Roger S. Greene, Chief Justice of Washington territory and Judge of the Third Judicial District of said Territory

I do hereby return that before the coming to me of the annexed writ, the said Ah Foong was committed to my custody, and is detained by virtue of another writ a copy of which is hereto annexed; the original of which I also herewith produce; were thereas[?] I have the body of the said Ah Foong before you at the day and place named in said annexed writ as I am therein commanded.

W. Murphy

acting chief of Police for this City of Seattle, King Co. W.T.

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 19th day of January, 1886.

C.L.F. Kellogg

Notary Public for Washington Territory
