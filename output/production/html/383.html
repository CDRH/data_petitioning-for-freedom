  • Summary of Proceedings: Sam Walton and Lulu Walton got married in October of 1908 and had a son, McKinley Walton. In April of 1909, Lulu Walton deserted Sam Walton for Edward Stanley, taking their son with them. Sam Walton filed a writ of habeas corpus for his son in December of 1918, with the hopes of Lulu Walton and Edward Stanley returning McKinley Walton to him. In January of 1920, the writ was dismissed and the fate of the parties are unknown.
