  • Summary of Proceedings: John Dick, Oscar Dick, and Nuema Jonrett were arrested and fined for disturbing the peace. The trio were jailed when they could not pay their fees. Nicholas Dick petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus on their behalf, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, the trio appear to have been released, since John and Oscar wasted little time filing suits for wrongful detainment. See Cases No. 5858 and 5859 in the same box.
