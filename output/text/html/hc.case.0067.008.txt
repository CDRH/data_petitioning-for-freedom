







INFORMATION.

IN JUSTICE'S COURT,
Before Thomas H. Cann, Justice of the Peace in and for Seattle Precinct.

Territory of Washington, }
County of King. } SS.

James F Woolery being first duly sworn, says that at Seattle in said County, on the
10 day of November 1882 one believe name unknown Chin Chung did commit the crime of
smoking visiting a house whose as follows. opium kept for the purpose of opium smoking
and having in his possession pipe &amp; appliances for opium smoking and did visit such
house or place for the purpose of smoking or inhaling opium contrary to the form of
the statute in such case made and provided, and against the peace and dignity of the
Territory of Washington.

as I verily believe JH. Woolery

Subscribed and sworn to before me this, the 11 day of November 1882
TH Cann
Justice of the Peace, Seattle Precinct.
