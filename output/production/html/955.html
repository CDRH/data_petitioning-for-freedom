  • Summary of Proceedings: In May 1923, the Umatilla County Circuit Court had seventeen-year-old Myrtle O'Grady transferred from the St. Rose Industrial School in Portland to the State Hospital for the Feeble Minded in Salem. In November 1923, Myrtle's mother, Harriet O'Grady, petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus on her behalf, which was granted. Harriet's petition raised questions about the qualifications of the medical authorities who examined her daughter. After hearing the evidence, Myrtle was remanded back into the custody of the hospital superintendent.
