  • Summary of Proceedings: Mary Wilson was arrested and jailed on suspicion of being a fugitive from slavery. She asserted that she was a free woman and petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. She claimed that the man who had her arrested had a personal grudge against her due to a labor dispute. On hearing the evidence, Wilson was released.
