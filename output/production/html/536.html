  • Summary of Proceedings: On April 3rd, 1919, Albert Larson filed a habeas corpus petition on behalf of his daughter Nellie Larson who was being wrongfully held by Louis and Mollie Saunders. The petition was approved and the Saunders were ordered to appear in court and bring Nellie on April 5th. The hearing found that Nellie was being wrongfully detained by the Saunders and Mr. Larson's writ was approved by the court. Nellie was returned to her father.
