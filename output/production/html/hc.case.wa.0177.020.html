IN THE UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT FOR THE WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON, NORTHERN DIVISION.

In the Matter of the Application of Tonka Bajlo for a Writ of Habeas Corpus.

No. 1328 WRIT OF HABEAS CORPUS.

THE STATE OF WASHINGTON The President of United States of America TO ELLIS DE BRUELLER, Commissioner of Immigration.

GREETING:

We command you that you have the body of Tonka Bajlo, by you imprisoned and detained, as it is said, together with the time and cause of such imprisonment and detention, by whatsoever name, said Tonka Bajlo shall be named or charged, before Hon. Edward E. Cushman, Judge of the United States District Court for the Western District of Washington, Northern Division, at the Federal Building in Seattle, State of Washington, on the 21st day of April, 1913, at 2 o'clock in the afternoon of that day, to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning the said Tonka Bajlo.

And have you then and there this writ.

WITNESS, Hon. Edward E Cushman Judge of the United States District Court for the Western Division District of Washington, Northern Division, at the Federal Building in Seattle Tacoma, State of Washington, at 12 M. on the 16th day of April, 1913.

ATTEST my hand and seal of said United States District Court the day and year last above written.

Frank L. Crosby
Clerk
By []
Deputy Clerk.
