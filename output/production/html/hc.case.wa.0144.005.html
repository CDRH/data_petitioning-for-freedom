Territory of Washington County of Spokane} ss

In Justice Court before J.J.L. Peel, J.P.

In matter of arrest of Frankie Switzinger on warrant issued out of this court on the 23rd day of November A.D. 1885 on the complaint by affidavit of one R.J. Rush.

Now comes said Frankie Switsinger as by her attorneys W.M. Murray and F.H. Graves, and moves the court:

1- To quash said warrant.

2- To discharge said Frankie Switsinger for the reasons that said Justice of the Peace had no jurisdiction to issue said warrant; and has no jurisdiction to hold said Frankie Sutsinger in this proceeding.

W.M. Murray and
F.H. Graves,
Attys. for Sutsinger

 

In matter of arrest of Frankie Switsinger &c.

Motion to Discharge

Filed Dec. 10th 1885
J.J.L. Peel J.P.
