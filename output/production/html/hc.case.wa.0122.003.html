In the District Court of Columbia County, holding terms on Dayton

In the matter of the application of S.M. McFarland for a Writ of Habeas Corpus

Now on this 10th day of May 1888, this matter comes before Hon William G. Langford at his Chambers in the Court Room in Walla Walla, Wash. upon the writ & return of Sheriff and demurrer to the said return, and after hearing argument of Counsel and being fully atained[?] on the premises. The demurrer[?] in said return is sustained and that SM Farland is illegally restrained of liberty It is ordered that said N.R. Marquiss release from custody the said McFarland and that he is hereby be discharged –

Dated May 10 1888

William G. Langford
Judge

 

Filed
May 11 1888
JW Jessel Clerk
By U.J. Ellis Deputy
