  • Summary of Proceedings: Evaline L. Young, petitioner, filed a writ of habeas corpus on behalf of her daughter, Mertie L. Young, against her former husband, Samuel J. Young. He had left her to move to Omaha, but apparently missed his young daughter so much that he decided to kidnap her from her mother's home in Minneapolis, Minnesota. The Omaha Daily Bee suspected that his impulsive behavior was the result of "mental paroxysm." Judge Melville Hopewell allowed the writ due to the "tender age and delicate health" of Mertie Young, who is sixteen months old and had an "enfeebled constitution." The judge determined that Evaline was the proper caretaker in this scenario.
