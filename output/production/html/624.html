  • Summary of Proceedings: On October 3, 1918, Joe Ronneburg filed for habeas against Chief of Police, Marshall Eberstein, Chief of Detectives, John Briggs, and Captain of Police, Vanous. In his petition, Ronneburg charged that he had been arrested on September 30 without warrant or complaint and, had since, not been brought to appear in any court. A writ was issued but later dismissed on January 26, 1920, for want of prosecution; the fate of Ronneburg is unknown.
