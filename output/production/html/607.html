  • Summary of Proceedings: William Chapman petitioned for habeas corpus on behalf of his wife, Mildred Chapman, on October 13th, 1922. He alleged that his wife was not of sound mind and that her sister, Ruth Podrawzek, was unduly influencing her. Ruth allegedly convinced Mildred to file for divorce from her husband. The petition was approved and a hearing was ordered; however, the events of the hearing are unknown. The appearance docket indicated the case was dismissed for want of prosecution on January 21st, 1924.
