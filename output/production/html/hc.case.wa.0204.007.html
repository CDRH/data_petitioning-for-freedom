Marshal's Return

In obedience to the within writ, I served Henry M. White, U.S. Commissioner of Immigration, by leaving a true and correct copy of this writ with John H. Sargent, Assistant U.S. Commissioner of Labor, personally.

Dated Seattle, Wash., January 27th, 1919.

John M. Boyle,
United States Marshal.

By Edwin P. Toley Deputy.

Marshal's fees $2.12
