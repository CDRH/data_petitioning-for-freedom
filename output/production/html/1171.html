  • Summary of Proceedings: Russell and Raymond Eyerly were being detained in the Salem city jail. They petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. The brothers claimed that they were being held on a telegram from California stating that they were wanted for stealing a Cadillac, although they countered that they had purchased the vehicle legally. Before the hearing could proceed, the Eyerlys' attorney withdrew the petition, suggesting that his clients were released. Their actual fate, however, is unknown.
