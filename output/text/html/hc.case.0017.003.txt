









County of St. Louis, SCT.

The State of Missouri,

To the Sheriff of Saint Louis County--Greeting.

You are hereby commanded to summon William Skinner - Herman S Hoffman Otis Reynolds
- Thomas Cohen
that setting aside all manner of excuse and delay, they be and appear in proper person
before the Judge of our Circuit Court, on the First day of December, at the City of
St. Louis, then and there to testify and the truth to say in a certain matter of controversy
now pending in our said Court, wherein Dolly is plaintiff and John Young is defendant
on the part of the plaintiff; and have you then there this writ.

Witness, ARCHIBALD GAMBLE, Clerk of our said Circuit Court, at the City of St. Louis,
this 27th day of October in the year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred and twenty
nine

Archibald Gamble Clerk C.C.
