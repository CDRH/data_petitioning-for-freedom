COUNTY OF ST. LOUIS, SS.

THE STATE OF MISSOURI,

To Rufus M Underwood JW Jurgen Greeting:

You are hereby commanded that, setting aside all manner of excuse and delay, you appear before our Law Commissioner's Court of St. Louis County, at the City of St. Louis, on the 25th day of August 1858, then and there to testify, and the truth to say, in a certain cause pending in said Court, wherein Martha Edwards is petitioner Plaintiff and writ of Habeas Corpus has Defendant on the part and of the been issued and herein you are in no wise to fail.

WITNESS, Warren Montford Clerk of the Law Commissioner's Court of St. Louis County, with the seal of said Court hereto affixed, at office, in the City of St. Louis, this 25 day of August in the year of our Lord eighteen h undred and fifty-eight

W Montford Clerk.
