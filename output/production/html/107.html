  • Summary of Proceedings: George Schriner was jailed in Lancaster County, Nebraska and facing extradition to Morgan County, Colorado to respond to an accusation that he had violated a state ordinance making it "unlawful to sell mortgaged personal property, contrary to the provisions of the mortgage." He petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. Schriner contended that the extradition request did not allege that he had committed a crime, only that he had "sold mortgaged property." After hearing the evidence, Schriner was released.
