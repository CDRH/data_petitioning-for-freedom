OFFICE OF THE CLERK United States District Court Western District of Washington Tacoma

Honorable Frank L. Crosby,
Clerk U.S.District Court,
Seattle, Washington.

Sir:—

We are in receipt of an Order transferring cause, in the case of TONKA BAJLO, No.1328, on Habeas Corpus, and hence, you will please find enclosed the following files in this case:

*1. - Application for Writ;

*2. - Order to Show cause;

3. - Certified copy of Order transferring;

You will also please find enclosed check for $8.70 being the balance, now unearned, of the $10.00 which Mr. Ennis deposited to cover costs of the Petitioner.

Respectfully,

E W Ellington,
Deputy Clerk.

April 22, 1913.

NOTE: - ** These two papers were mailed you on April 18, 1913, and have not been returned to this office.

 

UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON, SOUTHERN DIVISION.

IN THE MATTER OF THE APPLICATION OF TONKA BAJLO, FOR A WRIT OF HABEAS CORPUS.

No.1328.

ORDER TRANSFERRING CAUSE TO NORTHERN DIVISION.

On application of the attorneys for the Petitioner and respondent herein,

IT IS ORDERED that the above entitled cause be and the same is hereby transferred from the Southern Division of the Western District of Washington, to the Northern Division of said District for further proceeding; and the Clerk is hereby directed to transmit the files to the Northern Division.

Dated this 21st. day of April, 1913.

Edward E. Cushman
Judge.
