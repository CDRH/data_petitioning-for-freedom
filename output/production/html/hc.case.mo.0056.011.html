State of Missouri

County of S Louis } ss

Personally appeared before me Louis Du Breuil a Justice of the peace within and for the County of St Louis John J. Schuler who being duly sworn upon his oath says that he has reason to believe and does believe that one Mary Wilson so calling herself, a yellow woman about thirty-five years of age straight hair and one tooth out in front is a runaway slave, and now employed on steam boat Isabel as a Chambermaid; and he therefore prays for a warrant for her apprehension

(signed) John J. Schuler

Sworn to & subscribed before me this 20h April 1854

Louis Du Breiul

Justice
