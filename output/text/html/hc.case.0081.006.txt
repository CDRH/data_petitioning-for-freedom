







IN THE DISTRICT COURT,

Of the Third Judicial District of Washington Territory, holding Terms at Seattle,
for the Counties of King and Kitsap

5042

HABEAS CORPUS

The People of the United States of America to the Sheriff of King County Greeting:
J.E. Howland Marshal of the United States

We command you, that you have the body of C.E. Clayton, Geo Rosenthal, Wm. Lewis,
John McDonald, Chas Olsen, Chas Kales, Frederick Londe by you imprisoned and detained
as it is said, together with the time and cause of such imprisonment and detention
by whatsoever name said C.E. Clayton, Geo Rosenthal, Wm. Lewis, John McDonald, Chas
Olsen, Chas Kales, Frederick Londe &amp; .C. Roschman shall be called or charged before
the Hon. Roger S. Greene, Judge of the aforesaid Court at his office in Seattle aforesaid
on the 9th day of October 1886 at the hour of 4 oclock P.M. to do and receive what
shall then and there be considered concerning the said C.E. Clayton, Geo Rosenthal,
Wm. Lewis, John McDonald, Chas Olsen, Chas Kales, Frederick Londe and C. Roschman.
And have you then and there this writ.

Witness the Hon. Roger S. Greene, Judge of said District Court, and the seal thereof
this 9th day of October A.D. 1886
James Seavey, Clerk
By Wm. L. Ludlow Deputy
