#1570

In the District Court of Columbia County Washington Territory holding terms at Dayton.

Before Hon. William G. Langford Judge of the above named Court and Associate Justice of the Subpoena Court of Washington Territory

Territory of Washington County of Columbia } SS

The United States of America to N.R. Marquiss

Greeting: We command you that you have the body of S.M. McFarland by you imprisoned and detained as it is said, together with the time and cause of such imprisonment and detention by whatever name said S. M. McFarland shall be called or charged before William G. Langford Judge of the District Court of Columbia County, Washington Territory holding terms at Dayton, as the court rooms in Walla Walla, Walla Walla County, Washington Territory, forthwith said day to do and receive which shall there and then be considered, concerning the said S.M. McFarland and have you then   and true this writ.

Witness the Hon. William G. Langford Judge of the District Court of Columbia County, Washington Territory, holding terms at Dayton, and Associate Justice of the Supreme Court of Washington Territory, this 9th day of May 1888 Attest my hand and the seal of the said District Court affixed this the day and year above written

J.W. Jessel Clerk
By U.Z. Ellis Deputy
