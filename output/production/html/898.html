  • Summary of Proceedings: The Commissioners of Insanity of Johnson County, Nebraska had Jacob Blessing committed to the Nebraska State Hospital for the Insane. Blessing claimed that he had not been permitted to testify at his commission hearing and petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, the court found that "no valid sentence" had been "imposed upon said applicant," allowing the writ and releasing Blessing from custody.
