In obedience to the within writ, now appears Anne Mary Anne Scheiber and brings into Court the body of the child Edward Scheiber, and while she denies that she unlawfully detains said child, says that she is the mother of said child, who is still an infant, and that the petitioner, Joseph Scheiber is an improper person to have the custody of said child and has publicly proclaimed that the same is not his child. And therefore claims to be the legal & proper custodian of the same. this 13th day of May 1862

Mary Anne Scheiber

Sworn to and subscribed before me May 13th 1862

S Rice Clk

No 285

Feb. Term 1862

Ex parte Joseph Scheiber

By the "Habeas Corpus" Act

Writ issued May 12th '62

Stephen Rice Clerk

To Mary Anne Scheiber and Henry Scheiber

Proof heard and refused
