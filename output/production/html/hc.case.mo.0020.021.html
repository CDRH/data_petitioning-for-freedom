I have the body of the within named Michael before said court as within commanded. He was on the 26th instant taken out of the possession of Henry G Mitchel & H C Russel on a warrant for kidnapping against whom a warrant has issued for kidnapping the said Mitchel was arrested by the name of Martin Mitchel & the said Michael was put & has been detained in the jail of Jefferson county for safe keeping & for no other cause

May 30th 1832

G. Hammond

Michael "By the Habeas corpus" "act"
