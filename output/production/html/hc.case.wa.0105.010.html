United States of America

Territory of Washington

2nd Judicial District

To the Sheriff of Peirce Pierce County Greeting:

In the name of the United States of America you are hereby commanded to take the body of James Riley James Riley if to be found within this district and him safely keep, so that you have him forthwith before the United States District Court for the 2nd Judicial District of Washington Territory, now in session at Olympia in the district aforesaid, then and there to answer to the Territroy of Washington in a charge of an assault and Battery

Herein fail not and have you then and there this writ.

Witness the Honorable O. B. McFadden C. C. Hewitt Chief Justice of Washington Territory and Judge of the second Judicial District this 18th day of March September A. D. 1861

Andrew J. Moses.

Clerk
