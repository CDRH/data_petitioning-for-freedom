







Territory of Washington County of Walla Walla }

H.J. Mann being first duly sworn depose and says that Bessie Mann is illegally and
improperly held in custody and  possession of and by Cynthia Mann in the County of
Walla Walla in the Territory of Washington and



on account of the facts set forth in the petition hereto annexed believe that said
Cynthia Mann will carry said Bessie Mann out of the Territory of Washington before
before compliance with a writ of Habeas Corpus could be enforced

Wherefore affiant asks that a warrant may issue as provided by law to bring said Bessie
Mann before the Judge before whom the application is made to be dealt with according
to law

H.J. Mann

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 24th day of April 1888

Edgar Leurman
Notary Public
