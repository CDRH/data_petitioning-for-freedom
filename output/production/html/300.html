  • Summary of Proceedings: Collins, petitioner, was arrested for assault and battery, a charge pursued by Sam McClenneghan, of the said minor child Joe McClenneghan. Collins was found guilty of striking the minor child on the side of the neck by Justice of the Peace Perry Harrier on December 10th of 1889. On the 12th Collins filed for a writ of Habeas Corpus which was then granted by Judge Melville Hopewell and he was released from custody.
