  • Summary of Proceedings: Arthur Smith, identified as an incompetent man, was detained at the Dr. Benjamin F. Bailey Sanitarium Company, also known as the Green Gables Sanitarium. His guardian, George Smith, petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus, which was granted. At his hearing, the respondents mounted no defense and several doctors testified to Arthur's condition. After hearing the evidence, Arthur was released.
