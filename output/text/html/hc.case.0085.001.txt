







#5845

IN THE DISTRICT COURT,
Of the Third Judicial District of Washington Territory, holding Terms at Seattle,
for the Counties of King and Kitsap.

HABEAS CORPUS.

The People of the United States of America to the Sheriff of King County Greeting:
S.L. LaFebrve

We command you, that you have the body of Henry Luke LaFebvre by you imprisoned and
detained as it is said, together with the time and cause of such imprisonment and
detention, by whatseover name said Henry Luke LaFebvre shall be called or charged,
before the HON. ROGER S. GREENE, Judge of the aforesaid Court at 5 Oclock P.M. of
Tuesday 21st Day of February 1888 to do and receive what shall then and there be considered
concerning him the said Henry Luke LeFebvre. And have you then and there this writ.


WITNESS THE HON. ROGER S. GREENE, Richard A. Jones, Judge of said District Court,
and the seal thereof this 21st day of February A.D. 1888

W E Ledgerwood James Seavey, Clerk.
By  Deputy

Court Seal
