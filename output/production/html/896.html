  • Summary of Proceedings: Inous Carthrum was sent to a Detention Home at 22nd and St. Mary's Avenue by the Health Commissioner Ernest T. Manning to quarantine for venereal disease. Her husband William Carthrum petitioned for Habeas Corpus on October 16th, 1918, claiming there was no complaint against her for venereal disease. The writ was allowed and Inous was released on October 18, 1918.
