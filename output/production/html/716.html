  • Summary of Proceedings: On October 7, 1924, Daisy Cattleman was detained in the Douglas County jail on charges unknown to her and without a proper trial. On October 30 she filed a petition for a writ of habeas corpus. The proceedings revealed that the municipal judge of the city of Omaha ordered Cattleman's detention on charges of vagrancy, sentencing her to 30 days in county jail and payment of prosecution costs. The writ was dismissed for want of prosecution. Cattleman's fate is unknown.
