  • Summary of Proceedings: Arrested on the charge of larceny, Michael J. Corrigan petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus claiming that he had been denied his right to a speedy trial and that Sheriff Miller had no authority to jail him. Judge Holmes granted the petition. After hearing evidence, Holmes remanded Corrigan back into custody. One month later, Corrigan filed a motion for a new trial, which was overruled.
