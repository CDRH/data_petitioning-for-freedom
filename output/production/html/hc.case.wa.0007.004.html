I George F Thomas, Sheriff of Walla Walla County Washington Territory do here by certify and return that in obedience to the within writ of Habeas Corpus I took into custody the body of Myra Bell Montgomery the person named in the within writ and held the said Myra Bell Montgomery subject to the order of this Court here at Walla Walla County W. T. this 30th May 1877.

Geo F Thomas
Sheriff Walla Walla
County Wash Ter

J. B. Thompson
Deputy
