Mr. S. J. MURPHEY, a witness called on behalf of the Respondent, after being duly sworn, testified as follows:-

Q. [Gilman] Where do you reside? A. Tacoma.

Q. What is your business? A. Livery man.

Q. Have you during the past year been on the police force in Tacoma? A. Yes sir.

Q. Do you know the petitioner in this case, Alice McKay? A. Yes sir.

Q. Where does she live? A. She lives in Tacoma, 4th ward.

Q. What kind of a place does she live in? A. Well, she lives in a house along with Antone.

Q. Who is this man? A. He is Adago. He is here today.

Q. Describe his size? A. He is a large man, about as tall as I am and heavy set.

QQ. Is he blackx? A. Yes sir.

Q. He has been in the court room to day? A. Yes sir.

Q. When you were on the police force did you have any occasion to arrest Mrs. McKay? A. Yes sir.

Q. For what? A. Drunkenness.

RONALD: We object to that.

Q. Where did you arrest her? A. Right by her house in the street.

Q. Was she on a publicstreet in a drunken condition? A. Yes sir.

Q. When was that? A. That was in June, sometime, sir.

Q. This year? A. Yes sir.

Q. Do you know wtat the character of this Antone is, what his business is?

RONALD: We object.

The COURT: You may answer. Exception allowed.

A. He is a gambler.

Q. Have you ever at any other time seen Mrs. McKay drunk? A. Yes sir.

Q. How often have you seen her drunk? A. Oh, I have seen her drunk off and on for the last 14 years.

Q. Where? A. Well, in Tacoma, ten years in Tacoma, I have known her before.

Q. On the public streets.

A. Yes sir.

CROSS-EXAMINATION:

Q. [Ronald] You were discharged from the police force? A. I was not.

Q. She made an affidavit against you did she not? A. She did not..

Q. You dont like her? A. I have not got anything against her? at all.

Q. You have no prejudice against her at all? A. No sir.

Q. You seen her drunk a great many times in the last year? A. I have seen her drunk two or three times

Q. You arrested her before a Justice of the peace? A. Yes sir.

Q. What Justice? A. Judge Howes, he is dead.

Q. When did he die? A. He died two or three weeks ago.

Q. You never took her before Justice Senter? or Justice Harris? A. I did not sir.

Q. Why did you not take her before them? A. Well, they were not in the place.

Q. You swear she was drunk? A. I do sir.

Q. You have nothing against her? A. No sir.

Q. You also swear she lives in the house with this Italian Antone? A. Yes sir.

Q. You know that? A. I know she lives in the same house.

Q. Under the same roof? A. Yes sir.

Q. You are as certain of that as of anything else you have sworn to? A. I am sir.

Q. She is living in a state of adultery with him? A. I dont know anything about that.

Q. How many rooms are there in the house? A. There is two or three rooms

Q. Which one does anone use? A. I dont know, sir.

Q. How do you know that he uses either one? A. I know because he lives there.

Q. How do you know that? A. I have been in his house.

Q. Which room does he use in her house? A. I think he uses the back room. There is two rooms in the main part and he has got a bed room in the back part.

Q. You arrested her by her hsuse? A. Yes sir.

Q. What were you doing in her house? A. I was in there with her when she got the money to pay her fine.

Q. In his room? A. Yes sir.
