Territory of Washington County of Walla Walla

In District Court First Judicial District

In the matter of the application of J. H. Sheffler & Josephine Sheffler for Writ of Habease Corpus} Reply to answer & Return

J H Sheffler & Josephine Sheffler for reply to the answer and return of John B Montague

1. Deny that for several months prior to the execution of said agreement or for any length of time Josephine Sheffler or any other person voluntarily or at all let said Defendant have said child or placed the same in the family of Defendant to be by him or his wife kept or raised or educated or that said child was sick or required nursing or medical aid

2. Admit that at the time of execution of agreement   Defendant Montague kept a saloon and that the same was known to Josephine Sheffler but denies that Defendants family lived in the Saloon at the time of the execution of said instrument or that J. H Sheffler or Josephine Sheffler knew or believed that it was the intention of Defendant Montague to live with his family in a saloon or that he would keep said child in or about a saloon and over that the class character and kind of saloon kept by said Montague at the time of the execution of said agreement has been changed by said Montague

3. Denies that said Montague has treated said child as his he would his own child and aver that said child has been illtreated and abused by said Montague & wife

 

4 Denies that said Montague or his wife have become strongly or affectionatly attached to said child or that a like attachment exists with said child for Montague or his wife or such as a child has for its own parents

Wherefore J H Sheffler and Josephine Sheffler pray that said child be ordered into their custody & for costs

A E Isham N. T. Catan atys for Sheffler & wife
