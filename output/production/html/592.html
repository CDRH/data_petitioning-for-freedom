  • Summary of Proceedings: Mayor of South Omaha, Allen R. Kelly, was allegedly found taking bribes from several individuals and brewing companies (Omaha Brewing Association, Jos. Schlits Brewing Company, W. M. J. Lemp Brewing Company, Fred Krug Brewing Company, Metz Brewing Company, South Omaha Brewing Company, Anheuser-Busch Brewing Association, Pabst Brewing Company) to the sum of twenty-five hundred dollars. Allen R. Kelly accepted and received said money as a reason to veto any ordinance from the city council that would increase the liquor license tax above and beyond the sum of five hundred dollars. Additionally, Kelly prevented the arrest of those breaking codes related to alcohol serving. In response, Kelly claimed he was not guilty and not eligible for prosecution because he is not a ministerial officer but an executive one, making him a class of officer that is not included in the bribery codes. The case was appealed to the Supreme Court, and it was found that Kelly should remain in custody.
