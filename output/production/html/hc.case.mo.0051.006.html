State of Missouri County of St Louis ss

Andrew J. Crowwell personally appeared before me Peter W Johnstone a Justice of the Peace in and for said County and being duly sworn says he has taken up as a runaway slave a negro man by name James Allison Parker and prays a commitment may be had for his safe keeping

(Signed) A.J. Crowwell

Sworn to and subscribed before me this 11th day of September 1858

(Signed) Peter W. Johnstone, J.P.
