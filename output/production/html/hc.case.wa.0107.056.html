In the District Court at Montesano
Terry. of Washington vs. Gus Bendjus et al.

This application coming on for a reduction of Bail, and in being apparent that Defts cannot give the amount heretofore required and considering the great expense thereby about to be imposed on the County. The court having become satisfied a lesser amount of bail will be as effectual & be to better effect on above.

Therefore it is ordered that the bail heretofore filed be reducted to 400$ for each of the accused now under arrest, that to cover the three charges in each one's case and upon good security being given by each of said parties at the sum of $400- said parties to be released ill next term of court.

Frank Allyn
Judge

At Chambers. Oct 1. 1887
