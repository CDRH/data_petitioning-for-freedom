  • Summary of Proceedings: James McGarth and William Herbert were arrested and kept by Sheriff L.V Wycroff on the offense of passing counterfeit coin and kept in County Jail. They petitioned for habeas, claiming that Rosevelt Scott Coles, acting as Justice of the Peace, had no jurisdiction, no sufficient complaint was made, McGarth and Herbert weren't allowed counsel, and there was no reasonable cause. Writ was granted and McGarth and Herbert testified in court. Outcome is unknown.
