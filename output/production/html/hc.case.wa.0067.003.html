In the District Court holding terms at Seattle in King County, Washington Territory.

In the matter of the application of Chin Chung (a Chinaman) for a writ of Habeas Corpus.

On reading and filing the petition of Chin Chung duly signed and verified, whereby it appears that, he is illegally imprisoned and restrained of his liberty by John H. McGraw, Sheriff of said King County, at said King County, and stating wherein the illegality consists from which it appears to me that a writ of habeas corpus ought to issue. It is ordered that, a writ of habeas corpus issue out of and under the seal of this court, directed to the said John H. McGraw, commanding him to have the body of the said Chin Chung before me in the Court Room of the District Court at Tacoma Pierce County in said Territory on the Eighteenth 20h day of November AD 1882 at two ten Oclock A P.M. of that day, to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning the said Chin Chung together with the time and cause of his detention and that he have there and then the said writ and in the meantime he be admitted[?] to bail in the sum of sixty five dollars.

Dated the Eighteenth day of November 1882

Roger S Greene
Judge of said Court
