  • Summary of Proceedings: Miles W. Newby, the County Attorney for Monona Iowa accused Harry Davis, Morris Meyer, and George Meyer of robbing Moon Clothing Company in Onawa, Iowa. He had three witness statements corroborating that the three men stored clothes in the basement of their friend Kellerman's house and tried to sell clothes to Moe Sundheiner with the tags still on them. The Governor of the state of Iowa filed a warrant that the Governor of Nebraska approved. Harry Davis filed a petition for habeas corpus on September 13th, claiming that he hasn't left Omaha Nebraska in 20 years and there is no justification for the warrant or his arrest. The petition was approved and an order of return was received. There is no more information about the hearing or case outcome.
