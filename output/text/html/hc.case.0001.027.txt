







Testimony of E Trainor

John Dugan came to my yard yesterday morning and said that Mary Teller has shot killed
Frank meaning Franklin Teller I then went to the house of Mr Teller and saw him lying
between the gate and house dead there was a gun lying about two feet from him cocked
with new caps on it I dont think he could of fallen with the in his hands without
it going discharging

I also saw a board about four inches wide and three or four feet long with blood on
it lying near him
