UNITED STATES OF AMERICA.

TERRITORY OF WASHINGTON,} SS.

To the Sheriff of Pierce County---Greeting:

In the name of the United States of America, you are hereby commanded to subpoena John McLeod, Carl Gorich, J. Corcoran, R White John Esther Bradley Thos Talenton Thos Carroll to be and appear forthwith before the District Court of the United States for the Second Judicial District of Washington Territory, now in session at Olympia for the said District, it being the 6th day of September 1861 then and there to give evidence in a certain cause therein pending, wherein the Territory of Washington is plaintiff and James Riley and Thos Horn are defendant

Witness, on the part of the Territory

WITNESS, the Honorable C.C. Hewitt, Chief-Justice of Washington Territory and Judge of the Second Judicial District, this 6th day of Sept. A.D. 1861.

Andrew J. Moses Clerk.
