IN THE DISTRICT COURT, Of the Third Judicial District of Washington Territory, holding Terms at Seattle, for the Counties of King and Kitsap.

HABEAS CORPUS.

The People of the United States of America, to the Sheriff of King County. Greeting:

We command you, that you have the body of Qui (a Chinaman) by you imprisoned and detained as it is said, together with the time and cause of such imprisonment and detention, by whatsoever name said Qui (a chinaman) shall be called or charged, before the HON. ROGER S. GREENE, Judge of the aforesaid Court at the court house in the city of Seattle said King County at the hour of 12 M Nov. 18th 1884 to do and receive what shall then and there be considered concerning the said Qui. And have you then and there this wit.

WITNESS THE HON. ROGER S. GREENE, Judge of said District Court, and the seal thereof this 18th day of November A.D. 1884 JAMES SEAVEY, Clerk. By James P. Ludlow Deputy
