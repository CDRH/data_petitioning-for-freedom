Territory of Washington County of Spokane } SS

To Neil Smyth T.J. Perkins Jarvis[?] Magum[?] E Trainor ER Bird CS Penfield

In the name of the United States you are hereby required to appear before the undersigned one of the Justices of the Peace in & for said County on the 1st day of June 1887 at one o'clock PM (or forthwith) at his office in Deep Cr Falls Spokane Co WT to give evidence in a certain cause then and there to be tried before me the Territory of Washington plaintiff and Mary Teller John Dugan and Neil Smyth defendant on the part of the Territory

Given under my hand this 1st day of June 1887

FL Pierce Justice of the Peace in for Spokane Co WT
