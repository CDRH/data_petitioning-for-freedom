The President of the United States
To the Sheriff of Chehalis County

Greeting:
Whereas: Gus Bendjus, Robert Stranglaud, Pete Peterson, Ben Fredrickson   Frank Nelson, Fred Stromberger, John Blending, Andrew Johnson, Leander Brown, John Saxon, John Thompson, Alex Agren, Thomas Jacobs, E. Emil, Andrew Anderson, Charles Burke: having been duly indicted in the District Court of the Second Judicial District of Washington Territory holding terms at Montesano of the crime of Riot all of which appears to us of record.

Now, this is to command you, the said Sheriff to take the said Gus Bendjus, Robert Stranglaud, Ben Fredrickson, Pete Peterson, Frank Nelson, Fred Stromberger, John Blending, Andrew Johnson, Leander Brown, John Saxon, John Thompson, Alex Agren, Thomas Jacobs, E. Emel, Andrew Anderson, Charles Bunke, and them the said Gus Bendjus, Robert Stranglaud, Ben Fredrickson, Pete Peterson, Frank Nelson, Fred Stromberger, John Blending, Andrew Johnson, Leander Brown, John Saxon, John Thompson, Alex Agren, Thomas Jacobs, E. Emel, Andrew Anderson, Charles Burke, safely keep and have them forthwith in this Court, there to answer to said charge   and abide such further order as the Court may make in the premises — herein fail not.

Witness, the Hon. Frank Allyn judge of the said District Court and the Seal of said Court, this 16th day of Sept A.D. 1887.

H.M. Sutton,
Clerk.
