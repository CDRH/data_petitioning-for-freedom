  • Summary of Proceedings: Tony Carlo was being detained at the Nebraska Reformatory for an unspecified reason. E. E. Hairgrove, his attorney, petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus on his behalf, which was granted. After hearing the evidence, Carlo and Hairgrove were given one week to file a brief, which they failed to do. Carlo was therefore remanded to the custody of his jailer and ordered to pay court costs.
