St. Louis Law Commissioners' Court

August Term 1853, No. 94

Freeman, Henry,

In the matter of

Habeas Corpus

Defendant imprisoned in jail of St. Louis County "upon suspicion of being a slave, and this upon the oath of a certain A.B. Holton"; defendant claimed to be born a free man who lived in Alton, IL, for many years; order dated July 20, 1853, to keep him jailed "until he be taken hence by his Master... or until he be otherwise discharged according to Law"
