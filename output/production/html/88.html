  • Summary of Proceedings: On October 9, Lottie Parsons filed for Habeas with attorney, William Simeral, against the St. James Orphan Asylum. Parsons charged that William Henry Arnold, known as Harry Duncan, was her Six-and-a-half-year-old son born in lawful wedlock, and whose father is dead. Parsons also charged that a false Consent for Adoption was issued, that such consent was repudiated and canceled on September 23, and that the asylum refused to return child to Parsons. A writ was issued by Judge Day, but Parsons dismissed the writ on October 11.
