I, J H Woolery, Chief of Police of the City of Seattle, King County W.T. do hereby return to the Hon. Roger S. Greene, Chief Justice of Washington Territory, and Judge of the District Court, holding terms at Seattle, that before the coming to me of the awarded writ, the said Lee Kee was committed to my custody, and is detained by conviction of another writ a copy of which is hereto awarded; the original of which I also herewith produce; Nevertheless I have the said Lee Kee now before you as I am commanded.

J. H. Woolery Chief of Police

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 1st day of June, 1885. C. H. Hanford Notary Public
