In Justice Court before J H Cann JP.

comes the Deft by his attorneys Buck &amp; Haines and moves for a further  until 7 oclock
Nov 17 allowed

Now at 7 oclock PM Nov 17 1882 came the Defendant &amp; his said Attys and the case being
called on to be heard and two witnesses



two witnesses was sworn and testified on the part of the Territory and by agreement
Mr Maurice McMicken was sworn as reported to take the testimony the case on part of
the Territory the closed and the Defendant called no witness and the case closed the
Court being fully advised considered and adjudged that the Defendant is guilty as
charged in complaint and the Defend ant did that Defen did visit a house kept for
smoking or inhaling opium and the then and then smoke &amp; inhale opium in said house.
and it is the judgement that of this court that said Defendant is guilty of each charge
in the complaint and that he shall pay a fine of $25.00 and the cost on in this case
and all accruing cost and shall stand committed until said fine and costs are paid

Nov 17 1882
