IN THE DISTRICT COURT OF THE UNITED STATES FOR THE WESTERN DISTRICT OF WASHINGTON NORTHERN DIVISION.

IN THE MATTER OF THE APPLICATION OF CELIA BERG O'NEILL, ALIAS CELIA BERG, for a writ of HABEAS CORPUS. ))

No. 2963 WRIT OF HABEAS CORPUS.

*******

UNITED STATES OF AMERICA, to Henry M. White,

Commissioner of Immigration.

WHEREAS, it has been made to appear to our District Court that you are unlawfully detaining and unlawfully depriving of her liberty one Celia Berg O'Neill, alias Celia Berg and we being willing that the cause of her detention should be inquired into to the end that justice may be afforded her,--

NOW, THEREFORE, you are COMMANDED AND SOLEMNLY ENJOINED to produce before this Court at a regular session thereof, appointed to be holden at the Federal Building in the City of Seattle, in said district. on the 24th day of February,1915, the body of the said Celia Berg O'Neill, alias Celia Berg, together with the cause of her detention fully and at large set forth, and that you then and there this Writ have.

You are further commanded that you do forthwith certify unto this court all of the records, documents and proceedings had before you, or before any officer, relating to the deportation of the said Celia Berg O'Neill, alias Celia Berg, to the end that such things having been examined such order may be made therein as of right and justice ought to be made.

 

WITNESS The Honorable Edward E. Cushman, Judge of said Court, and the seal thereof, at Seattle, in said district on the 17th day of February, 1915,

Frank L. Crosby
Clerk

By Edw Lakin
Deputy.
