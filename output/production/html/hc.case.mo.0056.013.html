The State of Missouri,

To the Marshal of St. Louis County — Greeting:

We command you to attach William Scott by his body and him safely keep, so that you have his body before the Law Commissioner's Court of St. Louis County, now in session at the City of St. Louis, within and for the County of St. Louis, on the forthwith 1854 then and there to testify and the truth to say in a certain matter of controversy, now pending in said Court, between Habeas Corpus writ plaintiff and Mary Wilson defendant, wherein the said Mary Wilson ha heretofore been summoned on the part of the said Deft

Witness, John W. Colvin, the Law Commissioner of St. Louis County, with the seal of said Court hereto affixed, at office, in the City of St. Louis this 21 day of April in the year of our Lord eighteen hundred and fifty- three 4

The Law Commissioner of St. Louis County.
