  • Summary of Proceedings: Arrested and jailed on the charge of "getting and becoming drunk and indulging in loud, boisterous and tumultuous conduct," Susie Blue and Mary Woods petitioned for a writ of habeas corpus claiming that Chief of Police Hoagland was unlawfully detaining them. Judge Cornish granted the petition. After hearing evidence, he allowed the writ and ordered that Blue and Woods be released from custody. Months later, Hoagland moved for a new trial, which was denied.
