Territory of Washington
County of Walla Walla {

In Justice Court before
OP Lacy Justice of the Peace

Territory of Washington
vs
Adaline Oneil Deft }

Charged with being an habitual Drunkard and disorderly person

Jany 3rd[?] Complaint in the above entitled cause filed and Defendant Adaline Oneil arrested and led into Court and cause continued to Jany[?] 6th 1876 at ten oclock AM at said hour Deft Adaline Oneil in Court in person and being duly arraigned pleads not guilty whereupon the following witnesses were sworn on the law of the Territory John Lucas G P Foos[?] Henry Chase Henry Mind[?] John P. Justice Defendant offers no evidence and it appearing from the evidence that the said Adaline Oneil is an habitual drunkard and disorderly person the said court did adjudge and determine that the said Adaline Oneil give bonds in the sum of one hundred dollars for her good behavior for the sum of three months and in default thereof that the said Adaline Oneil be committed to the Jail of Walla Walla County and default [?]

 

Committed to Jail

Given under my hand this 6th day of January 1876

O P Lacy

Justice of the Peace

Territory of Washington vs Adaline Oneil Certificate of Conviction

Filed January 6th 1877

A Reeves Ayers
Clerk
