Territory of Washington 2nd Judicial District Thurston County

In the matter of James Riley}

To the Honorable CC Hewitt Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of said Territory and Judge 2nd Judicial District.

Your petitioner Edward Lander respectfully represents that James Riley is restrained of his liberty by within the jail of the said County of Thurston by E.H. Tucker Sheriff of the County of Pierce in said district under and by virtue of a certain warrant directed to him said Sheriff commanding him apprehend the said Riley and him safely keep to answer to a charge of assault & battery with intent to kill

That the said Tucker as such Sheriff of Pierce County has no power or authority to arrest and keep the said Riley in the said county of Thurston and that the charge   against said Riley as in said warrant set forth is not an offense by the laws of said Territory

Wherefore you petitioner asks that the writ of Habeas Corpus may issue directed to the said Tucker Sheriff of Pierce County and that the said Riley be discharged from custody and imprisonment

Edward Lander

Subscribed and sworn to be true before me this 21st day of September 1861

A J Moses Edw

Clerk
