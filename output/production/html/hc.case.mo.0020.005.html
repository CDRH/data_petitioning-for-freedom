Served this writ on Henry C. Russell on the 28 May 1832 in the city of St Louis by reading the writ & offering to read the declaration to him which he refused to hear read. I also at the same offered to read to him the copy of the order of the Judge permitting the plaintiff to institute suit & ordering that he shall not be removed out of the Jurisdiction of the said court &&

John K Walker
