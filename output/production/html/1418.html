  • Summary of Proceedings: M. J. McDonough was charged with contempt of court. He petitioned and argued that he did not break the law, and brought up sections of Washington-Territory code that stated the judge was not allowed to give him both a fine and jail time (only one or the other). Writ was granted and Judge Greene said he should be discharged from custody and recover his costs against the sheriff (McGraw).
