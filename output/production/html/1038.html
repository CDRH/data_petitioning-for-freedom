  • Summary of Proceedings: Gustav Rittmaster, the petitioner, was charged with obtaining money under false pretenses in Iowa and was then found in Nebraska. Iowa filed to extradite him, and he plead for a writ of Habeas Corpus in an effort to stay free in Nebraska. Judge Hopewell ordered for him to be brought before the court on the 24th day of March in 1891. The result of the hearing was not mentioned in the case papers. According to Gustav Rittmaster B, This case resulted in the detention of Rittmaster by Sherrif John F. Boyd for the alleged cause of being a fugitive from justice from Arapahoe County, Colorado.
