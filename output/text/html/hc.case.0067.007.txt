







UNITED STATES OF AMERICA.

IN JUSTICE'S COURT.

Before T H Cann Justice of the Peace.

Territory of Washington Plaintiff
vs
Chin Ching 
Territory of Washington,}
County of King.}SS

To the Sheriff or any Constable of said County, Greeting:

Whereas, J H Wooley has this day complained in writing, under oath, to the undersigned,
one of the Justices of the Peace in and for said County, that on the 10 day of November
1882 at Seattle in said County Defendants did enter the care of  a house kept for
opium smoking set forth  on part for the purpose of smoking opium

Therefore, in the name of the United States you are commanded forthwith to apprehend
the said Deft, Chin Chung and bring him before me, to be dealt with according to law.

Given under my hand this 11 day of Nov 1882
T H Cann
Justice of the Peace.
