In the matter of the confinement of prisoners Garfield and Assotin Counties

General Order

 

(A)

At Chambers April 18th 1884

In the matter of the Confinement of person held for trial at Pomeroy General Order

It Satisfactorily appearing to me that there is no jail in the Country of Garfield or the County of Assotin. It is ordered that all persons bound over to await trial before the District Court in either of said Counties, and who are unable to furnish bonds be confined in the County jail of the County of Columbia in Dayton until such time as the said Counties have caused suitable places of Confinement to be erected or until the further order of the Court

S.C. Wingard Judge &C
