In the District Court holding terms at Seattle

In the matter of the application of Mary Ellen Lyts for a writ of Habeas Corpus for the for custody of the child Francis W Lyts}

This cause coming on for hearing this 22nd day of March A. D. 1889 , in application of defendant it is ordered that the hearing of this cause be continued until Tuesday the 2nd day of April A. D. 1889 at 10 oclock A.M. and it is further ordered that until said hearing, said child Francis Walter Lyts be placed in the custody of John H. McGraw Sheriff of King County, Louisa Johnson and that the applicant Mary Ellen Lyts and the respondent W. D. Lyt should have the right during all reasonable hours to visit said child while in the custody of said Sheriff Lousia Johnson Thomas Burke Judge
